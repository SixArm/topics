# Disciplined Agile principle 4: Context counts

**Context counts: Acknowledge that every team and organization is unique. Your
"Way of Working" (WoW) must reflect your specific environment and evolve over
time.**

This principle is the logical partner to Disciplined Agile principle 3,
"Pragmatism." It builds on the observation that every team, project, and
organization faces a distinct set of challenges. A five-person startup building
a greenfield application operates in a fundamentally different reality than a
hundred-person department maintaining a legacy mainframe in a heavily
regulated industry. Treating them the same guarantees failure for at least one of
them.

Because of this, DA doesn't give you a "recipe"; it gives you a toolkit. Instead
of stating "All teams must do X", ask "What is the context?" then look toward
the strategies most likely to work for the specific scenario.
