# Disciplined Agile principle 4: Context counts

**Context counts: Acknowledge that every team and organization is unique. Your 'Way of Working' (WoW) must reflect your specific environment and evolve over time.**

Every team, every project, and every organization has its own set of challenges. In Disciplined Agile, this means you must account for several scaling factors.

- Team Size: A team of 5 people communicates differently than a team of 50 or 500 or 5000.
- Geographic Distribution: Being in one room is different from being spread across time zones.
- Organizational Complexity: Startups have different hurdles than hospitals with regulatory requirements.
- Technical Debt: Working on a brand-new app is different from modifying a legacy mainframe system.

Because of this, DA doesn't give you a "recipe"; it gives you a toolkit. Instead
of saying "Do X", ask "What is the context?" then look toward the strategies
most likely to work for the specific scenario.
