# Agile and Enterprise Project Portfolio Management (EPPM)

Agile and Enterprise Project Portfolio Management (EPPM) combine strategic oversight with flexible execution, enabling organizations to align high-level business goals with rapid, team-level delivery. EPPM provides the structure for governance, resource allocation, and prioritization, while Agile ensures iterative value delivery, creating a hybrid approach that enhances responsiveness to market changes. 

## Key Intersection of Agile and EPPM

Strategic Alignment: EPPM ensures that, even with decentralized, Agile-driven teams, all initiatives are directly aligned with long-term business strategy and objectives. 

Balancing Control and Flexibility: EPPM offers the necessary governance for finance and risk management, allowing Agile teams to operate autonomously within those guardrails. 

Optimized Resource Management: EPPM tools help manage capacity and demand across the organization, preventing resource bottlenecks and ensuring high-value initiatives are prioritized. 

Improved Visibility: Through centralized systems, leadership gains real-time visibility into the health and performance of both waterfall and agile projects. 

## Benefits of Integrating Agile in EPPM

Accelerated Value Delivery: By using Agile practices, organizations can deliver working solutions in smaller, faster increments, reducing the time to ROI. 

Increased Responsiveness: Organizations can adapt quickly to changing market conditions by reprioritizing the portfolio, reducing time spent on obsolete projects. 

Better Decision-Making: Data-driven insights from EPPM platforms empower leaders to make informed, fast decisions on project funding and continuation.
