# Agile and Enterprise Project Portfolio Management (EPPM)

Agile and Enterprise Project Portfolio Management (EPPM) combine strategic
oversight with flexible execution, enabling organizations to align high-level
business goals with rapid, team-level delivery. EPPM provides the structure for
governance, resource allocation, and prioritization, while Agile ensures
iterative value delivery.

EPPM ensures that even with decentralized, Agile-driven teams, all initiatives
remain directly aligned with long-term business strategy and objectives. This
balance of control and flexibility is central to the integration: EPPM offers
the governance needed for finance and risk management, while Agile teams operate
autonomously within those guardrails.

Organizations that integrate Agile into EPPM accelerate value delivery by
shipping working solutions in smaller, faster increments, reducing time to ROI.
They also gain responsiveness: the portfolio can be reprioritized quickly when
market conditions shift, cutting time spent on obsolete projects. Data-driven
insights from EPPM platforms empower leaders to make informed, fast decisions on
project funding and continuation.
