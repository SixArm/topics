# Customer Experience (CX)

Question: What is the primary goal of maintaining consistency across all customer touchpoints and interactions?

- [ ] To reduce the cost of customer service operations
- [ ] To build trust and reliability with customers
- [ ] To collect more customer data for analytics
- [ ] To increase the number of touchpoints available

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>To build trust and reliability with customers</p>
  <p>Consistency across all touchpoints and interactions builds trust and reliability. When customers receive a unified experience regardless of the channel or device they use to engage with a brand, it reinforces their confidence in the company and creates a dependable relationship that encourages loyalty.</p>
</details>
