# Agile principle 6: Face-to-face

Question: According to Agile Principle 6, what is considered the most efficient and effective method of conveying information to and within a development team?

- [ ] Comprehensive documentation with detailed specifications
- [ ] Asynchronous communication through email chains and ticket systems
- [ ] Face-to-face conversation
- [ ] Formal scheduled meetings with written agendas

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>Face-to-face conversation</p>
  <p>Agile Principle 6 explicitly states that face-to-face conversation is the gold standard for conveying information because it enables immediate feedback, reduces misunderstandings, and builds stronger relationships among team members. This approach allows for real-time clarification, gestures, drawings, and dynamic interaction that written communication cannot match.</p>
</details>
