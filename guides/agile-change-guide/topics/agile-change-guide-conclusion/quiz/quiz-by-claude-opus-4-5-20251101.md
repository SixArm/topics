# Agile Change Guide Conclusion

Question: What is the primary purpose of providing a repository URL and contact information in the Agile Change Guide conclusion?

- [ ] To showcase the author's technical credentials
- [ ] To enable readers to provide feedback, ask questions, and suggest improvements
- [ ] To promote commercial consulting services
- [ ] To restrict access to guide updates

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>To enable readers to provide feedback, ask questions, and suggest improvements</p>
  <p>The conclusion emphasizes that "feedback and suggestions are very much appreciated, because this helps the guide improve and evolve." The repository and email contact are provided specifically so readers can open issues to ask questions, suggest improvements, or point out errors, reflecting the agile principle of continuous improvement through collaboration.</p>
</details>
