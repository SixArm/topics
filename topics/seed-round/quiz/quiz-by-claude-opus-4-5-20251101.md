# Seed round

Question: What is the typical funding range for a seed round?

- [ ] $10 million to $50 million
- [ ] $100,000 to $2 million
- [ ] $5 million to $10 million
- [ ] $50,000 or less

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>$100,000 to $2 million</p>
  <p>Seed rounds typically provide funding in the range of $100,000 to $2 million. This early-stage financing comes from angel investors or early-stage venture capital firms and is used to support the startup's initial growth, including hiring employees, marketing, and product development.</p>
</details>
