# Return on Investment (ROI)

Question: How is Return on Investment (ROI) calculated?

- [ ] (Total Investment / Net Profit) x 100
- [ ] (Net Profit / Total Investment) x 100
- [ ] (Revenue - Total Investment) / Revenue
- [ ] Net Profit + Total Investment

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>(Net Profit / Total Investment) x 100</p>
  <p>ROI is calculated by dividing the net profit generated by an investment by the total amount invested, then multiplying by 100 to express it as a percentage. This formula provides a straightforward way to evaluate and compare the financial performance of different investments.</p>
</details>
