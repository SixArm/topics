# Assignment Agreement

Question: What is the primary function of an assignment agreement in business and legal contexts?

- [ ] To establish a partnership between two companies with shared ownership
- [ ] To create a new legal entity for joint business operations
- [ ] To transfer rights, property, or obligations from one party (assignor) to another party (assignee)
- [ ] To terminate an existing contract between two parties

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>To transfer rights, property, or obligations from one party (assignor) to another party (assignee)</p>
  <p>An assignment agreement is a legal contract that enables the transfer of specific rights, property, or obligations from the assignor to the assignee. This mechanism is widely used in intellectual property (patents, trademarks, copyrights), real estate (leases), and business transactions (contracts, customer lists). The agreement specifies terms, conditions, consideration, and any restrictions on the assignment.</p>
</details>
