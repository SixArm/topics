# Financial Products Markup Language (FPML)

Question: What organization developed Financial Products Markup Language (FPML) to standardize communication of complex financial products?

- [ ] World Bank Financial Standards Committee
- [ ] International Swaps and Derivatives Association (ISDA)
- [ ] Securities and Exchange Commission (SEC)
- [ ] Financial Accounting Standards Board (FASB)

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>International Swaps and Derivatives Association (ISDA)</p>
  <p>FPML was developed by the International Swaps and Derivatives Association (ISDA) as a response to the need for a standard way of communicating complex financial products across different platforms and systems. ISDA created this XML-based standard to facilitate communication between financial institutions and enable automation of trade capture, risk management, and trade confirmation processes.</p>
</details>
