# International Standard on Assurance Engagements 3000 (ISAE 3000)

Question: What is the primary purpose of ISAE 3000?

- [ ] To establish guidelines for auditing financial statements and balance sheets
- [ ] To provide a framework for assurance engagements on non-financial information such as internal controls and sustainability reporting
- [ ] To regulate international tax compliance and cross-border financial transactions
- [ ] To standardize software development lifecycle practices across global organizations

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>To provide a framework for assurance engagements on non-financial information such as internal controls and sustainability reporting</p>
  <p>ISAE 3000 is a global standard developed by the International Auditing and Assurance Standards Board (IAASB) specifically for assurance engagements that are NOT audits or reviews of financial statements. It covers engagements such as providing assurance on internal controls, sustainability reporting, social responsibility, information security, risk management, compliance, and corporate governance practices.</p>
</details>
