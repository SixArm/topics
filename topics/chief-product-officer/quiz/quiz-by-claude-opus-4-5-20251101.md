# Chief Product Officer (CPO)

Question: What is the primary role of a Chief Product Officer (CPO) in an organization?

- [ ] Managing the company's financial reporting and investor relations
- [ ] Leading the engineering team in writing and reviewing code
- [ ] Overseeing the development and management of the company's product portfolio
- [ ] Directing the company's legal compliance and regulatory affairs

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>Overseeing the development and management of the company's product portfolio</p>
  <p>The CPO is a high-level executive responsible for the development and management of a company's product portfolio. This includes identifying new product opportunities, developing product roadmaps, ensuring products align with company strategy, leading product teams, and managing the entire product lifecycle from ideation to retirement.</p>
</details>
