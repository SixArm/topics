# Venture capital books

Question: Which book is considered the comprehensive guide to the venture capital process, covering topics from negotiating term sheets to understanding valuation?

- [ ] "Mastering the VC Game" by Jeffrey Bussgang
- [ ] "Venture Deals" by Brad Feld and Jason Mendelson
- [ ] "The Startup Game" by William H. Draper III
- [ ] "Angel: How to Invest in Technology Startups" by Jason Calacanis

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>"Venture Deals" by Brad Feld and Jason Mendelson</p>
  <p>This book is widely regarded as the definitive guide to venture capital mechanics, providing a comprehensive walkthrough of the VC process including term sheet negotiation, valuation, and deal structures. It is essential reading for entrepreneurs seeking venture funding and professionals working in the startup ecosystem.</p>
</details>
