# AI Starter Guide Introduction

Question: What is the primary purpose of the AI Starter Guide?

- [ ] To provide comprehensive academic research papers on artificial intelligence algorithms
- [ ] To serve as a glossary guide for quick, easy learning about AI concepts, tactics, and ideas
- [ ] To offer certification training for AI engineering roles
- [ ] To document source code implementations for machine learning models

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>To serve as a glossary guide for quick, easy learning about AI concepts, tactics, and ideas</p>
  <p>The AI Starter Guide is explicitly described as "a glossary guide ebook that describes one topic per page" intended for "quick easy learning about concepts, tactics, and ideas." It helps project leaders, stakeholders, and students understand AI terminology to improve collaboration and communication in real-world projects.</p>
</details>
