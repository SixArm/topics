# Realtech (Real estate technology)

Question: What is the primary purpose of realtech (real estate technology)?

- [ ] To replace real estate agents entirely with automated systems
- [ ] To integrate technology into real estate to improve efficiency, accuracy, and transparency
- [ ] To exclusively focus on virtual reality property tours
- [ ] To eliminate the need for property inspections

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>To integrate technology into real estate to improve efficiency, accuracy, and transparency</p>
  <p>Realtech refers to the integration of technology into the real estate industry across various stages of the real estate lifecycle—including property management, buying and selling, leasing, and construction—with the goal of improving efficiency, accuracy, and transparency. It encompasses multiple technologies such as property management software, VR/AR, blockchain, big data analytics, and IoT rather than focusing on any single technology or replacing human professionals.</p>
</details>
