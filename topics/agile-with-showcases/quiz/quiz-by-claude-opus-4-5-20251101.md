# Agile with showcases

Question: What is the primary purpose of an agile showcase?

- [ ] To assign tasks and deadlines to development team members
- [ ] To demonstrate working software to stakeholders
- [ ] To conduct code reviews and technical debt assessments
- [ ] To estimate story points for upcoming sprints

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>To demonstrate working software to stakeholders</p>
  <p>Agile showcases are collaborative events where the team presents working software to stakeholders, allowing everyone involved to see new features and functionality together. This aligns with the agile principle of delivering working software frequently and gathering feedback through direct demonstration rather than documentation.</p>
</details>
