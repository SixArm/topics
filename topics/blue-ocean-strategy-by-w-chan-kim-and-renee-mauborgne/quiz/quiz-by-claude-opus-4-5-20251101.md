Question: What are the four action steps in the Blue Ocean Strategy framework for creating uncontested market space?

- [ ] Analyze, Plan, Execute, Monitor
- [ ] Eliminate, Reduce, Raise, Create
- [ ] Discover, Design, Develop, Deploy
- [ ] Research, Segment, Target, Position

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>Eliminate, Reduce, Raise, Create</p>
  <p>The Blue Ocean Strategy framework uses four actions: Eliminate factors no longer needed, Reduce factors below industry standard, Raise factors above industry standard, and Create factors the industry has never offered. This four-action framework helps companies break out of competitive "red oceans" and create new, uncontested market space where competition becomes irrelevant.</p>
</details>
