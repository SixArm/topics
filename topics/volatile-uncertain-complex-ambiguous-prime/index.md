# Volatile Uncertain Complex Ambiguous (VUCA) Prime

Volatile Uncertain Complex Ambiguous (VUCA) Prime is a model for navigating a VUCA World:

- Vision to counteract Volatility: Create a clear vision to steer through rapid change.
- Understanding to counter Uncertainty: Devote time to learning and gathering information.
- Clarity to counter Complexity: Simplify, focus on what matters, and clarify processes.
- Agility to counter Ambiguity: Remain flexible, adaptable, and responsive to new information.
