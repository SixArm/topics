# How to find startup mentors

Question: Which approach provides structured mentorship along with coaching, resources, and access to networks of experienced mentors as part of a formal program?

- [ ] Reaching out to industry experts via LinkedIn
- [ ] Attending networking events and pitch competitions
- [ ] Joining startup accelerators or incubators
- [ ] Seeking advice from angel investors and VCs

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>Joining startup accelerators or incubators</p>
  <p>Startup accelerators and incubators are specifically designed to provide comprehensive support including mentorship, coaching, and resources to help startups grow and succeed. Unlike informal networking or reaching out to individuals, these programs offer structured mentorship with established networks of experienced mentors who are committed to working with participating startups.</p>
</details>
