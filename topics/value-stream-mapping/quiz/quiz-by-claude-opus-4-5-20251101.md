# Value Stream Mapping (VSM)

Question: What is the primary purpose of Value Stream Mapping (VSM)?

- [ ] To create detailed financial budgets for manufacturing projects
- [ ] To analyze, visualize, and improve the flow of materials and information through a process
- [ ] To assign individual performance metrics to team members
- [ ] To document software architecture and database schemas

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>To analyze, visualize, and improve the flow of materials and information through a process</p>
  <p>Value Stream Mapping is a lean manufacturing technique that visualizes the sequence of activities required to transform raw materials or information into a finished product or service. Its key purpose is to identify areas of waste and inefficiency, enabling teams to design an improved future state that eliminates waste, improves efficiency, and reduces costs.</p>
</details>
