# Agile change SMART OKRs

Establish Core Agile Practices Across Development Teams:

- Increase teams practicing daily standups from 30% to 85% by Q2 end. Measure by team survey and observation audits.
- Boost sprint completion rate by 25% during Q1-Q2. Score by velocity tracking and sprint burndown charts.
- Run retrospectives for continuous improvement during each sprint. Track by retrospective attendance logs and action item completion rate.
- Complete Scrum Master certification for 12 team leads by June 30. Track by certification completion records.
- Launch story mapping workshops for backlog refinement on March 15. Track by workshop attendance and backlog quality assessments.

Build Agile Mindset and Culture:

- Present agile transformation progress to executive leadership on April 20 with the goal of securing continued support. Confirm by leadership approval and budget allocation.
- Raise employee engagement rating from 6.2 to 7.5 by year end. Score by quarterly engagement surveys.
- Ensure 50 managers are coming to Agile Leadership Summit on May 10. Quantify by registration confirmations and attendance.
- Improve psychological safety score by 40% during the next 6 months. Score by quarterly team health surveys.
- Interview 25 team members about agile adoption challenges during Q1 and report results. Quantify by interview completion rate and themes identified.

Accelerate Delivery Speed and Quality:

- Achieve lead time of 5 days or less during Q2-Q3. Time by cycle time metrics from project management tool.
- Accelerate deployment frequency from monthly to weekly by September 1. Time by deployment logs and release calendars.
- Grow automated test coverage by 8 steps (from 35% to 75%) by Q3 end. Measure by code coverage reports.
- Boost deployment success rate score by 30% during next quarter. Score by production incident reports and rollback frequency.
- Reduce defect escape rate from 15% to 5% by August 31. Measure by production defect tracking system.

Implement Technical Excellence Practices:

- Run pair programming sessions for knowledge sharing during each sprint. Track by pair programming logs and knowledge transfer assessments.
- Launch CI/CD pipeline for automated deployments on February 28. Track by pipeline usage metrics and deployment automation rate.
- Complete technical debt reduction initiative for legacy codebase by Q2 end. Track by code quality metrics and refactoring story points completed.
- Increase code review participation from 60% to 95% by June 15. Measure by pull request review statistics.
- Achieve 10 points for Definition of Done compliance by sprint 6. Score by DoD checklist adherence audits.

Strengthen Customer Collaboration:

- Interview 15 customers about product needs and pain points during Q1 and report results. Quantify by interview completion and insights documented.
- Improve customer satisfaction score by 35% during the next two quarters. Score by NPS surveys and customer feedback forms.
- Run sprint reviews with customer participation during each sprint. Track by customer attendance logs and feedback received.
- Increase customer-facing feature releases from 4 to 12 per quarter by Q3. Measure by release notes and feature deployment tracking.
- Present product roadmap to 30 key stakeholders on March 31 with the goal of alignment. Confirm by stakeholder approval signatures and feedback forms.

Scale Agile Practices Organization-Wide:

- Grow agile team adoption from 8 teams to 25 teams by December 31. Measure by team registration in agile program and practice assessments.
- Manage 5 Agile Coaches to accomplish team training rollout by Q3 end. Quantify by coaching engagement hours and teams coached.
- Evaluate 3 scaling frameworks (SAFe, LeSS, Scrum@Scale) for enterprise adoption during Q1 and choose the result. Confirm by framework assessment report and leadership decision.
  Boost Agile maturity assessment score by 45% during the year. Score by bi-annual maturity model evaluations.
  Launch Communities of Practice for agile practitioners on April 1. Track by CoP membership and monthly meeting attendance.

Optimize Agile Processes and Tools:

- Achieve sprint planning time of 2 hours or less during Q2. Time by sprint planning duration logs.
- Fire-drill time to restore service 3 times on June 15. Time by incident response drills and mean time to recovery metrics.
- Get unified project management tool for all agile teams by March 15. Confirm by tool procurement, implementation completion, and team adoption rate.
- Reduce meeting overhead from 35% to 20% of weekly capacity by Q2 end. Measure by calendar analysis and time-tracking data.
- Improve backlog refinement efficiency by 30% during next quarter. Score by story readiness at sprint planning and refinement session duration.

Develop Leadership Support for Agile Transformation:

- Interview 10 executives about transformation barriers during February and report results. Quantify by interview completion and action plan created.
- Present business case for agile investment to board of directors on March 20 with the goal of budget approval. Confirm by board vote and funding allocated.
- Ensure 8 senior leaders complete Agile Leadership training by May 31. Quantify by training completion certificates.
- Raise leadership agile fluency rating from 2.5 to 4.0 by Q3 end. Score by 360-degree leadership assessment.
- Manage 20 middle managers to accomplish team empowerment practices by August 15. Quantify by manager coaching sessions and team autonomy metrics.
