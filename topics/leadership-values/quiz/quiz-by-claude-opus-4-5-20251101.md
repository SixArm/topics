# Leadership values

Question: Which leadership value is most essential for building trust and confidence by being honest, ethical, and consistent in behavior and decision-making?

- [ ] Empathy
- [ ] Continuous learning
- [ ] Integrity
- [ ] Vision

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>Integrity</p>
  <p>Integrity involves being honest, ethical, and consistent in behavior and decision-making. Leaders with integrity are transparent and accountable for their actions, which inspires trust and confidence among team members and stakeholders. This value is foundational for technology professionals in leadership roles, as it establishes credibility and creates an environment where teams can collaborate effectively.</p>
</details>
