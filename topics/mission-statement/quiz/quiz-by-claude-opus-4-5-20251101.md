# Mission statement

Question: What are the four key elements that a well-crafted mission statement should include?

- [ ] Budget, timeline, resources, and milestones
- [ ] Purpose, values, target audience, and distinctiveness
- [ ] Revenue, market share, growth rate, and profitability
- [ ] Products, services, pricing, and distribution channels

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>Purpose, values, target audience, and distinctiveness</p>
  <p>A well-crafted mission statement should articulate the company's purpose (reason for existence), reflect its core values and principles, identify its target audience and their needs, and differentiate the company from competitors by highlighting unique strengths and advantages.</p>
</details>
