# First-mover advantage

Question: What is a key risk associated with pursuing a first-mover advantage strategy?

- [ ] The company gains exclusive patent rights automatically
- [ ] Brand recognition becomes impossible to achieve
- [ ] Competitors can learn from the first mover's results and enter the market with lower costs or better products
- [ ] Customer loyalty is guaranteed for the first entrant

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>Competitors can learn from the first mover's results and enter the market with lower costs or better products</p>
  <p>While first movers gain advantages like setting market standards and establishing early relationships, a significant risk is that competitors can analyze their results and leverage those insights to enter the market more efficiently—with lower costs, improved products, or more effective marketing—potentially capturing market share from the pioneer.</p>
</details>
