# Accel.com

Question: What type of startup stage does Accel primarily focus its investments on?

- [ ] Late-stage startups preparing for IPO
- [ ] Early-stage startups in the technology sector
- [ ] Mature companies seeking acquisition financing
- [ ] Pre-revenue startups without product-market fit

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>Early-stage startups in the technology sector</p>
  <p>Accel is a global venture capital firm that primarily invests in early-stage startups in the technology sector, with particular focus on enterprise software, cloud computing, cybersecurity, and fintech. The firm typically invests between $10 million to $50 million in each portfolio company and aims to partner with entrepreneurs building disruptive products that solve real-world problems.</p>
</details>
