# Fundraising

https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Fundraising

Fundraising or fund-raising is the process of seeking and gathering voluntary
financial contributions by engaging individuals, businesses, charitable
foundations, or governmental agencies. Although fundraising typically refers to
efforts to gather money for non-profit organizations, it is sometimes used to
refer to the identification and solicitation of investors or other sources of
capital for for-profit enterprises.
