# Business analysis

Question: What is the primary purpose of business analysis?

- [ ] To implement new software systems across all departments
- [ ] To evaluate operations, procedures, and systems to identify areas for improvement and growth
- [ ] To manage employee performance reviews and compensation
- [ ] To handle customer complaints and support tickets

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>To evaluate operations, procedures, and systems to identify areas for improvement and growth</p>
  <p>Business analysis is fundamentally about examining an organization's current state—its operations, procedures, and systems—to synthesize data into meaningful insights that inform decisions and help develop strategies for improving business processes, products, and services.</p>
</details>
