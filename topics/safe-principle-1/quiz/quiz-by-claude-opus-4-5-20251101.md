# Take an economic view

Question: According to SAFe Principle 1, what is the key factor that must guide everyday decisions when delivering value in the shortest sustainable lead time?

- [ ] Maximizing team velocity across all sprints
- [ ] Strict adherence to the original project scope
- [ ] Understanding the economics of building systems, including trade-offs between risk, Cost of Delay, and various costs
- [ ] Prioritizing technical excellence over business outcomes

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>Understanding the economics of building systems, including trade-offs between risk, Cost of Delay, and various costs</p>
  <p>SAFe Principle 1 "Take an economic view" emphasizes that delivering the best value and quality in the shortest sustainable lead time requires a fundamental understanding of the economics of building systems. This includes making everyday decisions in a proper economic context, considering trade-offs between risk, Cost of Delay (CoD), manufacturing, operational, and development costs, while operating within approved budgets and guardrails that support decentralized decision-making.</p>
</details>
