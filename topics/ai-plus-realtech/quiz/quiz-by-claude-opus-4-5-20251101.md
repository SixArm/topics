# AI + realtech

Question: Which AI application in real estate technology (realtech) combines analysis of historical sales data, market trends, and property features to generate accurate pricing recommendations?

- [ ] Smart Real Estate integration with IoT devices
- [ ] Property Valuation
- [ ] Financing Assistance for mortgage automation
- [ ] Property Management for maintenance detection

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>Property Valuation</p>
  <p>Property Valuation uses AI to analyze historical sales data, market trends, and property features to provide accurate pricing recommendations. This is distinct from other realtech applications like Property Management (which focuses on maintenance and repairs), Financing Assistance (which handles mortgage processes and creditworthiness), and Smart Real Estate (which integrates with IoT devices for energy optimization and security).</p>
</details>
