# Agile and flow state

Question: What is a key reason why agile methodologies support achieving flow state in developers?

- [ ] Agile requires all team members to work at the same pace regardless of individual preferences
- [ ] Agile eliminates the need for feedback by establishing fixed requirements upfront
- [ ] Agile emphasizes self-organizing teams that empower developers to make autonomous decisions about their work
- [ ] Agile maximizes cognitive load through mandatory pair programming sessions

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>Agile emphasizes self-organizing teams that empower developers to make autonomous decisions about their work</p>
  <p>Autonomy is crucial for flow state because it allows individuals to tailor their working methods to their personal rhythms and preferences. The agile emphasis on self-organizing teams gives developers ownership of their work and freedom in implementation approaches, which are essential conditions for achieving and maintaining flow state.</p>
</details>
