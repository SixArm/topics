# Pay-what-you-want (PWYW)

Question: What is a primary advantage of pay-what-you-want (PWYW) pricing for businesses?

- [ ] It guarantees higher revenue than fixed pricing models
- [ ] It eliminates the need for market research
- [ ] It increases customer satisfaction and loyalty by giving customers a sense of control
- [ ] It prevents customers from paying below the cost of production

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>It increases customer satisfaction and loyalty by giving customers a sense of control</p>
  <p>PWYW pricing enables customers to feel a sense of control over what they pay, which can increase customer satisfaction and loyalty. This psychological benefit is a key advantage of the model, though businesses must balance it against challenges like unpredictable revenue and the risk of customers paying very little or nothing.</p>
</details>
