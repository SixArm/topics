# Brand positioning

Question: What is the primary goal of brand positioning?

- [ ] To maximize advertising spend across all available marketing channels
- [ ] To create a distinctive logo and visual identity system for the brand
- [ ] To identify a unique, relevant market position that differentiates the brand from competitors
- [ ] To match competitors' pricing strategies and product features

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>To identify a unique, relevant market position that differentiates the brand from competitors</p>
  <p>Brand positioning is fundamentally about defining and communicating a brand's unique value proposition and competitive advantage. The goal is to identify a position in the market that is both different from competitors and meaningful to the target audience. This requires understanding customer needs, analyzing competitors, and articulating a clear value propositionâ€”not simply copying competitors or focusing solely on visual elements.</p>
</details>
