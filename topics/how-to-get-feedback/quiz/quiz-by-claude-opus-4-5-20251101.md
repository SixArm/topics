# How to get feedback

Question: What is the most effective approach when seeking feedback from others?

- [ ] Ask yes/no questions to get quick and direct answers
- [ ] Focus on collecting feedback only from supervisors since they have authority
- [ ] Become defensive to show you care deeply about your work
- [ ] Ask open-ended questions and be specific about what areas you want feedback on

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>Ask open-ended questions and be specific about what areas you want feedback on</p>
  <p>When seeking feedback, being specific about the areas you want feedback on helps others provide targeted insights, while asking open-ended questions encourages detailed, thoughtful responses rather than simple yes/no answers. This combination maximizes the value and actionability of the feedback you receive.</p>
</details>
