# AI for change management

Question: Which of the following best describes a key capability of AI in change management?

- [ ] Replacing all human decision-makers during organizational transitions
- [ ] Analyzing data to provide insights, predict change impacts, and deliver personalized communication to stakeholders
- [ ] Eliminating the need for employee training during technology adoption
- [ ] Automating the entire change process without any management oversight

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>Analyzing data to provide insights, predict change impacts, and deliver personalized communication to stakeholders</p>
  <p>AI assists change management by analyzing large volumes of data to identify the need for change and predict impacts, simulating scenarios before implementation, understanding stakeholder sentiments through communication analysis, and delivering personalized messages and training materials through chatbots and virtual assistants. AI supports and enhances human-led change management rather than replacing human oversight.</p>
</details>
