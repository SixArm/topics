# AI + biotech

Question: Which AI + biotech application involves analyzing individual patient data, including genomic information and medical records, to predict disease risk and enable personalized treatment plans?

- [ ] Bioprocess Optimization
- [ ] Precision Healthcare
- [ ] Synthetic Biology
- [ ] Imaging Diagnostics

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>Precision Healthcare</p>
  <p>Precision Healthcare uses AI to analyze individual patient data—including genomic information, medical records, and clinical data—to predict disease, identify optimal treatments, and enable personalized medicine. This represents one of the most transformative applications of AI in biotech because it shifts healthcare from a one-size-fits-all approach to tailored interventions based on each patient's unique biological profile.</p>
</details>
