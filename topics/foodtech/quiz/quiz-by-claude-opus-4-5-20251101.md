# Foodtech

Question: What technology is primarily used in foodtech to enhance food safety and traceability by monitoring and tracing the journey of food products from farm to fork?

- [ ] Virtual reality simulations
- [ ] Blockchain technology and IoT devices
- [ ] Social media analytics
- [ ] 3D printing systems

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>Blockchain technology and IoT devices</p>
  <p>Foodtech solutions utilize blockchain technology and Internet of Things (IoT) devices to implement robust tracking systems that monitor and trace food products throughout the supply chain. This combination enhances transparency, enables faster recalls during foodborne outbreaks, and helps build consumer trust by providing verifiable records of a product's journey from farm to fork.</p>
</details>
