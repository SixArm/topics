# Antler.co

Question: What distinguishes Antler's business model from traditional venture capital firms?

- [ ] Antler only invests in Series A or later funding rounds
- [ ] Antler proactively builds startups from scratch by forming teams of talented individuals to co-found new companies
- [ ] Antler focuses exclusively on acquiring and restructuring failing startups
- [ ] Antler operates as a passive investor without providing mentorship or support

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>Antler proactively builds startups from scratch by forming teams of talented individuals to co-found new companies</p>
  <p>Antler follows a unique startup generator model where, instead of solely investing in existing startups, it identifies talented individuals with diverse backgrounds and skill sets, forms them into teams, and aims to co-found and launch new startups. This proactive approach to building companies from scratch distinguishes Antler from traditional VC firms that typically only invest in already-formed startups.</p>
</details>
