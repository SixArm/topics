# SCAMPER

Question: What does the SCAMPER acronym represent as a creative thinking technique?

- [ ] Simplify, Create, Analyze, Measure, Prototype, Evaluate, Refine
- [ ] Substitute, Combine, Adapt, Modify, Put to another use, Eliminate, Reverse
- [ ] Scale, Collaborate, Automate, Monitor, Plan, Execute, Review
- [ ] Scope, Cost, Assumptions, Milestones, Priorities, Estimates, Resources

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>Substitute, Combine, Adapt, Modify, Put to another use, Eliminate, Reverse</p>
  <p>SCAMPER is a creative thinking technique where each letter represents a prompt for examining and transforming existing ideas: Substitute (replace parts), Combine (merge elements), Adapt (modify for different contexts), Modify (alter to improve), Put to another use (find alternative applications), Eliminate (remove or simplify), and Reverse (flip assumptions or perspectives).</p>
</details>
