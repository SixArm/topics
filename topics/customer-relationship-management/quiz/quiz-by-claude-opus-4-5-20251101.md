# Customer relationship management (CRM)

Question: What is the primary goal of Customer Relationship Management (CRM)?

- [ ] To automate all business operations without human intervention
- [ ] To improve customer satisfaction, loyalty, and retention
- [ ] To replace traditional marketing with digital advertising
- [ ] To minimize the need for customer service representatives

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>To improve customer satisfaction, loyalty, and retention</p>
  <p>CRM refers to the strategies, processes, and technologies that businesses use to manage and analyze interactions with their customers and potential customers. The fundamental goal of CRM is to improve customer satisfaction, loyalty, and retention by providing a better understanding of customer needs and preferences, enabling businesses to tailor their offerings and build lasting relationships.</p>
</details>
