# Company departments

Question: Which department is typically responsible for ensuring that a technology company complies with legal requirements, contracts, and intellectual property protections?

- [ ] Human resources department
- [ ] Legal department
- [ ] Operations department
- [ ] Finance department

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>Legal department</p>
  <p>The legal department is responsible for ensuring that the company complies with laws, regulations, and contractual obligations. For technology professionals, this department is particularly important as it handles intellectual property protections, software licensing agreements, data privacy compliance (such as GDPR), and contract negotiations with vendors and partners.</p>
</details>
