# Demand forecasting

Question: What is the primary purpose of demand forecasting for technology businesses?

- [ ] To eliminate the need for customer feedback and market research
- [ ] To guarantee exact future sales numbers with complete accuracy
- [ ] To plan and allocate resources such as production, inventory, and budgets to meet anticipated demand
- [ ] To replace qualitative analysis with purely automated systems

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>To plan and allocate resources such as production, inventory, and budgets to meet anticipated demand</p>
  <p>Demand forecasting is fundamentally about predicting future demand so businesses can effectively plan production schedules, capacity capabilities, inventory levels, pricing strategies, and marketing budgets. This enables organizations to align their resources with anticipated customer needs, reducing waste from overproduction while avoiding stockouts or capacity shortfalls.</p>
</details>
