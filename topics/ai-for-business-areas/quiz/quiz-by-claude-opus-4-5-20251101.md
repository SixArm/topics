# AI for business areas

Question: Which of the following best describes how AI can be applied across multiple business functions to transform organizational operations?

- [ ] AI is limited to customer-facing applications like chatbots and virtual assistants
- [ ] AI applications are restricted to technical departments such as IT and software development
- [ ] AI can be integrated across diverse areas including strategy, sales, marketing, HR, accounting, and customer service to improve efficiency and effectiveness
- [ ] AI is primarily useful for automating repetitive tasks and has limited strategic value

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>AI can be integrated across diverse areas including strategy, sales, marketing, HR, accounting, and customer service to improve efficiency and effectiveness</p>
  <p>AI has broad applicability across virtually all business functions. In strategy, it enables competitive intelligence and scenario analysis. In sales, it supports lead scoring and forecasting. In marketing, it powers customer segmentation and sentiment analysis. In accounting, it automates bookkeeping and fraud detection. In HR, it assists with résumé screening and employee engagement. In customer service, it enables chatbots and personalized assistance. This cross-functional capability makes AI a transformative technology for entire organizations, not just isolated departments.</p>
</details>
