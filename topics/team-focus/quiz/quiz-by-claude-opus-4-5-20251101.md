# Team Focus

Question: What two categories does the TEAM FOCUS framework divide team effectiveness into?

- [ ] Strategy and execution
- [ ] Interpersonal and analytical
- [ ] Planning and implementation
- [ ] Individual and organizational

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>Interpersonal and analytical</p>
  <p>The TEAM FOCUS framework, developed by McKinsey & Company, divides team effectiveness into two categories: TEAM (interpersonal) covering Talk, Evaluate, Assist, and Motivate; and FOCUS (analytical) covering Frame, Organize, Collect, Understand, and Synthesize.</p>
</details>
