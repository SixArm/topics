# Roll-on Roll-off (RoRo)

Question: What is the primary purpose of the Roll-on Roll-off (RoRo) strategy in project management?

- [ ] To deliver the entire project scope at once for faster completion
- [ ] To permanently lock in resources at the project start
- [ ] To add or remove resources and deliverables at specific intervals or stages
- [ ] To eliminate the need for stakeholder communication

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>To add or remove resources and deliverables at specific intervals or stages</p>
  <p>Roll-on Roll-off (RoRo) is a project execution strategy that allows resources and deliverables to be added or removed at different phases of a project. This approach enables flexibility, optimizes resource utilization, supports incremental delivery, and helps mitigate risks by breaking complex projects into manageable portions.</p>
</details>
