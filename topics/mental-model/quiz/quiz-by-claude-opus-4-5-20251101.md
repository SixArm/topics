# Mental Model

Question: What is the primary purpose of a mental model in technology and problem-solving contexts?

- [ ] To create detailed documentation of system architecture
- [ ] To replace the need for formal training and education
- [ ] To simplify complex phenomena by abstracting essential elements, enabling prediction and decision-making
- [ ] To ensure all team members have identical thought processes

  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>To simplify complex phenomena by abstracting essential elements, enabling prediction and decision-making</p>
