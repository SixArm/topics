# Economic Moat

Question: What is an economic moat?

- [ ] A financial metric measuring a company's debt-to-equity ratio
- [ ] A government subsidy program for technology startups
- [ ] A company's competitive advantage that protects its market share and profitability from competitors
- [ ] A type of corporate insurance against market downturns

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>A company's competitive advantage that protects its market share and profitability from competitors</p>
  <p>An economic moat describes a company's sustainable competitive advantage that protects it from external threats, similar to how a moat around a castle keeps out intruders. Examples include brand strength, network effects, cost advantages, switching costs, and regulatory advantages. Companies with strong economic moats can generate above-average returns and sustain their competitive position over the long term.</p>
</details>
