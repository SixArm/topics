# Brand visibility

Question: What is brand visibility primarily a measure of?

- [ ] The financial value of a brand's intellectual property
- [ ] How often and easily consumers can recognize and recall a brand
- [ ] The number of products a company offers in its portfolio
- [ ] The total advertising budget allocated to marketing campaigns

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>How often and easily consumers can recognize and recall a brand</p>
  <p>Brand visibility refers to the extent to which a brand is recognized and remembered by its target audience through various channels and touchpoints such as advertising, social media, packaging, and events. High brand visibility makes it easier for customers to remember and choose a brand over competitors when making purchase decisions, ultimately contributing to increased market share and customer loyalty.</p>
</details>
