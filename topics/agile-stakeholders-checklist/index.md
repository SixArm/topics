# Agile stakeholders checklist

Agile stakeholders checklist thanks to Mountain Goat Software.

- Stakeholders know which factors determine priorities.
- Stakeholders know which factors determine deadlines.
- Stakeholders accept plans that are expressed with ranges.
- Stakeholders and the team accept that plans may need to change.
- Stakeholders have frequent opportunities to evaluate the product.
- Stakeholders champion agile values and agile principles.
- Stakeholders see quality as a right and they respect it.
- Stakeholders delegate authority to teams.
- Stakeholders don’t take authority back at the first sign of trouble.
- Stakeholders support teams in experimenting.
- Stakeholders don't punish an unsuccessful experiment.
- Stakeholders communicate what they hope to achieve.
- Stakeholders communicate why they are becoming more agile.
- Stakeholders encourage learning new skills.
- Stakeholders encourage learning new ways of working.
- Stakeholders develop people.
