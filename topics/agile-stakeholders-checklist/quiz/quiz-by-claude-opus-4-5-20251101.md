# Agile stakeholders checklist

Question: According to the Agile stakeholders checklist, what should stakeholders do when a team's experiment is unsuccessful?

- [ ] Reassign the team to different projects
- [ ] Escalate the issue to senior management for review
- [ ] Support the team by not punishing them for the unsuccessful experiment
- [ ] Require detailed post-mortem documentation before allowing future experiments

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>Support the team by not punishing them for the unsuccessful experiment</p>
  <p>The checklist emphasizes that stakeholders should support teams in experimenting by not punishing unsuccessful experiments. This fosters a safe environment for innovation and learning, which is essential for agile teams to improve and discover better solutions through trial and iteration.</p>
</details>
