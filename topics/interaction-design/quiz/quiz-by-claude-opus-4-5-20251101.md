# Interaction design

Question: What is the primary focus of interaction design when creating digital products?

- [ ] Maximizing the visual complexity of interface elements
- [ ] Designing how users interact with a product to achieve specific goals and tasks
- [ ] Minimizing development costs through simplified features
- [ ] Implementing the latest technology trends regardless of user needs

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>Designing how users interact with a product to achieve specific goals and tasks</p>
  <p>Interaction design centers on creating meaningful and engaging interactions between users and digital products. It follows a user-centered approach, placing user needs, goals, and behaviors at the forefront to design interactions that help users accomplish their tasks effectively while ensuring usability and accessibility.</p>
</details>
