# Agile principle 8: Sustainable pace

Question: What is the primary reason Agile promotes sustainable development and a constant pace?

- [ ] To maximize short-term productivity during project deadlines
- [ ] To reduce the need for stakeholder involvement in the development process
- [ ] To ensure long-term success, team well-being, and consistent delivery of value
- [ ] To eliminate the need for technical practices like refactoring and testing

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>To ensure long-term success, team well-being, and consistent delivery of value</p>
  <p>Agile principle 8 recognizes that software development is a marathon, not a sprint. Sustainable pace avoids boom-and-bust cycles, reduces burnout, enables teams to maintain energy and creativity, and leads to benefits including reduced employee turnover, higher quality deliverables, more predictable timelines, and improved stakeholder satisfaction.</p>
</details>
