# Word-of-mouth marketing (WOMM)

Question: What is the foundational premise behind why word-of-mouth marketing is considered effective?

- [ ] It is less expensive than digital advertising campaigns
- [ ] It generates more website traffic than paid search ads
- [ ] People trust recommendations from friends and family more than traditional advertising
- [ ] It allows businesses to bypass social media algorithms

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>People trust recommendations from friends and family more than traditional advertising</p>
  <p>Word-of-mouth marketing is based on the premise that people trust the opinions and recommendations of their friends and family more than traditional advertising. This trust creates a sense of credibility that is difficult to achieve through other marketing channels, making personal recommendations highly influential in purchasing decisions.</p>
</details>
