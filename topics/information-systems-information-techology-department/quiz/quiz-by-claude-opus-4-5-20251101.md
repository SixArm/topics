# Information Systems (IS) or Information Technology (IT) Department

Question: What is a primary role of the IS/IT department in ensuring the organization's technology infrastructure remains effective?

- [ ] Conducting sales negotiations with external customers
- [ ] Managing quarterly financial reporting and audits
- [ ] Aligning technology initiatives with the organization's strategic goals
- [ ] Developing marketing campaigns for product launches

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>Aligning technology initiatives with the organization's strategic goals</p>
  <p>The IS/IT department collaborates with business leaders to align technology initiatives with the organization's overall strategic goals. This includes participating in strategic planning, assessing emerging technologies, and making recommendations on technology investments that can improve operational efficiency, drive innovation, and provide a competitive advantage. This strategic alignment ensures the technology infrastructure supports and enables business objectives.</p>
</details>
