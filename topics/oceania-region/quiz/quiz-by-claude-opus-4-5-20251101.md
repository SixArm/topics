# Oceania region

Question: What is one of the unique challenges that technology businesses in the Oceania region face?

- [ ] Lack of government support for startups
- [ ] Absence of venture capital investment opportunities
- [ ] Geographic distance from other major markets
- [ ] Limited natural resources for industry growth

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>Geographic distance from other major markets</p>
  <p>The Oceania region's isolation from other major markets can make it difficult for businesses to access resources and talent, and can increase the cost of doing business. However, this challenge is balanced by strong ties to Asia and the Pacific Rim, which can provide expansion opportunities.</p>
</details>
