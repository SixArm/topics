# Account Manager

Question: What is the primary focus of an Account Manager's role in an organization?

- [ ] Acquiring new customers through cold calling and lead generation campaigns
- [ ] Managing technical infrastructure and system deployments for clients
- [ ] Nurturing relationships with existing clients to ensure their satisfaction, retention, and long-term success
- [ ] Developing new products based on market research and competitor analysis

  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>Nurturing relationships with existing clients to ensure their satisfaction, retention, and long-term success</p>
