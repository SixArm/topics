# Code of Conduct

Question: What is the primary purpose of a code of conduct in an organization or community?

- [ ] To provide legal protection against lawsuits
- [ ] To establish guidelines for standards of behavior and ethical principles that individuals are expected to follow
- [ ] To create a hierarchy of authority within the organization
- [ ] To define salary structures and employee benefits

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>To establish guidelines for standards of behavior and ethical principles that individuals are expected to follow</p>
  <p>A code of conduct is fundamentally a set of guidelines that outlines the standards of behavior and ethical principles expected of individuals or organizations. It ensures that everyone is aware of their responsibilities and helps promote a positive and productive environment by establishing clear expectations for behavior, promoting accountability, and providing mechanisms for reporting and addressing violations.</p>
</details>
