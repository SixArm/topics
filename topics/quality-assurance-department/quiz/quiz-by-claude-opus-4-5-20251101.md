# Quality Assurance (QA) Department

Question: What is a primary responsibility of the QA department when defects or non-conformances are identified?

- [ ] Delegating all defect resolution to external contractors
- [ ] Tracking, managing, and coordinating timely resolution of defects while analyzing patterns to implement corrective actions
- [ ] Ignoring minor defects to focus only on critical issues
- [ ] Immediately halting all production until every defect is resolved

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>Tracking, managing, and coordinating timely resolution of defects while analyzing patterns to implement corrective actions</p>
  <p>Defect Management is a key QA responsibility that involves documenting, categorizing, and prioritizing defects, coordinating with relevant teams for timely resolution, and analyzing patterns and trends to identify root causes and implement corrective and preventive actions. This systematic approach ensures quality issues are addressed efficiently while preventing recurrence.</p>
</details>
