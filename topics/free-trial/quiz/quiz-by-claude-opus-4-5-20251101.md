# Free trial

Question: What is the primary purpose of offering a free trial in subscription-based businesses?

- [ ] To reduce the cost of customer acquisition by eliminating sales teams
- [ ] To allow customers to try the product before committing, building trust and demonstrating value
- [ ] To collect payment information for future marketing campaigns
- [ ] To permanently provide free access to basic features

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>To allow customers to try the product before committing, building trust and demonstrating value</p>
  <p>The primary purpose of a free trial is to give potential customers a chance to experience the product or service before making a purchase commitment.
