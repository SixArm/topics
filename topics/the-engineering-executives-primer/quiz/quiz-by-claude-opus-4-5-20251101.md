# The Engineering Executive's Primer

Question: According to The Engineering Executive's Primer, what is essential for building high-performing engineering teams?

- [ ] Focusing primarily on technical skills during hiring
- [ ] Creating a positive team culture, hiring the right people, and providing growth opportunities
- [ ] Minimizing team meetings to maximize individual productivity
- [ ] Implementing strict hierarchical management structures

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>Creating a positive team culture, hiring the right people, and providing growth opportunities</p>
  <p>The book emphasizes that building high-performing engineering teams requires creating a positive team culture, hiring the right people, and providing opportunities for professional growth and development. These elements work together to foster an environment where engineers can thrive and deliver excellent results.</p>
</details>
