# Pitching

Question: What is the primary purpose of an elevator pitch in a business context?

- [ ] To provide detailed financial forecasts and operational strategies
- [ ] To present a comprehensive visual slideshow to stakeholders
- [ ] To capture attention and convey a business idea in 30 to 60 seconds
- [ ] To outline the complete roadmap for achieving company objectives

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>To capture attention and convey a business idea in 30 to 60 seconds</p>
  <p>An elevator pitch is designed to be a concise and compelling presentation that captures someone's attention and communicates the essence of a business idea within the brief duration of an elevator ride (typically 30 to 60 seconds). The goal is to spark interest, generate curiosity, and leave a memorable impression that encourages further conversation or follow-up.</p>
</details>
