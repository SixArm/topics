# Product discovery

Question: What is the primary goal of product discovery?

- [ ] To develop a detailed product roadmap with milestones and timelines
- [ ] To create a product that solves a real problem for customers and is easy and intuitive to use
- [ ] To build high-fidelity prototypes for investor presentations
- [ ] To analyze competitor pricing strategies in the market

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>To create a product that solves a real problem for customers and is easy and intuitive to use</p>
  <p>Product discovery is fundamentally about identifying and solving real customer problems in a user-friendly way. While roadmaps and prototypes are part of the discovery process, they are means to an endâ€”the ultimate goal is creating a product that addresses genuine customer needs with good usability.
