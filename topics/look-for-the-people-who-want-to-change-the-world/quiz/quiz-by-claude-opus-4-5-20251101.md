# Look for the people who want to change the world

Question: What is Salesforce's "1-1-1 model" that reinforces its commitment to hiring people who want to change the world?

- [ ] Donating 1% of revenue, 1% of office space, and 1% of marketing budget to startups
- [ ] Allocating 1% of profits, 1% of inventory, and 1% of patents to government programs
- [ ] Donating 1% of equity, 1% of employees' time, and 1% of products to charitable causes
- [ ] Investing 1% of capital, 1% of technology, and 1% of partnerships into competitors

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>Donating 1% of equity, 1% of employees' time, and 1% of products to charitable causes</p>
  <p>Salesforce's 1-1-1 model demonstrates the company's commitment to values alignment by donating 1% of the company's equity, 1% of its employees' time, and 1% of its products to charitable causes. This approach embodies the belief that business can be a force for good and supports the company's mission of making a positive impact beyond typical business practices.</p>
</details>
