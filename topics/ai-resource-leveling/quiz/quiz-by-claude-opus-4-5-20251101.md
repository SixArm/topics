# AI resource leveling

Question: What is the primary purpose of AI in resource leveling?

- [ ] To replace human project managers entirely
- [ ] To efficiently allocate resources among tasks or projects
- [ ] To eliminate the need for skill assessments
- [ ] To reduce the number of employees needed

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>To efficiently allocate resources among tasks or projects</p>
  <p>AI resource leveling uses artificial intelligence to optimize the allocation of resources across tasks and projects. It accomplishes this through demand forecasting, optimal allocation considering constraints and objectives, skill matching, real-time adjustments, and continuous improvement based on past outcomes.</p>
</details>
