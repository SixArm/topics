# Strategic effects

Question: What is the primary purpose of strategic effects in business?

- [ ] To maximize short-term profits regardless of market position
- [ ] To reduce the number of products and services offered
- [ ] To improve a company's competitive advantage and market position through strategic decisions
- [ ] To minimize investment in research and development

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>To improve a company's competitive advantage and market position through strategic decisions</p>
  <p>Strategic effects refer to the benefits a company achieves by making strategic decisions that improve its position in the market. These effects are realized through strategies that increase competitive advantage, such as developing new products, improving customer service, entering new markets, achieving long-term sustainability, and creating a competitive edge through innovation and operational efficiency.</p>
</details>
