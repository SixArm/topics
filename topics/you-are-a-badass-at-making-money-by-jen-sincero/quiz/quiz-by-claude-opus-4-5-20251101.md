# You Are a Badass at Making Money by Jen Sincero

Question: According to Jen Sincero's book, what is the primary foundation for achieving financial success?

- [ ] Building a diverse investment portfolio early in your career
- [ ] Developing the right mindset and overcoming limiting beliefs
- [ ] Finding a high-paying job in a growing industry
- [ ] Minimizing all expenses and living frugally

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>Developing the right mindset and overcoming limiting beliefs</p>
  <p>Sincero emphasizes that mindset is the cornerstone of wealth creation. The first part of her book focuses on getting clear on what you want and understanding the role that mindset plays in achieving financial success. She provides strategies for overcoming limiting beliefs and building confidence through practices like positive affirmations and visualization, positioning mindset work as the essential foundation before practical financial steps.</p>
</details>
