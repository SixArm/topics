# Partner Relationship Management (PRM)

Question: What is the primary purpose of Partner Relationship Management (PRM) software?

- [ ] To replace Customer Relationship Management (CRM) systems entirely
- [ ] To manage internal employee performance reviews and compensation
- [ ] To facilitate and optimize management of partnerships and collaborations between organizations and their partners
- [ ] To automate financial accounting and tax compliance

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>To facilitate and optimize management of partnerships and collaborations between organizations and their partners</p>
  <p>PRM software provides a centralized platform to streamline partner interactions, communication, and collaboration, enabling businesses to enhance partner relationships and drive joint success. It encompasses enablement (onboarding, training, certification), sharing (communication, collaboration, performance tracking), opportunity management (deal registration, lead allocation), resource management, analytics, and integration with other enterprise systems like CRM and ERP.</p>
</details>
