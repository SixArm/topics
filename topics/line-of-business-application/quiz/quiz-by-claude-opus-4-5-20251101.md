# Line of Business (LOB) application

Question: What is the primary purpose of a Line of Business (LOB) application?

- [ ] To provide general-purpose computing capabilities across all departments
- [ ] To support and automate the operations of a specific department or business function
- [ ] To replace enterprise resource planning (ERP) systems entirely
- [ ] To serve as a central database for all company-wide data storage

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>To support and automate the operations of a specific department or business function</p>
  <p>LOB applications are specifically designed to address the unique needs, workflows, and processes of a particular department or business function within an organization. Examples include accounting systems, CRM software, inventory management systems, and HRMS. Unlike general-purpose software, LOB applications offer customized features, specialized reporting, and integrations tailored to support a specific line of business.</p>
</details>
