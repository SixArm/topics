# AliveCor Kardia 12L

The AliveCor Kardia 12L is a professional-grade, AI-powered 12-lead electrocardiogram system designed for clinical use by healthcare providers. It is FDA-cleared to detect 39 distinct cardiac determinations, including acute myocardial infarction and various arrhythmias. Unlike the consumer-oriented KardiaMobile 6L, the Kardia 12L targets medical professionals who need full diagnostic-quality ECG data at the point of care.

The device uses a reduced-lead design that produces a complete 12-lead ECG report from only five electrodes connected via a single cable with adhesive snap or tab connectors. This contrasts with the KardiaMobile 6L, which uses three touch-based electrodes and no cables. The Kardia 12L delivers clinical-grade signal quality while remaining far more portable than traditional 12-lead machines that require ten electrodes and multiple cables.

The system's AI engine, called KAI 12L, synthesizes the electrode data into a full 12-lead report and provides automated analysis. The companion KardiaStation app runs on tablets and smartphones, offering a professional interface for patient management, ECG review, and record-keeping suited to clinical workflows.

The Kardia 12L is not sold through retail channels. Activation requires a valid National Provider Identifier (NPI) number, restricting use to licensed healthcare professionals. Healthcare facilities typically request a quote directly from AliveCor. As of 2025, the device is eligible for reimbursement under newly established Category III CPT codes (0903T, 0904T, 0905T), which supports adoption by making the cost recoverable through standard billing processes.

The Kardia 12L fills a gap between consumer wearable ECG monitors and full-scale hospital equipment, giving clinicians a compact, AI-assisted tool for rapid cardiac assessment in outpatient, urgent care, and field settings.
