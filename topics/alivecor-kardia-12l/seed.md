# AliveCor Kardia 12L

The AliveCor Kardia 12L is a professional-grade, AI-powered 12-lead ECG system designed for clinical use rather than individual consumers. It is FDA-cleared to detect 39 different cardiac determinations, including acute myocardial infarction (heart attack) and various arrhythmias. 

Key Differences from KardiaMobile 6L
- While the 6L is a consumer device for personal monitoring, the 12L is a clinical tool intended for healthcare providers. 

| | KardiaMobile 6L	| Kardia 12L |
|-|----------------|------------|
| Intended User | Individuals/Consumers | Healthcare Professionals |
| Lead Count | 6 leads | 12 leads (Full clinical standard) |
| Electrodes | 3 (No cables) | 5 electrodes on a single cable |
| Heart Attack Detection | No | Yes (FDA-cleared for MI) |
| Determinations | ~6 common arrhythmias | 39 distinct cardiac conditions |

System Components & AI
- The system uses a unique reduced-lead design to produce a full 12-lead report using only five electrodes. 
- KAI 12L AI: The engine that synthesizes the 12-lead data and provides automated analysis.
- KardiaStation App: A specialized interface for tablets and smartphones that handles professional patient management.
- Cables & Electrodes: Unlike the touch-based 6L, the 12L uses a single cable with adhesive "snap or tab" electrodes for better clinical signal quality. 

Availability & Licensing
- The Kardia 12L is generally not available for direct purchase in retail stores.
- Professional Activation: To activate and use the device, a valid National Provider Identifier (NPI) number is required.
- Reimbursement: It is eligible for reimbursement under newly established Category III CPT codes (0903T, 0904T, 0905T) as of 2025.
- Purchase: Healthcare facilities must typically request a quote directly from AliveCor.
