# Demo Day

Question: What is the primary purpose of a demo day for startups?

- [ ] To finalize legal incorporation documents with potential partners
- [ ] To conduct market research surveys with industry experts
- [ ] To showcase progress, gain visibility, and potentially secure funding from investors
- [ ] To hire new employees through networking events

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>To showcase progress, gain visibility, and potentially secure funding from investors</p>
  <p>A demo day is an event typically held at the end of an accelerator program where startups pitch their products, services, and business plans to potential investors and stakeholders. The core purpose is to provide startups with an opportunity to demonstrate their progress, increase their visibility in the startup ecosystem, and attract the funding they need to grow their business.</p>
</details>
