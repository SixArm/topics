# Agile project management quotations

Question: Which quotation best captures the agile principle that the process of planning is more valuable than the resulting plan itself?

- [ ] "Goals are dreams with deadlines." ~ Diana Scharf
- [ ] "Plans are worthless. Planning is essential." ~ Dwight D. Eisenhower
- [ ] "A good plan today is better than a perfect plan tomorrow." ~ Proverb
- [ ] "Unity is strength... when there is teamwork and collaboration, wonderful things can be achieved." ~ Mattie Stepanek

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>"Plans are worthless. Planning is essential." ~ Dwight D. Eisenhower</p>
  <p>This quotation encapsulates a core agile principle: in dynamic environments, static plans quickly become outdated, but the act of planning builds shared understanding, surfaces risks, and aligns the team. Agile methodologies embrace iterative planning and adaptation over rigid adherence to upfront plans, making Eisenhower's insight particularly relevant for agile project managers.</p>
</details>
