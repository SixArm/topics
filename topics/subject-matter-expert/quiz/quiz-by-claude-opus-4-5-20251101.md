# Subject Matter Expert (SME)

Question: What is the primary role of a Subject Matter Expert (SME) in a technology organization?

- [ ] To manage project timelines and allocate team resources
- [ ] To provide specialized knowledge and guidance in a specific domain
- [ ] To handle customer support tickets and escalations
- [ ] To approve budgets and financial expenditures

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>To provide specialized knowledge and guidance in a specific domain</p>
  <p>A Subject Matter Expert (SME) is an individual who possesses specialized knowledge, expertise, and experience in a specific subject area. Their primary role is to advise colleagues and stakeholders by sharing subject knowledge, clarifying complex concepts, explaining industry practices, and accelerating decision-making related to their area of expertise.</p>
</details>
