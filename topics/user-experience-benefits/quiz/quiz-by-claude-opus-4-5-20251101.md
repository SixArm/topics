# User Experience (UX) - benefits

Question: What is a primary business benefit of investing in good user experience (UX) design?

- [ ] It eliminates the need for any product testing
- [ ] It reduces customer satisfaction by simplifying interfaces
- [ ] It increases support costs by adding more features
- [ ] It reduces support needs because users can easily understand and use the product

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>It reduces support needs because users can easily understand and use the product</p>
  <p>Good UX reduces the need for extensive customer support or training. When users can easily understand and use a product or service, they require fewer resources to get started or resolve issues. This translates to lower operational costs and better resource allocation for the organization.</p>
</details>
