# Advertising business model pros/cons

Question: Which of the following is a key advantage of the advertising business model for advertisers?

- [ ] Users are guaranteed to click on all displayed advertisements
- [ ] Advertisers can target ads to specific audiences based on demographics, interests, and behavior
- [ ] Ad-blocking software increases advertisement visibility
- [ ] Content creators receive 100% of advertising revenue

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>Advertisers can target ads to specific audiences based on demographics, interests, and behavior</p>
  <p>Targeting is a significant advantage of the advertising business model because it allows advertisers to reach specific audiences based on factors such as demographics, interests, and behavior. This precision helps increase the effectiveness of ads and reduces wasted ad spend, making advertising investments more efficient compared to broad, untargeted approaches.</p>
</details>
