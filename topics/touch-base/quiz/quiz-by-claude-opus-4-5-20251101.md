# Touch base

Question: What does the business idiom "touch base" most accurately describe?

- [ ] A formal meeting with a detailed agenda and documented outcomes
- [ ] A brief, informal communication to share updates or maintain connection
- [ ] A legal requirement for project documentation
- [ ] An in-depth technical review session

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>A brief, informal communication to share updates or maintain connection</p>
  <p>"Touch base" originates from baseball and refers to making brief, informal contact with someone to exchange information, get updates, or stay aligned. It emphasizes short check-ins rather than formal meetings, and is commonly used in professional contexts where teams need to periodically connect and coordinate.</p>
</details>
