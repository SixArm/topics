# Payment Card Industry Data Security Standard (PCI DSS)

Question: Which organizations must comply with PCI DSS requirements?

- [ ] Only banks and financial institutions that issue credit cards
- [ ] Any organization that accepts, processes, stores, or transmits credit card data
- [ ] Only e-commerce websites that process online payments
- [ ] Only large enterprises with over 1,000 employees handling payments

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>Any organization that accepts, processes, stores, or transmits credit card data</p>
  <p>PCI DSS applies broadly to any organization involved in handling cardholder data, regardless of size or industry. This includes merchants, service providers, and any entity that touches credit card information during transactions, storage, or transmission. The standard was established by major credit card companies to ensure consistent security practices across the entire payment ecosystem.</p>
</details>
