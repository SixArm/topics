# Total Addressable Market (TAM)

Question: What does Total Addressable Market (TAM) represent for a business?

- [ ] The company's current annual revenue from all product lines
- [ ] The total revenue potential of a market for a product or service
- [ ] The market share a company has captured relative to competitors
- [ ] The projected revenue after accounting for pricing and competition

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>The total revenue potential of a market for a product or service</p>
  <p>TAM represents the total market demand for a particular product or service, expressed as the total revenue potential available. It is distinct from a company's actual revenue or market shareâ€”TAM shows the entire opportunity in the market, while actual revenue depends on what portion of that market a company captures after factoring in competition, pricing, and other variables.</p>
