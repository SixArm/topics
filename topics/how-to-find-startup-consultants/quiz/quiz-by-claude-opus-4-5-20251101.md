# How to find startup consultants

Question: What is generally considered the most effective first step when seeking startup consultants?

- [ ] Posting job listings on general employment boards
- [ ] Cold emailing consultants found through internet searches
- [ ] Asking for referrals from other entrepreneurs or professionals in your network
- [ ] Hiring the first available consultant to save time

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>Asking for referrals from other entrepreneurs or professionals in your network</p>
  <p>Referrals from trusted contacts are highly valuable because they come with firsthand experience and insights about the consultant's work quality, reliability, and fit. Other entrepreneurs who have worked with consultants can provide candid assessments that help you make informed decisions and avoid potential mismatches.</p>
</details>
