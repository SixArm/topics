# Wizard UI

Question: What is the primary purpose of "task chunking" in a wizard UI?

- [ ] To enable users to skip irrelevant steps in the process
- [ ] To display visual progress bars showing completion percentage
- [ ] To break down complex processes into smaller, manageable steps that reduce cognitive load
- [ ] To provide real-time validation of user inputs at each step

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>To break down complex processes into smaller, manageable steps that reduce cognitive load</p>
  <p>Task chunking is a fundamental aspect of wizard UIs that divides complex tasks into smaller, focused chunks. This approach helps users concentrate on one step at a time, reducing the mental effort required to complete the overall process and making complex workflows more approachable.</p>
</details>
