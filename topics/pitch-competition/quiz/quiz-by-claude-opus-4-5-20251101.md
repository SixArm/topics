# Pitch competition

Question: What is the primary purpose of a pitch competition for entrepreneurs and startups?

- [ ] To conduct market research and gather customer feedback on product prototypes
- [ ] To present business ideas to judges and investors with the goal of securing funding or support
- [ ] To recruit technical co-founders and build engineering teams
- [ ] To finalize legal incorporation documents and equity structures

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>To present business ideas to judges and investors with the goal of securing funding or support</p>
  <p>A pitch competition is an event where entrepreneurs and startups pitch their business ideas or products to a panel of judges, investors, and sometimes a live audience, with the goal of securing funding or other forms of support for their venture. Beyond funding, winners gain valuable exposure and networking opportunities that can help grow their business.</p>
</details>
