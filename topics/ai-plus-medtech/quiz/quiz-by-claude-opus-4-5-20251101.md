# AI + medtech

Question: Which AI + medtech application involves analyzing patient data, genomic information, and clinical variables to identify patient-specific characteristics and predict treatment responses?

- [ ] Remote Patient Monitoring
- [ ] Precision Medicine
- [ ] Hospital Logistics
- [ ] Electronic Health Records Management

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>Precision Medicine</p>
  <p>Precision Medicine uses AI to analyze patient data, genomic information, and clinical variables to identify patient-specific characteristics, predict treatment responses, and support personalized treatment plans. This represents one of the most transformative applications of AI in healthcare, enabling treatments tailored to individual patients rather than one-size-fits-all approaches.</p>
</details>
