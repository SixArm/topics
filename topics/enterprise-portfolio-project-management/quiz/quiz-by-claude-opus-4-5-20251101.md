# Enterprise Portfolio Project Management (EPPM)

Question: What is the primary purpose of Enterprise Portfolio Project Management (EPPM)?

- [ ] To track individual employee performance metrics across departments
- [ ] To align projects with organizational goals and strategically allocate resources
- [ ] To eliminate all project risks before implementation begins
- [ ] To reduce the total number of projects an organization undertakes

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>To align projects with organizational goals and strategically allocate resources</p>
  <p>EPPM is a methodology that focuses on aligning projects with the organization's goals and objectives, and ensuring that resources are allocated appropriately to achieve those goals. This strategic approach helps organizations make better use of their resources and achieve their strategic objectives more efficiently, particularly for large organizations with complex project portfolios.</p>
</details>
