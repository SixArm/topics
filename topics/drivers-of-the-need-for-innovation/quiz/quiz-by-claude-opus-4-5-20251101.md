# Drivers of the need for innovation

Question: According to Sheth and Ram, what are the four key drivers that create urgency for organizations to embrace innovation?

- [ ] Market saturation, regulatory compliance, cost reduction, and employee turnover
- [ ] Brand awareness, shareholder demands, supply chain optimization, and risk management
- [ ] Intensified competition, technological advances, changing business environments, and changing customers and needs
- [ ] Profit margins, organizational restructuring, talent acquisition, and infrastructure modernization

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>Intensified competition, technological advances, changing business environments, and changing customers and needs</p>
  <p>Sheth and Ram identified these four drivers as the forces that collectively create urgency for organizations to innovate. Intensified competition pressures companies to differentiate themselves; technological advances (AI, machine learning, automation) create new opportunities; changing business environments require adaptation to globalization, regulations, and economic shifts; and changing customer needs demand personalized experiences and customer-centric solutions.</p>
</details>
