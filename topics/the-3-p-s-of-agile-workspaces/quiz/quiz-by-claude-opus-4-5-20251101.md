# The 3 P's of Agile Workspaces

Question: What are the 3 P's that form the foundation of effective agile workspace management?

- [ ] Planning, Programming, Production
- [ ] People, Processes, Places
- [ ] Productivity, Performance, Profitability
- [ ] Projects, Priorities, Practices

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>People, Processes, Places</p>
  <p>The 3 P's of agile workspaces are People (team dynamics, skill development, collaborative relationships), Processes (iterative development, feedback loops, continuous improvement), and Places (physical and virtual environments that support agile work patterns). This framework emphasizes how these three elements are interconnected in creating productive, adaptive work environments for modern software engineering teams.</p>
</details>
