# Product design

Question: What is the primary purpose of creating personas, user stories, and use cases during the product design process?

- [ ] To generate marketing materials for the product launch
- [ ] To inform the design process based on user needs, pain points, and behaviors
- [ ] To create high-fidelity prototypes for stakeholder approval
- [ ] To document technical specifications for the engineering team

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>To inform the design process based on user needs, pain points, and behaviors</p>
  <p>The product design process begins with understanding user needs, pain points, and behaviors through research and analysis. This information is then used to create personas, user stories, and use cases that serve as foundational inputs to guide design decisions throughout the process.</p>
</details>
