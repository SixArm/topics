# Chief Operating Officer (COO)

Question: What is the primary role of a Chief Operating Officer (COO) in a technology company?

- [ ] Leading the company's marketing campaigns and brand strategy
- [ ] Managing investor relations and fundraising activities
- [ ] Overseeing day-to-day operations and ensuring the company runs efficiently while meeting strategic goals
- [ ] Designing and architecting the company's technical infrastructure

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>Overseeing day-to-day operations and ensuring the company runs efficiently while meeting strategic goals</p>
  <p>The COO is responsible for ensuring that the company's daily operations run smoothly and efficiently. They work closely with the CEO to implement strategic plans, manage company resources (people, practices, and property), develop operational policies and procedures, and measure performance using KPIs. While other executives handle marketing, investor relations, or technical architecture, the COO's core focus is operational excellence and execution of the company's strategy.</p>
</details>
