# Digital transformation

Question: What is the primary goal of digital transformation in an organization?

- [ ] Replacing all employees with automated systems
- [ ] Using digital technologies to fundamentally improve operations, processes, products, and services
- [ ] Installing the latest software regardless of business needs
- [ ] Reducing IT department budgets through technology consolidation

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>Using digital technologies to fundamentally improve operations, processes, products, and services</p>
  <p>Digital transformation is the process of leveraging technology to drive significant changes that enhance efficiency, innovation, employee capabilities, customer success, and overall business performance. It encompasses key aspects including innovation, technology adoption, user experience, data-driven insights, agile culture, process optimization, and organizational change management.</p>
</details>
