# Agile Principle 4: Work Together

Question: According to Agile Principle 4, how often should business people and developers collaborate during a project?

- [ ] Weekly during sprint planning meetings
- [ ] At the beginning and end of each project phase
- [ ] Daily throughout the project
- [ ] Only when requirements change or issues arise

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>Daily throughout the project</p>
  <p>Agile Principle 4 explicitly states that "Business people and developers must work together daily throughout the project." This frequent collaboration ensures rapid feedback loops, prevents misunderstandings, enables early course-correction, and promotes shared ownership between business stakeholders and technical teams.</p>
</details>
