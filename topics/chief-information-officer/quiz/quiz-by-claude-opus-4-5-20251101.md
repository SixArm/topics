# Chief Information Officer (CIO)

Question: What is the primary responsibility of a Chief Information Officer (CIO) in an organization?

- [ ] Managing the organization's financial accounting and tax compliance
- [ ] Overseeing human resources recruitment and employee relations
- [ ] Managing the IT strategy, policies, and operations to support business goals
- [ ] Leading the sales team and developing customer acquisition strategies

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>Managing the IT strategy, policies, and operations to support business goals</p>
  <p>The CIO is a senior executive accountable for the overall use of information technology and digital assets in support of the organization's business goals and objectives. This includes developing IT strategy, driving innovation, ensuring security, managing the IT budget, leading the IT team, overseeing data analytics, and creating alignment between IT initiatives and business strategy.</p>
</details>
