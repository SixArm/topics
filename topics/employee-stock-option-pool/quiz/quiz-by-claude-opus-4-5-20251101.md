# Employee stock option pool

Question: What is the primary purpose of creating an employee stock option pool?

- [ ] To reduce the company's tax liability by distributing shares
- [ ] To give founders a mechanism to sell their shares early
- [ ] To attract and retain talented personnel by offering equity ownership
- [ ] To satisfy regulatory requirements for publicly traded companies

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>To attract and retain talented personnel by offering equity ownership</p>
  <p>An employee stock option pool is a reserve of company shares set aside specifically to grant as stock options to employees, contractors, or advisors. The core purpose is to attract and retain talent by offering them potential equity ownership in the company, which could become more valuable as the company grows. This aligns employee interests with company success.</p>
</details>
