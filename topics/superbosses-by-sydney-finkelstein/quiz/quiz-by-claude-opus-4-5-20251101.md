# Superbosses by Sydney Finkelstein

Question: According to Sydney Finkelstein's research, what are the three key qualities that all superbosses share?

- [ ] Technical expertise, micromanagement skills, and risk aversion
- [ ] Extreme confidence, a willingness to break rules, and a deep commitment to finding and cultivating talent
- [ ] Cost-cutting focus, strict hierarchy enforcement, and competitive secrecy
- [ ] Formal mentorship programs, standardized processes, and long-term employee retention

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>Extreme confidence, a willingness to break rules, and a deep commitment to finding and cultivating talent</p>
  <p>Finkelstein's research, based on interviews with more than 200 people including leaders like Ralph Lauren, Larry Ellison, and Bill Walsh, identified these three qualities as the common traits among exceptional leaders who consistently develop talented individuals into successful professionals.</p>
</details>
