# University partnership

Question: What is a primary benefit of university partnerships for technology companies seeking innovation?

- [ ] Reduced legal liability through academic oversight
- [ ] Exclusive ownership of all university intellectual property
- [ ] Combining academic knowledge with practical industry insights through collaborative research
- [ ] Guaranteed government funding for commercial products

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>Combining academic knowledge with practical industry insights through collaborative research</p>
  <p>University partnerships foster innovation by combining academic research expertise with practical industry knowledge. Collaborative research projects allow organizations to leverage university resources, faculty expertise, and cutting-edge research while contributing real-world applications and commercial perspectives, leading to breakthroughs that neither party could achieve alone.</p>
</details>
