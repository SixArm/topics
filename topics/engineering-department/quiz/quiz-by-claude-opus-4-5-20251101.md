# Engineering department

Question: What are the primary functions of an engineering department in a company?

- [ ] Marketing, Sales, Human Resources, and Finance
- [ ] R&D, Design, Testing/QA, Manufacturing, Maintenance/Support, and Innovation
- [ ] Accounting, Legal, Procurement, and Public Relations
- [ ] Customer Service, Billing, Compliance, and Auditing

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>R&D, Design, Testing/QA, Manufacturing, Maintenance/Support, and Innovation</p>
  <p>The engineering department encompasses six key functions: Research and Development (creating and improving products), Design (creating detailed specifications), Testing and Quality Assurance (ensuring products meet requirements), Manufacturing (production), Maintenance and Support (post-delivery service), and Innovation and Continuous Improvement (driving ongoing advancement). These functions work together to deliver and maintain a company's technical products, services, and systems.</p>
</details>
