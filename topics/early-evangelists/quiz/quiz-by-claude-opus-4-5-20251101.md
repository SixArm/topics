# Early evangelists

Question: What is the primary role of early evangelists in the context of startups?

- [ ] To provide financial investment in exchange for equity
- [ ] To generate buzz, validate the product, and promote it to potential customers
- [ ] To handle technical development and product engineering
- [ ] To manage regulatory compliance and legal documentation

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>To generate buzz, validate the product, and promote it to potential customers</p>
  <p>Early evangelists are enthusiastic early adopters who actively promote a product to others, provide valuable feedback for product refinement, and help demonstrate the product's value to potential customers. They serve as "superfans" who create excitement and momentum for startups in the market.</p>
</details>
