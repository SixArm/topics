# BANT-qualified sales leads

Question: What does the BANT framework assess when qualifying sales leads?

- [ ] Brand awareness, Advertising reach, Net promoter score, and Target demographics
- [ ] Business size, Annual revenue, Number of employees, and Territory location
- [ ] Budget, Authority, Need, and Timeline
- [ ] Behavior patterns, Account history, Navigation data, and Transaction frequency

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>Budget, Authority, Need, and Timeline</p>
  <p>BANT is a sales qualification framework where Budget assesses financial capacity, Authority identifies decision-makers, Need evaluates genuine requirements for the product or service, and Timeline determines the expected timeframe for purchase. This framework helps sales teams prioritize leads most likely to convert into successful sales.</p>
</details>
