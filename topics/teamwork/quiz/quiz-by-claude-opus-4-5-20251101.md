# Teamwork

Question: What is the phenomenon where a team's combined efforts produce results greater than the sum of individual contributions?

- [ ] Division of labor
- [ ] Synergy
- [ ] Collaboration
- [ ] Mutual support

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>Synergy</p>
  <p>Synergy is the key teamwork concept where combined efforts produce results greater than the sum of individual contributions. This occurs when team members effectively leverage their diverse skills, perspectives, and expertise together, creating outcomes that no individual could achieve alone. For technology professionals, understanding synergy helps in building high-performing teams where cross-functional collaboration amplifies innovation and problem-solving capabilities.</p>
</details>
