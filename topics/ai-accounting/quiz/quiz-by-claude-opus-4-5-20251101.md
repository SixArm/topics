# AI Accounting

Question: What is a primary benefit of using AI in auditing processes?

- [ ] Replacing all human auditors entirely
- [ ] Automating control testing and detecting anomalies to flag potential fraud
- [ ] Eliminating the need for regulatory compliance
- [ ] Reducing the number of financial statements required

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>Automating control testing and detecting anomalies to flag potential fraud</p>
  <p>AI assists in audit processes by automating the testing of controls and detecting anomalies, which helps flag potential issues, risks, or fraud. This enhances compliance with internal controls and regulatory requirements while improving audit efficiency and accuracy.</p>
</details>
