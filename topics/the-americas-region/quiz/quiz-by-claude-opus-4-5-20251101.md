# The Americas Region

Question: What is the approximate combined population of the Americas business region, representing a key market opportunity for technology companies?

- [ ] Approximately 500 million people
- [ ] More than one billion people
- [ ] Approximately 2.5 billion people
- [ ] Approximately 750 million people

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>More than one billion people</p>
  <p>The Americas region has a combined population of more than one billion people across North America, Central America, South America, and the Caribbean. This large consumer base, combined with a growing middle class, creates significant opportunities for technology professionals and businesses across sectors including healthcare technology, retail tech, and consumer applications.</p>
</details>
