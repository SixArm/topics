# Marketing

Question: What are the "4 Ps" that form the foundation of the marketing mix strategy?

- [ ] People, Process, Physical evidence, Performance
- [ ] Profit, Planning, Positioning, Promotion
- [ ] Product, Price, Place, Promotion
- [ ] Purpose, Profit, People, Planet

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>Product, Price, Place, Promotion</p>
  <p>The 4 Ps are the fundamental components of marketing strategy. Product refers to what you're selling, Price is the cost to customers, Place covers distribution channels to reach your target market, and Promotion encompasses how you communicate value to customers through advertising, PR, and other tactics.</p>
</details>
