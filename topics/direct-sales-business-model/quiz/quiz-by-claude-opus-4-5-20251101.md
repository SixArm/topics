# Direct sales business model

Question: What is the defining characteristic of a direct sales business model?

- [ ] Products are sold through retail stores and distributors
- [ ] The company sells products or services directly to customers without involving a middleman or retailer
- [ ] Sales are conducted exclusively through online marketplaces
- [ ] Revenue is generated primarily through advertising partnerships

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>The company sells products or services directly to customers without involving a middleman or retailer</p>
  <p>The direct sales business model eliminates intermediaries such as retailers or distributors, allowing the company's sales team to build direct relationships with customers. This approach is particularly effective for complex or high-value products that require personalized explanation and a more in-depth sales process.</p>
</details>
