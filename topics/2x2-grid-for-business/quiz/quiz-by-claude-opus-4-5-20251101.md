# 2x2 grid for business

Question: In a 2x2 business grid assessing market attractiveness and competitive position, what is the recommended strategy for a market with LOW attractiveness where your business has a STRONG competitive position?

- [ ] Divest and retire from the market
- [ ] Invest and grow aggressively
- [ ] Build position or exit the market
- [ ] Protect market share and harvest profits

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>Build position or exit the market</p>
  <p>When a market has low attractiveness but the business holds a strong competitive position, the strategy is to maintain the competitive advantage while exploring opportunities to transition or pivot into more attractive markets. This "Build/Exit" approach acknowledges that while the market itself may not be growing, the strong position can still be leveragedâ€”either to build toward adjacent opportunities or to plan a strategic exit while still in a position of strength.</p>
</details>
