# Vision board

Question: What is the primary purpose of a vision board in professional development?

- [ ] To create a decorative display for your workspace
- [ ] To document completed projects and past achievements
- [ ] To clarify and visualize goals, dreams, and aspirations you want to achieve
- [ ] To organize daily task lists and meeting schedules

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>To clarify and visualize goals, dreams, and aspirations you want to achieve</p>
  <p>A vision board is fundamentally a tool for clarifying and visualizing what you want to achieve across different areas of life, including career and personal growth. It combines visual elements like images, quotes, and symbols to represent your goals, and serves as both inspiration and motivation to take practical steps toward bringing your vision to life.</p>
</details>
