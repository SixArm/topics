# Onboarding and offboarding

Question: What is the primary goal of the onboarding process for new employees?

- [ ] To collect company property and terminate system access
- [ ] To conduct exit interviews and gather feedback
- [ ] To administer final payroll and benefits documentation
- [ ] To familiarize the employee with their role, responsibilities, company policies, and organizational values

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>To familiarize the employee with their role, responsibilities, company policies, and organizational values</p>
  <p>Onboarding aims to integrate new employees into the company and its culture by helping them understand their role, responsibilities, company policies, procedures, and organizational values. It also helps employees build relationships with colleagues and understand the company's expectations during their first weeks or months of employment.</p>
</details>
