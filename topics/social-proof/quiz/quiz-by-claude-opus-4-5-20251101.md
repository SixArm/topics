# Social proof

Question: What is social proof in the context of technology products and marketing?

- [ ] A mathematical proof technique used to verify software correctness
- [ ] A psychological phenomenon where people make decisions based on the actions and opinions of others
- [ ] A security protocol for authenticating users on social media platforms
- [ ] A formal documentation process required before launching social features

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>A psychological phenomenon where people make decisions based on the actions and opinions of others</p>
  <p>Social proof leverages the tendency for people to follow and trust the actions of others, especially those in their social group or perceived authorities. In technology, this manifests as customer reviews, testimonials, social media engagement metrics, and expert endorsements that influence user adoption and purchasing decisions.</p>
</details>
