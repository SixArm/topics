# Company Leadership Roles

Question: Which executive role is primarily responsible for overseeing day-to-day operations and ensuring that business processes are efficient and effective?

- [ ] Chief Financial Officer (CFO)
- [ ] Chief Operating Officer (COO)
- [ ] Chief Marketing Officer (CMO)
- [ ] Chief Executive Officer (CEO)

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>Chief Operating Officer (COO)</p>
  <p>The COO is responsible for overseeing the day-to-day operations of the company, ensuring that business processes are efficient and effective, and managing the company's resources. While the CEO sets overall strategy and vision, and the CFO manages finances, the COO focuses on operational execution.</p>
</details>
