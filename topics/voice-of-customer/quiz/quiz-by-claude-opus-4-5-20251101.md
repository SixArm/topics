# Voice of Customer (VoC)

Question: What is the primary purpose of Voice of Customer (VoC) in an organization?

- [ ] To increase marketing spend on customer acquisition campaigns
- [ ] To capture customer feedback and make informed decisions about meeting their needs
- [ ] To reduce the number of customer support interactions
- [ ] To automate product development without human input

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>To capture customer feedback and make informed decisions about meeting their needs</p>
  <p>Voice of Customer (VoC) is fundamentally about capturing customer feedback, opinions, preferences, and needs through various channels such as surveys, focus groups, customer support interactions, and social media. By analyzing this feedback, organizations can make data-driven improvements to their products and services, resulting in improved customer satisfaction, increased loyalty, enhanced product development, and better overall decision-making.</p>
</details>
