# Conversion Rate Optimization

Question: What is the primary goal of Conversion Rate Optimization (CRO)?

- [ ] To reduce the overall traffic to a website
- [ ] To increase the percentage of visitors who complete a desired action
- [ ] To maximize the number of pages a visitor views per session
- [ ] To decrease the time visitors spend on a website

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>To increase the percentage of visitors who complete a desired action</p>
  <p>CRO focuses on improving the conversion rate, which measures the percentage of website visitors who take a desired action such as making a purchase, filling out a form, or generating a lead. By analyzing user behavior, developing hypotheses, and testing variations through A/B or multivariate testing, businesses optimize their websites to convert more visitors into customers or leads, ultimately driving increased revenue and achieving key performance indicators.</p>
</details>
