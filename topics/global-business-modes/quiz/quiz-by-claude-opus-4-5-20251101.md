# Global business modes

Question: Which global business mode provides a company with the most direct control over its foreign operations while also enabling access to local markets and resources?

- [ ] Importing and Exporting
- [ ] Licensing and Franchising
- [ ] Foreign Direct Investment (FDI)
- [ ] Contract Manufacturing and Outsourcing

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>Foreign Direct Investment (FDI)</p>
  <p>FDI involves investing in establishing or acquiring business operations in another country, such as setting up manufacturing plants, opening subsidiaries, or acquiring existing businesses. Unlike other modes like licensing or outsourcing where operations are handled by third parties, FDI gives companies direct ownership and control over their foreign operations while providing access to local markets and resources.</p>
</details>
