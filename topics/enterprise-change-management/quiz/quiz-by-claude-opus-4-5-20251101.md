# Enterprise Change Management

Question: What are the three stages of Enterprise Change Management (ECM)?

- [ ] Assessment, Deployment, Monitoring
- [ ] Planning, Implementation, Evaluation
- [ ] Design, Testing, Rollout
- [ ] Analysis, Execution, Review

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>Planning, Implementation, Evaluation</p>
  <p>ECM follows three distinct stages: Planning (defining scope, identifying stakeholders, assessing risks, and developing a change management plan), Implementation (executing the plan through training, communication, and stakeholder engagement), and Evaluation (assessing effectiveness and making adjustments to ensure sustainability).</p>
</details>
