# Trend analysis

Question: What is the primary objective of trend analysis?

- [ ] To eliminate all fluctuations and anomalies from historical data
- [ ] To compare performance metrics between competing organizations
- [ ] To identify patterns in data over time and predict future outcomes
- [ ] To standardize data collection methods across different departments

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>To identify patterns in data over time and predict future outcomes</p>
  <p>Trend analysis is a statistical method that examines data over time to identify patterns such as upward or downward trends, seasonality, or cyclicality. The core purpose is to help decision-makers understand how factors are changing over time and use historical patterns to make forecasts and informed decisions about future outcomes.</p>
</details>
