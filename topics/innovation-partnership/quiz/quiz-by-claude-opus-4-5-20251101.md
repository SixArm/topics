# Innovation partnership

Question: What is the primary purpose of an innovation partnership between organizations?

- [ ] To eliminate competition by acquiring smaller companies in the industry
- [ ] To foster collaborative innovation and drive advancements through shared resources, knowledge exchange, and joint R&D efforts
- [ ] To outsource all research and development activities to reduce internal costs
- [ ] To establish exclusive intellectual property ownership for a single partner

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>To foster collaborative innovation and drive advancements through shared resources, knowledge exchange, and joint R&D efforts</p>
  <p>Innovation partnerships are collaborative relationships where entities pool resources (financial, facilities, IP, human capital), exchange knowledge and expertise, conduct joint R&D, and work toward shared objectives. The goal is mutual benefit through combined capabilities, not elimination of competition, outsourcing, or exclusive ownership by one party.</p>
</details>
