# AI + adtech

Question: Which AI-powered adtech capability analyzes available ad inventory and user data to evaluate placement opportunities, bid on impressions, and optimize delivery for reach and efficiency?

- [ ] Attribution modeling
- [ ] Audience segmentation
- [ ] Real-time bidding
- [ ] Creative generation

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>Real-time bidding</p>
  <p>Real-time bidding (RTB) is the AI capability that analyzes available ad inventory and user data to evaluate ad placement opportunities, bid on available impressions, and optimize ad delivery for reach and efficiency. This programmatic approach enables advertisers to make automated, data-driven decisions in milliseconds during ad auctions.</p>
</details>
