# If you want to go fast, go alone; if you want to go far, go together

Question: According to this proverb about speed versus distance, what is the primary benefit of collaboration and teamwork?

- [ ] It allows for immediate results and quick decision-making
- [ ] It enables full control over project direction and outcomes
- [ ] It achieves more significant and enduring outcomes through diverse perspectives and shared effort
- [ ] It eliminates the need for coordination and consensus

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>It achieves more significant and enduring outcomes through diverse perspectives and shared effort</p>
  <p>The proverb emphasizes that "going far together" leverages the power of collaboration, combining skills, knowledge, and resources to achieve long-term success. Teamwork fosters synergy, innovation, and the ability to tackle complex challenges through complementary strengths and mutual support.</p>
</details>
