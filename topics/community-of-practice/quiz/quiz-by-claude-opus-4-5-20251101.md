# Community of Practice

Question: What is the primary purpose of a Community of Practice (CoP)?

- [ ] To enforce standardized procedures across an organization's departments
- [ ] To create formal reporting structures between team members
- [ ] To bring together people who share a passion for a specific field to learn from each other and share best practices
- [ ] To assign mandatory training requirements to employees

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>To bring together people who share a passion for a specific field to learn from each other and share best practices</p>
  <p>A Community of Practice is an informal network that connects individuals with a common interest in a particular area. Unlike formal organizational structures, CoPs focus on voluntary knowledge sharing, collaboration, and continuous learning among members who want to deepen their expertise together.</p>
</details>
