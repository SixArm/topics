# Predictive analytics

Question: What is the primary purpose of predictive analytics?

- [ ] To store and organize large volumes of historical data in databases
- [ ] To analyze historical data and make predictions about future events or trends
- [ ] To create real-time dashboards displaying current operational metrics
- [ ] To automate the execution of business processes without human intervention

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>To analyze historical data and make predictions about future events or trends</p>
  <p>Predictive analytics uses statistical methods and machine learning models to discover patterns in historical data, then applies those patterns to forecast future outcomes. This enables organizations to make data-driven decisions, optimize operations, and anticipate trends in areas such as customer behavior, financial markets, healthcare diagnostics, and fraud detection.</p>
</details>
