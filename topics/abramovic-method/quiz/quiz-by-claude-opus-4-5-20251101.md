# Abramović Method

Question: What is the primary purpose of the Abramović Method developed by performance artist Marina Abramović?

- [ ] To create efficient workflows for agile software development teams
- [ ] To enhance awareness, presence, and focus through exercises emphasizing stillness and concentration
- [ ] To optimize system performance through durational load testing
- [ ] To establish communication protocols between distributed team members

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>To enhance awareness, presence, and focus through exercises emphasizing stillness and concentration</p>
  <p>The Abramović Method is a set of exercises and techniques designed to enhance awareness, presence, and focus. Its core principles include being fully present in the moment, cultivating inner stillness, developing heightened concentration, and practicing mindfulness and sensory awareness. For technology professionals, these principles can help combat distraction, improve deep work capabilities, and enhance focus during complex problem-solving tasks.</p>
</details>
