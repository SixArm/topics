# Corporate social responsibility (CSR)

Question: Which international framework outlines ten principles in areas such as human rights, labor rights, and environmental sustainability to guide companies in developing CSR strategies?

- [ ] ISO 9001 Quality Management System
- [ ] United Nations Global Compact
- [ ] Sarbanes-Oxley Act
- [ ] Basel III Accord

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>United Nations Global Compact</p>
  <p>The United Nations Global Compact is a key framework that provides ten principles covering human rights, labor rights, environmental sustainability, and anti-corruption to guide companies in developing and implementing corporate social responsibility strategies. ISO 26000 also provides CSR guidance, but the UN Global Compact specifically outlines the ten principles mentioned. The other options relate to quality management, financial reporting compliance, and banking regulations respectively.</p>
</details>
