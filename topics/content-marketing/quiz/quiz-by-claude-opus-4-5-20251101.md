# Content marketing

Question: What is the primary purpose of content marketing?

- [ ] To directly promote products and services through advertisements
- [ ] To create and distribute valuable content that attracts and retains a target audience
- [ ] To generate immediate sales through promotional campaigns
- [ ] To increase social media follower counts as quickly as possible

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>To create and distribute valuable content that attracts and retains a target audience</p>
  <p>Content marketing focuses on creating and distributing valuable, relevant, and consistent content to attract and retain a clearly defined audience. Rather than directly promoting products or services, content marketing aims to provide information, education, or entertainment that builds relationships with potential customers and establishes authority in the industry, ultimately driving profitable customer action.</p>
</details>
