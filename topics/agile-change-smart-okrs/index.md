# Agile Change SMART OKRs

Strengthen Customer Collaboration:

- Interview {count} customers about product needs and pain points during {timebox} and report results. Quantify by interview completion and insights documented.
- Improve customer satisfaction score by {percent} from {date} to {date}. Score by NPS surveys.
- Run showcase customer participation every {period}. Track by customer attendance logs and feedback received.
- Increase customer-facing feature releases from {count} to {count} during {duration}. Measure by release notes and feature deployment tracking.
- Present product roadmap to {count} key stakeholders on {date} with the goal of alignment. Confirm by stakeholder approval signatures and feedback forms.

Build Agile Mindset and Culture:

- Present agile transformation progress to executive leadership on {date} with the goal of securing continued support. Confirm by leadership approval and budget allocation.
- Raise employee engagement rating from {score} to {score} by end of {period}. Score by engagement survey.
- Ensure {count} managers are coming to Agile Leadership Summit on May 10. Quantify by registration confirmations and attendance.
- Improve psychological safety score by {percent} during the next {number} months. Score by quarterly team health surveys.
- Interview 25 team members about agile adoption challenges during Q1 and report results. Quantify by interview completion rate and themes identified.

Implement Technical Excellence Practices:

- Run pair programming sessions for knowledge sharing during each sprint. Track by pair programming logs and knowledge transfer assessments.
- Launch CI/CD pipeline for automated deployments on February 28. Track by pipeline usage metrics and deployment automation rate.
- Complete technical debt reduction initiative for legacy codebase by Q2 end. Track by code quality metrics and refactoring story points completed.
- Increase code review participation from 60% to 95% by June 15. Measure by pull request review statistics.
- Achieve 10 points for Definition of Done compliance by sprint 6. Score by DoD checklist adherence audits.

Scale Agile Practices Organization-Wide:

- Grow agile team adoption from 8 teams to 25 teams by December 31. Measure by team registration in agile program and practice assessments.
- Manage 5 Agile Coaches to accomplish team training rollout by Q3 end. Quantify by coaching engagement hours and teams coached.
- Evaluate 3 scaling frameworks (SAFe, LeSS, Scrum@Scale) for enterprise adoption during Q1 and choose the result. Confirm by framework assessment report and leadership decision.
Boost Agile maturity assessment score by 45% during the year. Score by bi-annual maturity model evaluations.
Launch Communities of Practice for agile practitioners on April 1. Track by CoP membership and monthly meeting attendance.

Develop Leadership Support for Agile Transformation:

- Interview 10 executives about transformation barriers during February and report results. Quantify by interview completion and action plan created.
- Present business case for agile investment to board of directors on March 20 with the goal of budget approval. Confirm by board vote and funding allocated.
- Ensure 8 senior leaders complete Agile Leadership training by May 31. Quantify by training completion certificates.
- Raise leadership agile fluency rating from {x} to {y} by {date}. Score by 360-degree leadership assessment.
- Manage 20 middle managers to accomplish team empowerment practices by August 15. Quantify by manager coaching sessions and team autonomy metrics.
