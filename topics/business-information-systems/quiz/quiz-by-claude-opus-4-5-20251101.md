# Business Information Systems (BIS)

Question: What is the primary purpose of decision support systems (DSS) and business intelligence (BI) tools within Business Information Systems?

- [ ] To manage employee payroll and benefits administration
- [ ] To facilitate video conferencing and team messaging
- [ ] To analyze data, generate reports, and provide insights for strategic, tactical, and operational decision-making
- [ ] To process online payment transactions and manage shopping carts

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>To analyze data, generate reports, and provide insights for strategic, tactical, and operational decision-making</p>
  <p>Decision support systems (DSS) and business intelligence (BI) tools are core components of Business Information Systems specifically designed to transform raw data into actionable insights. They help organizations make informed decisions at all levels—strategic (long-term planning), tactical (medium-term resource allocation), and operational (day-to-day activities)—by providing data analysis, reporting, and visualization capabilities.</p>
</details>
