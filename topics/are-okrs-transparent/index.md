# Are OKRs transparent?

Yes. All OKRs default to be transparent, meaning they are visible to everyone in
the organization. 

In practice, there might be very rare OKRs that are transparency exceptions,
such as OKRs for maximum-security projects or maximum-secrecy research.

Transparency and visibility build mutual understanding, and also can be
significant for helping for inter-team goals, team-of-teams projects,
cross-functional areas, and roll-ups.
