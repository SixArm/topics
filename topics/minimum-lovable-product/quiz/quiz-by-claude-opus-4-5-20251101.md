# Minimum Lovable Product (MLP)

Question: What is the key difference between a Minimum Lovable Product (MLP) and a Minimum Viable Product (MVP)?

- [ ] MLP requires a larger development team and budget than MVP
- [ ] MLP prioritizes the emotional and experiential aspects of a product, not just functionality
- [ ] MLP is designed only for enterprise customers while MVP targets consumers
- [ ] MLP eliminates the need for user testing during development

<details>

  <p>MLP prioritizes the emotional and experiential aspects of a product, not just functionality</p>
