# Sales

Question: What is the primary purpose of using a Customer Relationship Management (CRM) system in sales pipeline management?

- [ ] To replace the need for a marketing department
- [ ] To manage leads, track interactions, and forecast sales
- [ ] To automate cold calling and email outreach exclusively
- [ ] To eliminate the need for relationship building with customers

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>To manage leads, track interactions, and forecast sales</p>
  <p>A CRM system is used in sales pipeline management to organize the sales process from initial lead generation to deal closure. It helps sales teams manage their leads effectively, track all customer interactions throughout the sales cycle, and forecast future sales based on pipeline data. This enables better decision-making and more efficient sales operations.</p>
</details>
