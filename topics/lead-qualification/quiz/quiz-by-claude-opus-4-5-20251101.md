# Lead qualification

Question: What is the primary purpose of lead qualification in the sales process?

- [ ] To collect contact information for marketing databases
- [ ] To prioritize sales efforts on leads most likely to convert into customers
- [ ] To automate email campaigns for all potential prospects
- [ ] To eliminate the need for sales representatives

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>To prioritize sales efforts on leads most likely to convert into customers</p>
  <p>Lead qualification is the process of determining the potential value and suitability of a sales lead by evaluating their level of interest, readiness to purchase, and alignment with your ideal customer profile. This helps businesses focus resources efficiently on leads with the highest conversion potential, improving overall sales productivity and increasing the chances of closing deals.</p>
</details>
