# General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)

Question: What is the maximum penalty for non-compliance with GDPR?

- [ ] €10 million or 2% of global annual revenue
- [ ] €50 million or 10% of global annual revenue
- [ ] €20 million or 4% of global annual revenue
- [ ] €5 million or 1% of global annual revenue

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>€20 million or 4% of global annual revenue</p>
  <p>The GDPR includes strict penalties for non-compliance, with fines of up to €20 million or 4% of a company's global annual revenue, whichever is higher. This significant penalty structure underscores the importance of GDPR compliance for technology professionals working with personal data of EU individuals.</p>
</details>
