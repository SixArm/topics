# Early adopters

Question: What is the primary role of early adopters in the technology adoption lifecycle?

- [ ] They wait for products to be perfected before purchasing to avoid any defects
- [ ] They provide feedback and create buzz that helps shape product development and drive further adoption
- [ ] They only purchase products that have been widely reviewed and validated by mainstream consumers
- [ ] They focus exclusively on finding the lowest price for established products

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>They provide feedback and create buzz that helps shape product development and drive further adoption</p>
  <p>Early adopters are the first to experience new products and their feedback is instrumental in shaping future development. They also serve as opinion leaders and influencers who create excitement around products, helping to drive adoption by later consumer groups in the adoption curve.</p>
</details>
