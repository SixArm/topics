# Stealth mode

Question: What is the primary purpose of a startup operating in stealth mode?

- [ ] To generate maximum media coverage before product launch
- [ ] To attract as many investors as possible during early development
- [ ] To keep activities and plans secret from competitors until ready to launch
- [ ] To publicly test product ideas with potential customers

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>To keep activities and plans secret from competitors until ready to launch</p>
  <p>Stealth mode refers to a period when a startup keeps its activities, plans, and product development under wraps and out of the public eye. The goal is to protect intellectual property, avoid copycats, focus on product development without public scrutiny, and build anticipation for the eventual launch.</p>
</details>
