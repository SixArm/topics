# Anticipatory design

Question: What is the primary goal of anticipatory design in UX?

- [ ] To create visually appealing interfaces with consistent branding
- [ ] To predict and fulfill user needs before they explicitly express them
- [ ] To reduce development costs by using standardized templates
- [ ] To ensure compliance with accessibility regulations

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>To predict and fulfill user needs before they explicitly express them</p>
  <p>Anticipatory design focuses on using data, context, and intelligent algorithms to proactively provide users with relevant information, options, or interactions before they ask for them. This simplifies decision-making and enhances the overall user experience through predictive analytics, context awareness, automation, and adaptive interfaces.</p>
</details>
