# Legal department

Question: What is the primary role of a technology company's legal department regarding intellectual property?

- [ ] Filing lawsuits against competitors to gain market share
- [ ] Managing and protecting the company's trademarks, patents, copyrights, and trade secrets from infringement
- [ ] Developing new software products for the company
- [ ] Hiring and terminating employees in technical roles

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>Managing and protecting the company's trademarks, patents, copyrights, and trade secrets from infringement</p>
  <p>The legal department's intellectual property management function involves managing all forms of IP (trademarks, patents, copyrights, trade secrets) and ensuring they are protected from infringement. This is critical for technology companies where IP often represents significant competitive advantage and business value.</p>
</details>
