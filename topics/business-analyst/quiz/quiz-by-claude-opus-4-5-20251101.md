# Business Analyst

Question: What is the primary function of a business analyst in an organization?

- [ ] Writing production code and deploying software applications to servers
- [ ] Managing company finances and preparing annual budget reports
- [ ] Bridging the gap between business objectives and technology solutions
- [ ] Conducting employee performance reviews and hiring decisions

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>Bridging the gap between business objectives and technology solutions</p>
  <p>A business analyst serves as the critical link between business stakeholders and technical teams. They gather requirements, analyze business processes, and ensure that technology solutions align with organizational goals. This involves facilitating communication, documenting specifications, and validating that delivered solutions meet defined business needs.</p>
</details>
