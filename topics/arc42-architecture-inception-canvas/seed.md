The Architecture Inception Canvas (AIC) is a lightweight, one-page tool designed to kickstart software projects by capturing essential architectural drivers and goals. Based on the [Business Model Canvas](https://canvas.arc42.org/) concept, it focuses on "greenfield" projects (new development) to ensure the entire product team understands the "why" and "what" before diving into deep technical design. ## Key Sections of the Canvas
The canvas is structured into several collaborative areas that map directly to the [arc42 template](https://arc42.org/canvas): [6, 7] 

* Business Case: A brief description of the economic drivers behind the system.
* Quality Goals: The top 3–5 most important quality attributes (e.g., scalability, security) that shape the architecture.
* Functional Overview: The most critical high-level requirements.
* Business Context: A "black box" view of the system, identifying external users and neighbouring systems.
* Constraints: Both Organisational (budget, team) and Technical (platforms, legacy systems) limitations.
* Risks & Challenges: Identified technical risks, known hurdles, or assumptions made early on.
* Architectural Hypotheses: Initial high-level design ideas and significant, potentially expensive decisions. [4, 5, 7, 8, 9, 10, 11] 

## Why Use It?

* Alignment: It bridges the gap between stakeholders and developers, ensuring everyone agrees on the system's "playground".
* Efficiency: It streamlines the initial documentation phase, replacing long workshops with a single visual framework.
* Iterative Nature: The canvas includes fields for "Iteration" and "Date," encouraging teams to update it as the project evolves.
* Transition to arc42: Once the canvas is stable, its contents form the foundation for a full [arc42 document](https://canvas.arc42.org/downloads/architecture-inception-canvas-hints.pdf). [6, 9, 10, 12, 13] 

You can download official templates in formats like [PDF](https://canvas.arc42.org/downloads/architecture-inception-canvas.pdf), [Miro](https://miro.com/templates/architecture-inception-canvas/), and [PowerPoint](https://canvas.arc42.org/faq) from the [arc42 site](https://canvas.arc42.org/downloads).
