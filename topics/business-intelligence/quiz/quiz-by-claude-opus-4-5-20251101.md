# Business Intelligence

Question: What is the primary purpose of business intelligence (BI)?

- [ ] To replace human decision-makers with automated systems
- [ ] To collect, analyze, and present data for better decision-making
- [ ] To store large amounts of data in cloud databases
- [ ] To create visually appealing marketing materials

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>To collect, analyze, and present data for better decision-making</p>
  <p>Business intelligence refers to the processes, tools, and technologies that businesses use to collect, analyze, and present data for better decision-making. BI enables businesses to make data-driven decisions rather than relying on intuition or guesswork, helping reduce risk, optimize operations, and identify new opportunities for growth.</p>
</details>
