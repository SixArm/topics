# The Law of Supply and Demand

Question: What happens at the intersection of the supply and demand curves in a market?

- [ ] Suppliers maximize their production capacity regardless of buyer interest
- [ ] The market experiences a shortage that drives innovation
- [ ] The equilibrium price and quantity are determined, where quantity supplied equals quantity demanded
- [ ] Prices become fixed and no longer respond to market changes

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>The equilibrium price and quantity are determined, where quantity supplied equals quantity demanded</p>
