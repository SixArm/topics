# Partnership quotations

Question: According to Warren Buffett, what is partnership fundamentally about?

- [ ] A legal contract that protects both parties equally in business dealings
- [ ] A defensive arrangement to prevent worst-case scenarios with business partners
- [ ] An emotional alliance between two people committed to each other's success
- [ ] A financial arrangement where profits are split based on contribution levels

  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>An emotional alliance between two people committed to each other's success</p>
  <p>Warren Buffett's quotation emphasizes that partnership transcends legal agreements. He states: "Partnership is not a legal contract between two equal individuals.
