# Chief Financial Officer (CFO)

Question: What is the primary role of a Chief Financial Officer (CFO) in an organization?

- [ ] Managing the company's marketing campaigns and brand strategy
- [ ] Overseeing the recruitment and development of all employees
- [ ] Managing the financial activities and ensuring the company's financial health
- [ ] Leading product development and engineering teams

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>Managing the financial activities and ensuring the company's financial health</p>
  <p>The CFO is a top-level executive responsible for managing all financial activities of the organization, including financial planning and analysis, accounting, budgeting, forecasting, reporting, risk management, and fundraising to ensure the company's financial health and success.</p>
</details>
