# Agile ways of working (WOW)

Agile ways of working (Agile WoW) represent a shift from rigid, plan-driven processes to a flexible mindset focused on adaptability, collaboration, and continuous value delivery.

Originally born in software development, these methods are now widely applied across non-technical fields like HR, marketing, and finance to handle complexity and rapid market changes.

## Core Components of Agile Working

Teams strive to "be" Agile by embracing four core values:

- Individuals & Interactions over processes and tools.
- Working Solutions (or products) over comprehensive documentation.
- Customer Collaboration over rigid contract negotiation.
- Responding to Change over strictly following a pre-set plan.

## Benefits for Organizations

Adopting these ways of working can lead to significant operational gains:

- Increased Speed: Iterative work allows for faster releases and quicker response times to market shifts.
- Higher Quality: Constant feedback and testing throughout the process reduce the risk of building something that doesn't meet user needs.
- Better Morale: Empowering teams to self-organise often leads to higher employee engagement and lower turnover.
