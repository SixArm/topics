# Agile + manufacturing sector

Question: Which manufacturing company demonstrates the principle of continuous delivery by deploying software updates directly to products already in customer hands?

- [ ] Boeing, through their flight control software development process
- [ ] Siemens, through their manufacturing execution systems
- [ ] Tesla, through their over-the-air vehicle software updates
- [ ] General Electric, through their Predix platform development

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>Tesla, through their over-the-air vehicle software updates</p>
  <p>Tesla revolutionized automotive manufacturing by applying agile principles that include continuous delivery in practice. Their over-the-air software updates allow rapid deployment of new features and bug fixes to vehicles already owned by customers, demonstrating how agile's continuous delivery principle can extend beyond development environments into actual products in the field.</p>
</details>
