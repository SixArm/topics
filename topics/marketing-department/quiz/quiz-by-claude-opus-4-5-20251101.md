# Marketing department

Question: What is the primary goal of a marketing department in an organization?

- [ ] To manage the company's financial reporting and budgeting processes
- [ ] To develop and maintain the company's technology infrastructure
- [ ] To identify, attract, and retain customers
- [ ] To handle employee recruitment and performance management

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>To identify, attract, and retain customers</p>
  <p>The marketing department focuses on understanding and satisfying customer needs through the creation, promotion, and delivery of products and services. Its primary goal is to identify potential customers, attract them through advertising and promotional campaigns, and retain them through brand management and ongoing engagement strategies.</p>
</details>
