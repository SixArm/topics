# Fintech (financial technology)

Question: What is the primary purpose of fintech?

- [ ] To replace all traditional banks with cryptocurrency exchanges
- [ ] To use technology to provide and improve financial services
- [ ] To eliminate the need for government regulation of financial markets
- [ ] To create proprietary payment systems exclusive to large corporations

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>To use technology to provide and improve financial services</p>
  <p>Fintech (financial technology) refers to the use of innovative technologies to create new financial products and services, as well as to improve existing ones. The goal is to make financial services more accessible, affordable, and convenient to a wider range of consumers through technologies like mobile payments, peer-to-peer lending, robo-advisory services, and online banking.</p>
</details>
