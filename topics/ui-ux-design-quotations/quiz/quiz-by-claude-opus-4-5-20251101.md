# UI/UX Design Quotations

Question: Which quote best captures the holistic concept that design encompasses both aesthetics and functionality?

- [ ] "Design adds value faster than it adds costs." - Joel Spolsky
- [ ] "Simplicity is the ultimate sophistication." - Leonardo da Vinci
- [ ] "Design is not just what it looks like and feels like. Design is how it works." - Steve Jobs
- [ ] "The details are not the details. They make the design." - Charles Eames

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>"Design is not just what it looks like and feels like. Design is how it works." - Steve Jobs</p>
  <p>This quote from Steve Jobs is foundational to UI/UX design because it emphasizes that good design goes beyond visual appeal. It reminds technology professionals that true design excellence requires the product to function well for users, not just look attractive. This principle is essential for creating products that deliver genuine value and positive user experiences.</p>
</details>
