# Cold start strategy

Question: What is the primary purpose of a cold start strategy for startups?

- [ ] To maximize profit margins during the first year of operation
- [ ] To secure venture capital funding before product development begins
- [ ] To gain initial traction and overcome barriers when launching in a new market
- [ ] To establish a permanent competitive advantage over established companies

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>To gain initial traction and overcome barriers when launching in a new market</p>
  <p>A cold start strategy is how startups plan to enter a market, gaining initial traction, and overcoming barriers to launching an offering. This involves tactics such as targeting early adopters, building an MVP, leveraging partnerships, and implementing referral mechanisms to build momentum from zero.</p>
</details>
