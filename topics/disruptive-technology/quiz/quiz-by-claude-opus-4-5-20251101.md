# Disruptive technology

Question: What is a key characteristic that distinguishes disruptive technologies from incremental innovations?

- [ ] They always offer superior performance from the moment they are introduced
- [ ] They fundamentally alter existing markets or business models, often displacing established companies
- [ ] They require approval from industry regulators before market entry
- [ ] They are exclusively developed by large established corporations

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>They fundamentally alter existing markets or business models, often displacing established companies</p>
  <p>Disruptive technology refers to an innovation that significantly alters or disrupts existing markets, industries, or business models. Unlike incremental improvements, disruptive technologies introduce substantial shifts in how things are doneâ€”such as personal computers displacing mainframes, digital photography displacing film, or streaming services displacing video rentals. Notably, disruptive technologies may initially offer lower performance compared to established solutions, but they improve rapidly over time to eventually exceed them.</p>
