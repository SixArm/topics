# ADKAR change management model

Question: What does ADKAR stand for in the Prosci change management model?

- [ ] Analysis, Development, Knowledge, Action, Review
- [ ] Awareness, Desire, Knowledge, Ability, Reinforcement
- [ ] Assessment, Design, Knowledge, Application, Results
- [ ] Awareness, Direction, Knowledge, Accountability, Recognition

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>Awareness, Desire, Knowledge, Ability, Reinforcement</p>
  <p>ADKAR is a Prosci change management model where each letter represents a sequential step: Awareness (understanding why change is needed), Desire (wanting to participate), Knowledge (learning how to change), Ability (having resources to implement change), and Reinforcement (sustaining the change in organizational culture).</p>
</details>
