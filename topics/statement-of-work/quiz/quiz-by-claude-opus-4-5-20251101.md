# Statement of Work (SOW)

Question: What is the primary purpose of a Statement of Work (SOW) in a project or service contract?

- [ ] To serve as a legal contract that can be used in court proceedings
- [ ] To outline the scope of work and establish clear expectations between client and service provider
- [ ] To replace the need for a project manager during execution
- [ ] To provide marketing materials for the project stakeholders

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>To outline the scope of work and establish clear expectations between client and service provider</p>
  <p>A Statement of Work (SOW) is a document that defines the project's goals, objectives, deliverables, timeline, and costs. Its primary purpose is to establish clear expectations for both the client and service provider, ensuring everyone involved has a shared understanding of what work will be performed, when it will be completed, and at what cost.</p>
</details>
