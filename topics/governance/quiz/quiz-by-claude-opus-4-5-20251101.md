# Governance

Question: What is the primary purpose of governance in a business context?

- [ ] To maximize short-term profits for shareholders
- [ ] To establish the system of rules, processes, and practices through which a company is directed and controlled
- [ ] To handle day-to-day operational tasks and employee scheduling
- [ ] To develop marketing strategies and advertising campaigns

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>To establish the system of rules, processes, and practices through which a company is directed and controlled</p>
  <p>Governance defines how decisions are made, how authority is exercised, and how accountability happens within an organization. It encompasses key aspects including board oversight, shareholder rights, ethical standards, risk management, financial reporting, compliance, stakeholder engagement, and performance evaluation.</p>
</details>
