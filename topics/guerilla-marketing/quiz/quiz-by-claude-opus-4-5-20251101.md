# Guerilla marketing

Question: What is the primary characteristic that distinguishes guerilla marketing from traditional marketing approaches?

- [ ] It requires official sponsorship of major events
- [ ] It relies on high-budget paid advertising campaigns
- [ ] It uses creative, unconventional, and low-cost tactics to generate buzz
- [ ] It focuses exclusively on digital advertising channels

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>It uses creative, unconventional, and low-cost tactics to generate buzz</p>
  <p>Guerilla marketing is defined by its use of unexpected, unconventional, and often ambush-style tactics that don't rely on large marketing budgets.
