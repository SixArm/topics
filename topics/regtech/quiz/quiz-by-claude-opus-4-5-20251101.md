# Regtech (Regulatory technology)

Question: What is the primary purpose of regtech solutions in modern organizations?

- [ ] To replace human compliance officers entirely with automated systems
- [ ] To create new financial regulations for government agencies
- [ ] To automate and streamline regulatory compliance processes using advanced technologies
- [ ] To provide legal defense services for companies facing regulatory violations

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>To automate and streamline regulatory compliance processes using advanced technologies</p>
  <p>Regtech (regulatory technology) leverages advanced technologies such as machine learning, artificial intelligence, big data analytics, and blockchain to automate and streamline compliance processes. This helps companies manage regulatory risks more effectively, reduce compliance failures, and improve operational efficiency in an environment where regulations are increasingly complex and changing rapidly.</p>
</details>
