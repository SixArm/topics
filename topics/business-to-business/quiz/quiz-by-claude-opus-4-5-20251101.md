# Business-to-Business (B2B)

Question: What is a key characteristic that distinguishes B2B transactions from B2C transactions?

- [ ] B2B transactions only involve digital products
- [ ] B2B transactions typically involve a single decision-maker
- [ ] B2B transactions are typically larger and more complex, often requiring more negotiation between parties
- [ ] B2B transactions are always one-time purchases

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>B2B transactions are typically larger and more complex, often requiring more negotiation between parties</p>
  <p>B2B transactions differ from B2C transactions in their scale and complexity. They often involve large quantities of products or services, require extensive negotiation and communication between parties, involve multiple decision-makers in the buying process, and frequently form ongoing long-term business relationships rather than one-time purchases.</p>
</details>
