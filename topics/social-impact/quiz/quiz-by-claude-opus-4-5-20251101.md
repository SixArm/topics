# Social impact

Question: What is the primary purpose of measuring and evaluating outcomes in social impact initiatives?

- [ ] To generate marketing materials for the organization
- [ ] To assess the effectiveness of interventions on targeted social issues and community well-being
- [ ] To compare an organization's financial performance against competitors
- [ ] To fulfill regulatory compliance requirements

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>To assess the effectiveness of interventions on targeted social issues and community well-being</p>
  <p>Measuring and evaluating impact is essential for understanding whether social impact initiatives are actually achieving their intended goals. By tracking progress, collecting data, and analyzing outcomes, organizations can determine if their interventions are making a meaningful difference on the social issues they're addressing and improving the well-being of individuals and communities. This evidence-based approach enables continuous improvement and helps ensure resources are being used effectively to create positive change.</p>
</details>
