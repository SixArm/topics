# AI + legtech

Question: Which AI application in legal technology analyzes historical case data, court decisions, and legal precedents to forecast litigation outcomes and guide legal strategy?

- [ ] Contract Management
- [ ] Predictive Analytics
- [ ] Legal Research
- [ ] Compliance Monitoring

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>Predictive Analytics</p>
  <p>Predictive Analytics uses AI algorithms to analyze historical case data, court decisions, and legal precedents to predict case outcomes, assess litigation risks, and guide legal strategy. This capability helps attorneys make data-driven decisions about whether to settle or proceed with litigation, estimate potential damages, and allocate resources more effectively.</p>
</details>
