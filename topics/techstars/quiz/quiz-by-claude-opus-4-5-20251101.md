# Techstars

Question: What does Techstars provide to early-stage companies in exchange for taking a small equity stake?

- [ ] Only venture capital funding with no additional support
- [ ] Mentorship, seed funding, office space, and access to an extensive investor network
- [ ] A permanent office location and long-term employment contracts
- [ ] Marketing services exclusively for product launches

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>Mentorship, seed funding, office space, and access to an extensive investor network</p>
  <p>Techstars is a startup accelerator that provides selected startups with seed funding, office space, mentorship, access to its extensive network of investors, and other resources such as legal and accounting services during its approximately three-month program. In exchange for these benefits, Techstars takes a small equity stake in each participating startup.</p>
</details>
