# Code of Ethics

Question: What is the primary purpose of a code of ethics for technology professionals?

- [ ] To provide a competitive advantage over other organizations in the industry
- [ ] To outline ethical standards and behaviors expected of individuals while maintaining integrity and professionalism
- [ ] To create legal documentation that protects the organization from lawsuits
- [ ] To establish salary guidelines and promotion criteria for employees

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>To outline ethical standards and behaviors expected of individuals while maintaining integrity and professionalism</p>
  <p>A code of ethics is a set of principles or guidelines that outlines the ethical standards and behaviors expected of individuals or organizations. It is designed to maintain ethical behavior, integrity, and professionalism, promote accountability and transparency, help prevent unethical or illegal behavior, and build trust and credibility with clients, customers, and stakeholders.</p>
</details>
