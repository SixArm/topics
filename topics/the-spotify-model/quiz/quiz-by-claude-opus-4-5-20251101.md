# The Spotify Model

Question: In the Spotify Model, what is the primary organizational unit responsible for delivering specific business objectives or features?

- [ ] Guilds
- [ ] Squads
- [ ] Chapters
- [ ] Tribes

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>Squads</p>
  <p>Squads are cross-functional teams of 6-12 people (including developers, designers, and product owners) that are self-organizing and work together to deliver specific business objectives or features. They have a high degree of autonomy to make decisions about how they work, what technologies they use, and how they deliver value to customers.</p>
</details>
