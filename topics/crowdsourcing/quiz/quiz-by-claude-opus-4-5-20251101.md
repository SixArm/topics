# Crowdsourcing

Question: What is the primary challenge organizations face when managing crowdsourced contributions?

- [ ] Limited access to diverse talent pools
- [ ] High costs compared to traditional hiring
- [ ] Quality control of submissions
- [ ] Slow project turnaround times

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>Quality control of submissions</p>
  <p>While crowdsourcing provides access to a large pool of talent at lower cost with faster turnaround, the main challenge is managing quality. Organizations often receive numerous low-quality submissions that require significant time and resources to filter, evaluate, and sort before identifying usable contributions.</p>
</details>
