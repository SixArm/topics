# Standard Operating Procedure (SOP)

Question: What is the primary purpose of a Standard Operating Procedure (SOP) in an organization?

- [ ] To replace the need for employee training programs
- [ ] To ensure consistency, efficiency, and quality in executing routine or critical processes
- [ ] To track employee performance metrics and productivity
- [ ] To establish organizational hierarchy and reporting structures

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>To ensure consistency, efficiency, and quality in executing routine or critical processes</p>
  <p>SOPs are documented step-by-step instructions developed specifically to ensure consistent execution of tasks, improve operational efficiency, and maintain quality standards. While SOPs support training and compliance, their primary purpose is to provide a standardized approach that produces reliable, repeatable results across the organization.</p>
</details>
