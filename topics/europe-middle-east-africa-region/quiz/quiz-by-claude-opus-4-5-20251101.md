# Europe, Middle East, Africa (EMEA)

Question: What is the primary challenge that makes the EMEA region complex for technology businesses to operate in?

- [ ] Limited internet infrastructure across all countries
- [ ] Lack of skilled technology workforce
- [ ] Diverse regulatory landscape with different legal frameworks across countries
- [ ] Absence of major economies in the region

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>Diverse regulatory landscape with different legal frameworks across countries</p>
  <p>The EMEA region spans multiple countries across three continents, each with its own legal and regulatory frameworks. For technology professionals, this means navigating data protection laws (like GDPR in Europe), varying compliance requirements, and different business regulations. This complexity requires significant resources and expertise to ensure operations remain compliant across all markets where a company operates.</p>
</details>
