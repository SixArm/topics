# Front-office applications

Question: What is the primary focus of front-office applications in an organization?

- [ ] Managing internal payroll and human resources operations
- [ ] Customer interactions and revenue generation
- [ ] Database administration and server maintenance
- [ ] Supply chain logistics and inventory tracking

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>Customer interactions and revenue generation</p>
  <p>Front-office applications are designed to be customer-facing and focus on customer interactions and revenue generation. They include systems like CRM, Point-of-Sale, and E-commerce platforms. Key characteristics include user-friendly interfaces, real-time data access, integration with customer communication channels, and an emphasis on enhancing the customer experience through personalized services and efficient problem resolution.</p>
</details>
