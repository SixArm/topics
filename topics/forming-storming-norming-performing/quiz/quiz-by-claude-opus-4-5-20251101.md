# Forming, Storming, Norming, Performing

Question: In the Forming, Storming, Norming, Performing model of team development, during which stage do team members typically experience conflicts, challenge leadership, and compete for power?

- [ ] Performing
- [ ] Storming
- [ ] Forming
- [ ] Norming

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>Storming</p>
  <p>The Storming stage is when group members begin to voice their opinions and ideas, which can lead to conflicts. Team members may challenge the leader, question goals, and compete for power. This stage is often marked by tension and frustration, but it is an essential step in the development process as the team works through differences before establishing norms and becoming fully functional.</p>
</details>
