# Agile principle 1: Satisfy the customer

Question: According to Agile Principle 1, what is the highest priority for achieving customer satisfaction?

- [ ] Comprehensive documentation before development begins
- [ ] Lengthy development cycles to ensure complete features
- [ ] Early and continuous delivery of valuable software
- [ ] Strict adherence to the original project plan

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>Early and continuous delivery of valuable software</p>
  <p>Agile Principle 1 states: "Our highest priority is to satisfy the customer through early and continuous delivery of valuable software." This approach shifts away from lengthy, monolithic releases toward providing immediate value to end users, enabling teams to validate assumptions, gather real-world feedback, and iterate based on actual user needs.</p>
</details>
