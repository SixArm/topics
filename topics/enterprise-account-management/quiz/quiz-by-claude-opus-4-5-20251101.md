# Enterprise account management

Question: What is the primary purpose of enterprise account management?

- [ ] To reduce customer service costs by automating support tickets
- [ ] To nurture relationships with large company customers and drive long-term growth
- [ ] To replace traditional sales teams with digital marketing channels
- [ ] To standardize pricing across all customer segments

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>To nurture relationships with large company customers and drive long-term growth</p>
  <p>Enterprise account management (also called key account management or strategic account management) is a comprehensive approach focused on nurturing relationships with customers at large companies. It involves understanding the customer's business deeply, aligning resources to deliver value, fostering loyalty, and driving long-term growth through activities like building trust, collaborating across departments, and maximizing customer lifetime value.</p>
</details>
