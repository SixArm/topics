# Government Relations

Question: What is the primary purpose of a Government Relations (GR) team in a technology company?

- [ ] To develop new software products for government contracts
- [ ] To manage the company's relationship with government entities and influence public policy in favor of the company's interests
- [ ] To provide technical support exclusively to government customers
- [ ] To recruit employees from government agencies

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>To manage the company's relationship with government entities and influence public policy in favor of the company's interests</p>
  <p>Government Relations (also known as Government Affairs or Public Affairs) is a company function that manages relationships with government officials, policymakers, and regulatory bodies. The GR team's core mission is to advocate for the company's interests, influence public policy, and ensure a favorable regulatory environment through activities such as policy analysis, lobbying, compliance management, and political engagement.</p>
</details>
