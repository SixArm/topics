# Subscription pricing

Question: What is considered a key advantage of the subscription pricing model for businesses?

- [ ] It eliminates the need for ongoing product development
- [ ] It provides a predictable and recurring revenue stream
- [ ] It requires customers to pay the full cost upfront
- [ ] It prevents customers from canceling their service

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>It provides a predictable and recurring revenue stream</p>
  <p>Unlike one-time purchases, subscription pricing generates consistent revenue over time, creating a more stable financial foundation and providing greater visibility into future cash flows for the business.</p>
</details>
