- [ ] A psychological construct describing an individual's preference for distributing resources, ranging from prosocial (cooperative) to individualistic to competitive orientations
- [ ] A software development methodology that prioritizes competitive sprints between team members to maximize productivity

  <p>Social value orientation (SVO) describes how individuals prefer to distribute resources in social situationsâ€”whether they prioritize cooperation and helping others (prosocial), focus on maximizing their own outcomes (individualistic), or seek to outperform others even at a cost (competitive).
