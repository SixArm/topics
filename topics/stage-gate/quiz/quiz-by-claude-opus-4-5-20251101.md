# Stage-gate

Question: What is the primary purpose of the "gates" in the stage-gate process?

- [ ] To assign team members to different project phases
- [ ] To serve as decision points where progress is evaluated against predefined criteria before advancing
- [ ] To automatically generate project documentation
- [ ] To schedule recurring team meetings throughout the project lifecycle

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>To serve as decision points where progress is evaluated against predefined criteria before advancing</p>
  <p>Gates are the critical decision points between stages where a cross-functional gatekeeping team evaluates the project's viability, alignment with strategic goals, risks, and resource requirements.
