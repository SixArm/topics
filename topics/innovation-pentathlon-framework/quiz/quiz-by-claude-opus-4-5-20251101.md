# Innovation Pentathlon framework

Question: What are the five dimensions of the Innovation Pentathlon framework?

- [ ] Ideation, Validation, Execution, Scaling, and Measurement
- [ ] Research, Development, Testing, Marketing, and Sales
- [ ] Planning, Budgeting, Staffing, Controlling, and Reporting
- [ ] Generation, Selection, Implementation, Strategy, and People

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>Generation, Selection, Implementation, Strategy, and People</p>
  <p>The Innovation Pentathlon framework outlines five key dimensions: Generation (fostering creativity and idea generation), Selection (evaluating and choosing promising ideas), Implementation (turning ideas into tangible outputs), Strategy (defining innovation direction and positioning), and People (building teams and culture that support innovation).</p>
</details>
