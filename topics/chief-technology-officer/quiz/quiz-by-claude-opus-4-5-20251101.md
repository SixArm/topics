# Chief Technology Officer (CTO)

Question: What is the primary focus of the Chief Technology Officer (CTO) role?

- [ ] Managing the company's financial budget and investment portfolio
- [ ] Overseeing human resources policies and employee recruitment
- [ ] Using technology to create new products, streamline business processes, and drive growth
- [ ] Handling legal compliance and regulatory affairs

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>Using technology to create new products, streamline business processes, and drive growth</p>
  <p>The CTO is an executive-level position focused on the overall technology strategy, innovation, and implementation. The role centers on leveraging technology to develop new products and services, improve existing business processes, and ultimately drive company growth. This distinguishes the CTO from other C-suite roles like the CFO (finance), CHRO (human resources), or CLO (legal).</p>
</details>
