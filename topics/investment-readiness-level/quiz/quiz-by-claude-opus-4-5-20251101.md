# Investment Readiness Level (IRL)

Question: What does an Investment Readiness Level (IRL) of 8 indicate about a startup?

- [ ] The startup is conducting basic research to determine feasibility
- [ ] The startup has achieved product-market fit and is scaling operations to meet demand
- [ ] The startup has validated its idea and started developing a business plan
- [ ] The startup has a fully developed product and is focused on bringing it to market

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>The startup has achieved product-market fit and is scaling operations to meet demand</p>
  <p>IRL 8 represents the "Scaling" stage, where a startup has successfully achieved product-market fit and is now focused on scaling its operations to meet growing demand. This is a critical milestone that indicates the business model is proven and the company is ready for significant growth investment.</p>
</details>
