# Business Model Canvas

Question: What is the primary purpose of the Business Model Canvas (BMC)?

- [ ] To create detailed financial projections and budgets for investors
- [ ] To describe, design, and analyze a business model visually
- [ ] To document technical architecture and system requirements
- [ ] To track daily operational metrics and KPIs

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>To describe, design, and analyze a business model visually</p>
  <p>The Business Model Canvas is a visual tool that helps entrepreneurs and businesses describe, design, and analyze their business model by identifying key components (such as customer segments, value proposition, channels, revenue streams, and cost structure) and how they interrelate. This enables strategic decision-making about resources, offerings, customers, and growth opportunities.</p>
</details>
