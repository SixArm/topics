# Culture of innovation

Question: What is a defining characteristic of an organizational culture of innovation?

- [ ] Strict hierarchical approval processes for all new ideas
- [ ] Viewing failure as an opportunity for learning and growth
- [ ] Limiting idea contributions to senior leadership
- [ ] Maintaining separate departmental silos to preserve expertise

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>Viewing failure as an opportunity for learning and growth</p>
  <p>A culture of innovation embraces calculated risk-taking and experimentation, treating failures as learning opportunities rather than reasons for punishment. This approach encourages employees to try new approaches without fear, which is essential for fostering creativity and breakthrough innovations.</p>
</details>
