# Business Model Innovation

Question: What is the primary goal of business model innovation (BMI)?

- [ ] To reduce employee headcount and minimize operational costs
- [ ] To copy competitors' successful business strategies
- [ ] To change how a company creates, delivers, and captures value to achieve competitive advantage
- [ ] To maintain existing operations without any modifications

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>To change how a company creates, delivers, and captures value to achieve competitive advantage</p>
  <p>Business model innovation involves the creation, modification, or reconfiguration of fundamental business model elements—including value proposition, revenue model, distribution channels, cost structure, partnerships, customer experience, and social impact—to deliver new value propositions, capture new markets, or improve existing operations. The core purpose is to achieve competitive advantage and drive growth through these strategic changes.</p>
</details>
