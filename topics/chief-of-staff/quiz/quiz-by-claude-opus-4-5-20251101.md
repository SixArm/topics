# Chief of Staff

Question: What is the primary role of a Chief of Staff (CoS) in an organization?

- [ ] To manage the organization's financial accounting and tax compliance
- [ ] To serve as the top legal counsel for the company
- [ ] To serve as a key partner to the CEO, providing strategic guidance and managing operations
- [ ] To lead the engineering team in software development projects

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>To serve as a key partner to the CEO, providing strategic guidance and managing operations</p>
  <p>The Chief of Staff serves as a trusted advisor to the CEO or top-level executive, helping to drive the organization's vision, goals, and overall performance. This involves providing strategic guidance, managing day-to-day operations, facilitating communication between departments, and supporting decision-making through research and analysis.</p>
</details>
