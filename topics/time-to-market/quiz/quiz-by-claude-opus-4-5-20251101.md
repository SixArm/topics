# Time-to-market

Question: What is the primary business benefit of shortening time-to-market (TTM)?

- [ ] Reducing the need for regulatory compliance
- [ ] Eliminating the product testing phase
- [ ] Gaining competitive advantage by responding faster to market demands
- [ ] Increasing manufacturing costs

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>Gaining competitive advantage by responding faster to market demands</p>
  <p>Shortening TTM provides a competitive advantage because companies can respond more effectively to changes in customer demand, stay ahead of competitors, and capture market share before others do. This enables businesses to be more agile in the marketplace rather than eliminating important processes like testing or compliance.</p>
</details>
