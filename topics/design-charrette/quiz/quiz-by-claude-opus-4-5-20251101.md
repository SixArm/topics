# Design charrette

Question: What is the primary purpose of a design charrette?

- [ ] To assign individual tasks to team members for independent completion over several weeks
- [ ] To conduct user acceptance testing on a completed product before launch
- [ ] To bring together stakeholders in a collaborative, intensive, time-limited session to generate ideas and develop solutions
- [ ] To document technical specifications and system requirements for developers

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>To bring together stakeholders in a collaborative, intensive, time-limited session to generate ideas and develop solutions</p>
  <p>A design charrette is specifically defined as a collaborative, intensive, and time-limited design process that brings together designers, stakeholders, and subject matter experts to generate ideas, explore solutions, and develop a comprehensive plan. The key characteristics are its collaborative nature, time constraints, and focus on producing cohesive design solutions that meet all stakeholders' needs.</p>
</details>
