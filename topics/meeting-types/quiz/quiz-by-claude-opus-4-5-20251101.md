# Meeting types

Question: Which type of meeting is specifically designed to gather input, discuss options, and reach consensus or make a final determination on a course of action?

- [ ] Status meetings
- [ ] Decision-making meetings
- [ ] Training meetings
- [ ] Informational meetings

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>Decision-making meetings</p>
  <p>Decision-making meetings focus specifically on gathering input from participants, discussing available options, and reaching a consensus or making a final decision. This distinguishes them from other meeting types: informational meetings are one-way communications, status meetings share progress updates, and training meetings focus on education and skill development.</p>
</details>
