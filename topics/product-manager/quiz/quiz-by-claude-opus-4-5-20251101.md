# Product Manager

Question: What is the primary purpose of cross-functional collaboration for a Product Manager?

- [ ] To delegate technical decisions entirely to the engineering team
- [ ] To focus exclusively on competitor analysis and market research
- [ ] To work with sales, marketing, design, and engineering teams to develop go-to-market strategies, positioning, and ensure successful product delivery
- [ ] To manage the company's financial budgets and accounting processes

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>To work with sales, marketing, design, and engineering teams to develop go-to-market strategies, positioning, and ensure successful product delivery</p>
  <p>A Product Manager collaborates across multiple functions—engineering, design, marketing, and sales—to ensure successful product delivery that provides customer value and aligns with company goals. This includes developing go-to-market strategies, positioning, messaging, product training, sales collateral, and customer support guidance.</p>
</details>
