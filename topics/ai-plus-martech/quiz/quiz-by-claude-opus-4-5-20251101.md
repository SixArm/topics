# AI + martech

Question: Which AI application in martech enables real-time customer interaction by providing personalized recommendations and guiding customers through the buyer's journey?

- [ ] Social Listening
- [ ] Chatbots
- [ ] Customer Segmentation
- [ ] Content Creation

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>Chatbots</p>
  <p>AI chatbots and virtual assistants can interact with customers in real-time, providing personalized recommendations, answering queries, and guiding customers through the buyer's journey. This real-time interaction capability distinguishes chatbots from other AI martech applications like segmentation or content creation, which operate asynchronously.</p>
</details>
