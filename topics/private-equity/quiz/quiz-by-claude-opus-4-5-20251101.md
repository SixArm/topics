# Private equity (PE)

Question: What is the typical investment horizon for private equity investors?

- [ ] 1 to 2 years
- [ ] 5 to 7 years or more
- [ ] 10 to 15 years minimum
- [ ] Less than 6 months

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>5 to 7 years or more</p>
  <p>Private equity investors typically have a long-term investment horizon, often holding onto their investments for five to seven years or more. This extended timeframe allows them to implement operational and financial improvements, support growth initiatives, and ultimately realize returns through a sale or public offering.</p>
</details>
