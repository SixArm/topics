# Critical to quality (CTQ)

Question: What is the primary purpose of Critical to Quality (CTQ) metrics in Six Sigma methodology?

- [ ] To reduce the number of employees involved in quality control processes
- [ ] To capture customer requirements in a measurable and quantifiable way
- [ ] To increase the complexity of manufacturing processes
- [ ] To eliminate the need for customer feedback collection

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>To capture customer requirements in a measurable and quantifiable way</p>
  <p>CTQ metrics are designed to translate customer expectations into specific, measurable characteristics of a product or service. This enables organizations to identify gaps between current performance and customer expectations, set performance targets, and prioritize process improvements that directly impact customer satisfaction.</p>
</details>
