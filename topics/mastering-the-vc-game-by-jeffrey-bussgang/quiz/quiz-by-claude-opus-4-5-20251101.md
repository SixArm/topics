# Mastering the VC Game by Jeffrey Bussgang

Question: According to Jeffrey Bussgang in "Mastering the VC Game," what is one of the most critical factors for entrepreneurs seeking venture capital funding?

- [ ] Focusing exclusively on the product and avoiding investor meetings until launch
- [ ] Submitting identical pitches to all investors regardless of their stage or preferences
- [ ] Building and cultivating strong relationships with potential investors over time
- [ ] Accepting the first term sheet offered to avoid prolonged negotiations

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>Building and cultivating strong relationships with potential investors over time</p>
  <p>Bussgang emphasizes that relationship-building is fundamental to successful fundraising. Entrepreneurs need to establish strong connections with potential investors and nurture those relationships over time. He also stresses the importance of persistence in the face of rejection and tailoring pitches to meet the specific expectations of different investors at various stages of the fundraising process.</p>
</details>
