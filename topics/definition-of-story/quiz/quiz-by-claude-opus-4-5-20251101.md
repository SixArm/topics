# Definition of Story

Question: What is the standard format for writing a user story in agile software engineering?

- [ ] "Given [precondition], when [action], then [expected result]"
- [ ] "As a developer, I will implement [feature] using [technology]"
- [ ] "The system shall [requirement] with [performance metric]"
- [ ] "I am a [type of user]. I want [some goal] so that [some reason]"

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>"I am a [type of user]. I want [some goal] so that [some reason]"</p>
  <p>This format ensures the story identifies the user, defines the desired functionality, and explains the business value or benefit. By focusing on user needs and outcomes rather than technical implementation details, stories help teams maintain alignment with business objectives while enabling iterative development and continuous feedback.</p>
</details>
