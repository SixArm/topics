# Business plan competition

Question: What is the primary purpose of a business plan competition for technology entrepreneurs?

- [ ] To provide legal incorporation services for new startups
- [ ] To evaluate business viability and connect startups with investors, mentors, and funding opportunities
- [ ] To guarantee venture capital funding for all participants
- [ ] To replace the need for market research and validation

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>To evaluate business viability and connect startups with investors, mentors, and funding opportunities</p>
  <p>Business plan competitions are designed to assess the viability and potential of proposed businesses through judging panels, while also providing valuable support including mentorship, coaching, networking with investors, and often cash prizes or funding to help promising startups launch and grow.</p>
</details>
