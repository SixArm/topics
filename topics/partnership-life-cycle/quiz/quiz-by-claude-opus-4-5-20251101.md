# Partnership Life Cycle

Question: What is the primary purpose of the Maintenance Phase in the Partnership Life Cycle?

- [ ] To negotiate and formalize the partnership agreement, including roles and responsibilities
- [ ] To identify potential partners and explore the feasibility of collaboration
- [ ] To review achievements and decide whether to renew, modify, or terminate the partnership
- [ ] To manage relationships, address conflicts, build capacity, and adapt to changes

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>To manage relationships, address conflicts, build capacity, and adapt to changes</p>
  <p>The Maintenance Phase focuses on ongoing partnership management: fostering strong relationships, addressing conflicts, maintaining regular communication, building capacity through training or resources to enhance capabilities, and adapting strategies or scope as needed to keep the partnership effective over time.</p>
</details>
