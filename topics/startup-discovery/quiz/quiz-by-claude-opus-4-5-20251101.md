# Startup discovery

Question: What are the three key areas of startup discovery that entrepreneurs should focus on to validate a business idea?

- [ ] Financial discovery, legal discovery, and operational discovery
- [ ] Market discovery, customer discovery, and product discovery
- [ ] Investor discovery, competitor discovery, and technology discovery
- [ ] Brand discovery, channel discovery, and revenue discovery

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>Market discovery, customer discovery, and product discovery</p>
  <p>Startup discovery involves three key areas: market discovery (researching market size and potential), customer discovery (identifying target customers and understanding their needs and pain points), and product discovery (testing the product idea to determine viability). This iterative process helps entrepreneurs develop products that meet the needs of their target market.</p>
</details>
