# Microcredentials

Question: What is a key advantage of microcredentials for technology professionals looking to develop new skills?

- [ ] They require enrollment in a full-time academic program
- [ ] They are only available through traditional universities
- [ ] They offer flexibility with self-paced, online delivery that allows completion while working
- [ ] They replace the need for any practical work experience

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>They offer flexibility with self-paced, online delivery that allows completion while working</p>
  <p>Microcredentials are designed to be flexible, often self-paced and delivered online, enabling professionals to complete them while working full-time or managing other commitments. This makes them particularly valuable for technology professionals who need to upskill or reskill in response to rapidly changing workforce demands without interrupting their careers.</p>
</details>
