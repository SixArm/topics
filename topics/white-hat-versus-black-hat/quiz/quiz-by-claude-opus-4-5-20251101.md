# White hat versus black hat

Question: What is the primary distinction between white hat hackers and black hat hackers in cybersecurity?

- [ ] White hat hackers work alone while black hat hackers work in teams
- [ ] White hat hackers focus on hardware while black hat hackers focus on software
- [ ] White hat hackers identify vulnerabilities ethically and legally to improve security, while black hat hackers exploit systems for malicious purposes
- [ ] White hat hackers use older tools while black hat hackers use newer technologies

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>White hat hackers identify vulnerabilities ethically and legally to improve security, while black hat hackers exploit systems for malicious purposes</p>
  <p>White hat hackers operate with permission and within legal boundaries to find and report security weaknesses, conducting activities like penetration testing and security audits. Black hat hackers engage in unauthorized intrusions for malicious goals such as data theft, ransomware attacks, or other cybercrimes, which are illegal and punishable by law.</p>
</details>
