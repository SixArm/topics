# Growth hacking

Question: What is the primary approach that distinguishes growth hacking from traditional marketing?

- [ ] Focusing exclusively on paid advertising campaigns
- [ ] Using data-driven experimentation with rapid iteration to test marketing approaches
- [ ] Relying solely on word-of-mouth recommendations
- [ ] Maintaining consistent long-term strategies without changes

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>Using data-driven experimentation with rapid iteration to test marketing approaches</p>
  <p>Growth hacking is fundamentally about using data-driven experimentation, creativity, and unconventional methods to rapidly test and iterate on different marketing approaches. This distinguishes it from traditional marketing by emphasizing metrics-focused rapid experimentation, cross-functional collaboration, and the ability to quickly determine what works and pivot accordingly.</p>
</details>
