# Asia-Pacific (APAC)

Question: What is one of the key challenges for businesses operating in the APAC region?

- [ ] Limited access to manufacturing facilities
- [ ] Lack of global trade connections
- [ ] Diverse regulatory landscape across different countries
- [ ] Shortage of technology industry presence

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>Diverse regulatory landscape across different countries</p>
  <p>The APAC region encompasses many countries with different legal and regulatory frameworks. Businesses must navigate these differences to operate successfully, making regulatory diversity one of the key challenges alongside linguistic and cultural differences across the region.</p>
</details>
