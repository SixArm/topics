# Strategy map

Question: What is the primary purpose of a strategy map?

- [ ] To create detailed financial budgets for each department
- [ ] To document technical architecture and system dependencies
- [ ] To visually represent and communicate an organization's strategic objectives and how they relate to each other
- [ ] To track individual employee performance reviews

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>To visually represent and communicate an organization's strategic objectives and how they relate to each other</p>
  <p>A strategy map is a visual representation of a company's strategy and objectives, designed to help organizations define and communicate their strategic goals, align resources, and monitor progress. It shows how strategic objectives relate to each other and to the organization's overall mission and vision, typically using a hierarchical structure with the mission at the top, followed by strategic themes, specific goals, and linked KPIs.</p>
</details>
