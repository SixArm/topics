# Radical Candor by Kim Scott

Question: What is the core idea behind Radical Candor that Kim Scott advocates for effective leadership?

- [ ] Challenge directly while maintaining emotional distance from employees
- [ ] Care personally and challenge directly
- [ ] Avoid difficult conversations to maintain team harmony
- [ ] Focus primarily on positive feedback to boost morale

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>Care personally and challenge directly</p>
  <p>Kim Scott's Radical Candor framework is built on two fundamental dimensions that leaders must balance simultaneously: caring personally about employees as individuals while also being willing to challenge them directly with honest feedback. This combination creates a supportive environment where employees feel valued yet receive the candid guidance they need to grow. Without both elements, feedback can come across as either insincere (challenging without caring) or unhelpfully soft (caring without challenging).</p>
</details>
