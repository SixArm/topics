# Stay Hungry, Stay Foolish

Question: What is the core philosophy behind the phrase "Stay Hungry, Stay Foolish"?

- [ ] Maintain financial frugality and avoid expensive business ventures
- [ ] Never lose curiosity and drive for learning, and don't be afraid to take risks and make mistakes
- [ ] Focus on foolish ideas that competitors overlook for easy market wins
- [ ] Prioritize speed to market over careful planning and research

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>Never lose curiosity and drive for learning, and don't be afraid to take risks and make mistakes</p>
  <p>Steve Jobs used this phrase from The Whole Earth Catalog's 1974 final issue in his 2005 Stanford commencement speech. "Hungry" means constantly seeking new ideas and knowledge, pushing beyond perceived limits, and never becoming complacent. "Foolish" means taking risks, thinking outside the box, following intuition, and not being afraid to failâ€”even if it means going against conventional wisdom.</p>
</details>
