# UI/UX Design Guide Conclusion

Question: What is the primary purpose of providing a repository URL and contact information in a UI/UX guide conclusion?

- [ ] To advertise consulting services to potential clients
- [ ] To enable readers to provide feedback, ask questions, and suggest improvements
- [ ] To showcase the author's portfolio of design work
- [ ] To require readers to submit bug reports before using the guide

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>To enable readers to provide feedback, ask questions, and suggest improvements</p>
  <p>The conclusion emphasizes that feedback and suggestions are "very much appreciated, because this helps the guide improve and evolve." The repository and email contact are provided specifically so readers can "ask any question, suggest any improvement, point out any error, and the like." This reflects a collaborative, iterative approach to maintaining useful documentation.</p>
</details>
