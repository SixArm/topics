# How to interview a user

Question: What is the primary purpose of using open-ended questions during a user interview?

- [ ] To quickly confirm yes/no answers and save time
- [ ] To guide users toward predetermined responses
- [ ] To encourage detailed responses and allow users to share their experiences, thoughts, and preferences
- [ ] To limit the scope of the conversation to specific topics

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>To encourage detailed responses and allow users to share their experiences, thoughts, and preferences</p>
  <p>Open-ended, non-biased questions are essential in user interviews because they allow users to express themselves freely and provide rich, detailed information about their experiences. Unlike closed questions that yield simple yes/no answers, open-ended questions help uncover deeper insights, unexpected perspectives, and genuine user needs that are crucial for product development and improvement.</p>
</details>
