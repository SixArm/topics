# Compliance manager

Question: What is the primary purpose of a compliance manager in an organization?

- [ ] To maximize quarterly profits and shareholder returns
- [ ] To manage employee recruitment and onboarding processes
- [ ] To ensure the organization operates in accordance with relevant laws, regulations, and industry standards
- [ ] To develop marketing strategies and brand positioning

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>To ensure the organization operates in accordance with relevant laws, regulations, and industry standards</p>
  <p>A compliance manager is responsible for ensuring that an organization operates in accordance with relevant laws, regulations, and industry standards. This role promotes ethical conduct and is crucial in maintaining the integrity and reputation of the organization. Key responsibilities include regulatory compliance monitoring, compliance program development, risk assessment, training and education, monitoring and auditing, and investigation of compliance violations.</p>
</details>
