# Service Obtainable Market (SOM)

Question: What does Service Obtainable Market (SOM) represent in market analysis?

- [ ] The total potential demand for a company's offerings across all possible markets worldwide
- [ ] The portion of the Service Addressable Market (SAM) a company can realistically capture given its resources and competitive environment
- [ ] The entire segment of customers who might theoretically be interested in a product category
- [ ] The maximum revenue a company could achieve if it had unlimited resources and no competition
