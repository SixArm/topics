# UI/UX Design Guide Introduction

Question: What is the primary purpose of the UI/UX Design Guide as described in the introduction?

- [ ] To provide comprehensive coding tutorials for building user interfaces from scratch
- [ ] To serve as a glossary guide for quick, easy learning about UI/UX concepts one topic per page
- [ ] To certify professionals in user experience design methodologies
- [ ] To replace formal academic education in computer science and design

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>To serve as a glossary guide for quick, easy learning about UI/UX concepts one topic per page</p>
  <p>The guide explicitly states it is "a glossary guide ebook that describes one topic per page" intended for "quick easy learning about concepts, tactics, and ideas." It helps practitioners teach stakeholders, helps stakeholders collaborate with their teams using the right terminology, and helps students bridge the gap between academic studies and industry jobs.</p>
</details>
