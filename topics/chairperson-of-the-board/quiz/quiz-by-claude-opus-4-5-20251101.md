# Chairperson of the Board

Question: What is the primary role of the Chairperson of the Board (COB) in an organization?

- [ ] Managing day-to-day operations and employee performance reviews
- [ ] Leading the board of directors and providing oversight and guidance
- [ ] Handling all financial transactions and budget approvals
- [ ] Writing and implementing the organization's software systems

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>Leading the board of directors and providing oversight and guidance</p>
  <p>The Chairperson of the Board is responsible for leading the board of directors, running board meetings, setting agendas, facilitating communication between the board and organizational leadership, fostering board culture, overseeing board performance, and ensuring compliance with legal and ethical responsibilities.
