# Silicon Valley Product Group (SVPG)

Question: What is the "Product Triad" concept promoted by Silicon Valley Product Group?

- [ ] A three-phase development cycle of planning, building, and testing
- [ ] A framework for measuring product success using three key metrics
- [ ] Strong collaboration between Product Manager, Designer, and Tech Lead
- [ ] Three sequential stages of product discovery, delivery, and deployment

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>Strong collaboration between Product Manager, Designer, and Tech Lead</p>
  <p>SVPG's Product Triad emphasizes that effective product development requires close collaboration among three key roles: the Product Manager (who owns the problem and business viability), the Designer (who owns the user experience), and the Tech Lead (who owns technical feasibility). This cross-functional partnership ensures products are valuable, usable, and feasible.</p>
</details>
