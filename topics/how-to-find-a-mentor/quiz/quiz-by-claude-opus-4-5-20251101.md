# How to find a mentor

Question: What is the recommended first step when seeking a mentor for professional growth?

- [ ] Send messages to as many industry leaders as possible
- [ ] Wait for a mentor to approach you naturally
- [ ] Define your goals and clarify what guidance you need
- [ ] Join every professional organization available

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>Define your goals and clarify what guidance you need</p>
  <p>Before seeking a mentor, you should first clarify your objectives and determine what areas you want guidance and support in. Having a clear understanding of your goals will help you identify the right mentor who can address your specific needs for personal and professional growth.</p>
</details>
