# The 5 C's of Agile Management

Question: Which of the following correctly lists all five C's of Agile Management?

- [ ] Coding, Compliance, Certification, Control, Completion
- [ ] Communication, Collaboration, Customer Focus, Continuous Improvement, Courage
- [ ] Commitment, Coordination, Consistency, Clarity, Competence
- [ ] Change, Capacity, Creativity, Consensus, Capability

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>Communication, Collaboration, Customer Focus, Continuous Improvement, Courage</p>
  <p>The 5 C's of Agile Management are Communication (transparent exchanges between all parties), Collaboration (working as a unified cross-functional team), Customer Focus (delivering genuine value through feedback loops), Continuous Improvement (kaizen-driven reflection and enhancement), and Courage (challenging assumptions and embracing change). These principles drive successful agile transformation by ensuring teams remain aligned, work together effectively, deliver real user value, constantly improve their processes, and have the courage to make difficult decisions when necessary.</p>
</details>
