# High availability (HA)

Question: What is the primary goal of high availability (HA) in system design?

- [ ] To maximize the processing speed of individual servers
- [ ] To reduce the total cost of hardware infrastructure
- [ ] To eliminate or minimize single points of failure and ensure continuous operation with minimal downtime
- [ ] To simplify system administration and reduce complexity

  <p>To eliminate or minimize single points of failure and ensure continuous operation with minimal downtime</p>
