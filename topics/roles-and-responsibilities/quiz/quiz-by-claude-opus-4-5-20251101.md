# Roles and responsibilities

Question: What is the primary purpose of establishing clear roles and responsibilities within an organization?

- [ ] To limit employee creativity and autonomy
- [ ] To establish accountability and promote efficient communication and collaboration
- [ ] To reduce the number of employees needed
- [ ] To create a rigid hierarchy that prevents change

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>To establish accountability and promote efficient communication and collaboration</p>
  <p>Clear roles and responsibilities are essential components of organizational structure because they define who is accountable for what tasks, which enables teams to work together more effectively. When everyone understands their duties and how they contribute to the organization's goals, communication improves and collaboration becomes more efficient.</p>
</details>
