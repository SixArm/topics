# Red tape

Question: In technology organizations, what does "red tape" primarily refer to?

- [ ] A type of data backup medium used in legacy systems
- [ ] Excessive bureaucracy and administrative procedures that delay progress
- [ ] A color-coded priority system for critical bugs
- [ ] Security protocols for protecting sensitive documents

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>Excessive bureaucracy and administrative procedures that delay progress</p>
  <p>"Red tape" refers to unnecessary bureaucratic obstacles, rigid protocols, and excessive paperwork that slow down decision-making, hinder efficiency, and can restrict innovation in technology organizations. The term originated from British government practices of tying official documents with red tape to indicate importance.</p>
</details>
