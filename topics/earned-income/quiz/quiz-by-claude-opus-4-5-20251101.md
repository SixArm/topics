# Earned income

Question: What is the primary distinction between earned income and other forms of income such as investment income or passive income?

- [ ] Earned income is taxed at a lower rate than other income types
- [ ] Earned income is generated through owning assets or investments
- [ ] Earned income comes from employment or self-employment activities
- [ ] Earned income is exempt from government taxation

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>Earned income comes from employment or self-employment activities</p>
  <p>Earned income is specifically income generated through work, including wages, salaries, commissions, bonuses, tips, and freelance or contract work. This distinguishes it from investment income or passive income, which are earned through owning assets or investments rather than through active labor.</p>
</details>
