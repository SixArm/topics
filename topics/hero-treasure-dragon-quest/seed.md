# Hero Treasure Dragon Quest (HTDQ) 

The "Hero-Treasure-Dragon-Quest" is a leadership presentation approach to create a narrative with clear logical hierarchy.

## The Pyramid Structure

* The Answer (The Tip): To secure the legendary treasure, the Hero must systematically neutralize the Dragon's threat and navigate the dungeon's physical barriers.
* Supporting Arguments (The Logic):
1. Preparation: Equipping for the specific draconic threat.
   2. Infiltration: Accessing the lair through environmental obstacles.
   3. Execution: Engaging the dragon and extracting the treasure.

## 1. Combat Readiness (The "How")

* Offensive: Acquire a Dragon-slaying sword or piercing spells.
* Defensive: Obtain fireproof armor or a heat-shielding potion.
* Intellect: Study the Dragon’s specific weakness (e.g., a missing scale).

## 2. Environmental Navigation (The "Where")

* Entry: Locating the hidden entrance in the Crag of Despair.
* Hazards: Mapping the lava pits and pressure-plate traps.
* Exit Strategy: Identifying a secondary route for a heavy-treasure escape.

## 3. Asset Recovery (The "What")

* Containment: Bringing bags of holding or pulleys for heavy gold.
* Verification: Ensuring the "Sun-Stone" is the genuine artifact, not a decoy.
* Post-Quest Logistics: Planning the transport back to the Kingdom.
