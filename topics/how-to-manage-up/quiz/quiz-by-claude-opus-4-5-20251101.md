# How to manage up

Question: What is the most effective approach when facing challenges with your manager?

- [ ] Wait for your manager to notice the problem and provide direction
- [ ] Escalate immediately to your manager's supervisor
- [ ] Come prepared with potential solutions to demonstrate problem-solving skills
- [ ] Focus only on explaining why the challenge exists

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>Come prepared with potential solutions to demonstrate problem-solving skills</p>
  <p>Being solutions-oriented is a key aspect of managing up effectively. Rather than simply presenting problems, technology professionals who come prepared with potential options demonstrate initiative, problem-solving skills, and respect for their manager's time. This approach builds trust, shows competence, and positions you as a valuable contributor who can handle challenges proactively.</p>
</details>
