# How to lead a meeting

Question: What is the recommended practice for concluding a meeting effectively?

- [ ] End the meeting abruptly once all agenda items are covered to save time
- [ ] Summarize outcomes and action items, thank participants, and send follow-up communication
- [ ] Allow the meeting to continue until all participants have finished speaking
- [ ] Assign all action items via email the following week

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>Summarize outcomes and action items, thank participants, and send follow-up communication</p>
  <p>Closing a meeting effectively involves summarizing the key outcomes and action items discussed, thanking participants for their contributions, and following up with communication that includes the summary and any relevant resources. This ensures clarity on next steps and maintains accountability among team members.</p>
</details>
