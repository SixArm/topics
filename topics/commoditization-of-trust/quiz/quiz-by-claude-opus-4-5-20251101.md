# Commoditization of trust

Question: What is the primary mechanism by which digital platforms like Airbnb and Uber have commoditized trust?

- [ ] Requiring government-issued identification from all users
- [ ] Building personal relationships between users before transactions
- [ ] Enabling users to rate and review each other based on their experiences
- [ ] Using blockchain technology to verify transactions

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>Enabling users to rate and review each other based on their experiences</p>
  <p>Digital platforms have commoditized trust by creating standardized rating and review systems. Instead of relying on personal relationships or reputation built over time, users can now depend on aggregated feedback from other users' experiences.
