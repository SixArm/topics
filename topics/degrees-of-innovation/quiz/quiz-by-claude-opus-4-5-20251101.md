# Degrees of Innovation

Question: What distinguishes radical innovation from incremental and breakthrough innovation?

- [ ] It focuses on reducing costs and improving efficiency of existing products
- [ ] It represents a complete departure from existing practices, introducing entirely new concepts that fundamentally change how things are done
- [ ] It makes iterative changes to refine existing features and optimize performance
- [ ] It introduces novel approaches within a particular industry while building on existing foundations

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>It represents a complete departure from existing practices, introducing entirely new concepts that fundamentally change how things are done</p>
  <p>Radical innovation goes beyond incremental improvements (small enhancements) and breakthrough innovation (significant advancements within existing paradigms). It involves introducing entirely new concepts or paradigms that fundamentally transform industries, often displacing existing solutions or creating new markets. Examples include personal computers, the internet, and blockchain technology.</p>
</details>
