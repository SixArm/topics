# AliveCor KardiaMobile 6L

The AliveCor KardiaMobile 6L is a portable, medical-grade electrocardiogram (ECG) monitor that provides a six-lead heart rhythm analysis in 30 seconds. It is the first and only FDA-cleared and CE-marked six-lead personal ECG device. 

Key Features & Detections
- Six-Lead Data: Unlike smartwatches that record a single lead, the 6L records Lead I, II, III, aVR, aVL, and aVF. This provides a more detailed view for doctors to identify conditions like atrial flutter or heart block.
- Arrhythmia Detection: Instantly detects Atrial Fibrillation (AFib), Bradycardia, Tachycardia, and Normal Sinus Rhythm.
- Membership Options: While a KardiaCare membership is not required for basic use, it unlocks more advanced determinations like Sinus Rhythm with PVCs or Wide QRS.
- Clinical Trust: It is recommended by the NHS (NICE recommended) and used by cardiologists worldwide for remote monitoring.
