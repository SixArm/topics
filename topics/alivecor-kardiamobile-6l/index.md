# AliveCor KardiaMobile 6L

The AliveCor KardiaMobile 6L is a portable, medical-grade electrocardiogram (ECG) monitor that captures a six-lead heart rhythm analysis in 30 seconds. It is the first FDA-cleared and CE-marked six-lead personal ECG device, bridging the gap between consumer wearables and clinical-grade diagnostics.

Unlike smartwatch-based ECG features that record only a single lead, the KardiaMobile 6L records leads I, II, III, aVR, aVL, and aVF. This expanded lead set gives clinicians a more comprehensive electrical picture of the heart, improving the ability to identify conditions such as atrial flutter, heart block, and other rhythm abnormalities that a single-lead recording would miss.

The device provides instant on-device detection of atrial fibrillation (AFib), bradycardia, tachycardia, and normal sinus rhythm. An optional KardiaCare membership unlocks additional determinations, including sinus rhythm with premature ventricular contractions (PVCs) and wide QRS detection. Recordings sync to the Kardia smartphone app and can be shared directly with a physician for remote review.

From a clinical adoption standpoint, the KardiaMobile 6L is recommended by the UK's National Institute for Health and Care Excellence (NICE) and is used by cardiologists worldwide for remote patient monitoring. This positions it as a trusted tool in telehealth workflows, where patients capture ECGs at home and transmit them for clinical interpretation without an office visit.

For technology professionals evaluating connected health devices, the KardiaMobile 6L represents a well-validated example of edge-based medical sensing paired with cloud-connected data sharing. Its combination of regulatory clearance, multi-lead capability, and smartphone integration makes it a reference point for personal cardiac monitoring solutions.
