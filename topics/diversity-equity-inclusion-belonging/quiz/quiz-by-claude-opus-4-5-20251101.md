# Diversity, Equity, Inclusion, Belonging (DEIB)

Question: In the DEIB framework, which component specifically focuses on removing barriers that prevent individuals from having equal opportunities to succeed?

- [ ] Diversity
- [ ] Belonging
- [ ] Inclusion
- [ ] Equity

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>Equity</p>
  <p>Equity refers to the fair and just treatment of all individuals, regardless of their differences. It specifically involves identifying and removing barriers that prevent individuals from having equal opportunities to succeed. This is distinct from diversity (recognizing differences), inclusion (creating a welcoming environment), and belonging (feeling accepted and valued as part of a group).</p>
</details>
