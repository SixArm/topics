# Market discovery questions

Question: What is the PRIMARY purpose of asking "How do potential customers currently solve the problem your product/service addresses?" during market discovery?

- [ ] To copy competitor features exactly
- [ ] To identify gaps and differentiation opportunities in existing solutions
- [ ] To determine your company's internal resource allocation
- [ ] To establish your product's pricing structure

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>To identify gaps and differentiation opportunities in existing solutions</p>
  <p>Understanding how customers currently solve a problem reveals the pain points and limitations of existing solutions. This knowledge enables you to differentiate your product by addressing unmet needs and positioning your offering against alternatives. It's a foundational market discovery question because it directly informs your value proposition and competitive strategy.</p>
</details>
