# The 7 dimensions for agile product development

Question: Which dimension in the 7 dimensions for agile product development addresses non-functional requirements such as performance, security, scalability, and maintainability?

- [ ] Control
- [ ] Environment
- [ ] Quality Attribute
- [ ] Data

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>Quality Attribute</p>
  <p>The Quality Attribute dimension spans non-functional requirements such as performance, security, scalability, and maintainability. These quality attributes are woven throughout the development process, ensuring the software meets essential standards beyond just functional correctness.</p>
</details>
