# Biotech

Question: What is genetic engineering in the context of biotechnology?

- [ ] A method for producing biofuels from agricultural waste
- [ ] The manipulation of DNA in organisms to produce desired traits or behaviors
- [ ] A process for analyzing protein structures using computer simulations
- [ ] The study of how organisms interact with their environment

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>The manipulation of DNA in organisms to produce desired traits or behaviors</p>
  <p>Genetic engineering is one of the key tools in biotechnology that allows scientists to manipulate the DNA of organisms to produce desired traits or behaviors. This technique has been used to create crops resistant to herbicides and pests, as well as to produce drugs and other therapeutic agents.</p>
</details>
