# Storyboard steps

Question: What is the primary purpose of adding annotations and notes to storyboard panels?

- [ ] To replace the need for thumbnail sketches in the process
- [ ] To finalize the visual design and color palette for production
- [ ] To provide additional context or guidance for the production team
- [ ] To arrange panels in their correct sequential order

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>To provide additional context or guidance for the production team</p>
  <p>Annotations and notes are added to storyboard panels to provide additional context, instructions, or dialogue indications that help the production team understand details not captured in the visual sketch alone. This includes camera movements, character emotions, or specific actions that need to occur in each scene.</p>
</details>
