# Startup exits

Question: Which startup exit strategy involves offering company shares to the public for the first time on a stock exchange?

- [ ] Management Buyout
- [ ] Initial Public Offering (IPO)
- [ ] Secondary Market Sales
- [ ] Acquihire

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>Initial Public Offering (IPO)</p>
  <p>An IPO occurs when a startup offers its shares to the public for the first time on a stock exchange. This rigorous regulatory process enables the company to raise substantial capital for growth and expansion, and is often considered one of the most significant exit strategies for successful startups.</p>
</details>
