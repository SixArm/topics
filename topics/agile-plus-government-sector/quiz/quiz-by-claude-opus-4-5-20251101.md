# Agile + government sector

Question: Which government initiative successfully rescued the failing Healthcare.gov website in 2013 by implementing agile practices such as iterative development cycles and continuous user feedback?

- [ ] UK Government Digital Service (GDS)
- [ ] U.S. Digital Service (USDS)
- [ ] Australia's Digital Transformation Agency
- [ ] City of Boston's Digital Services Team

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>U.S. Digital Service (USDS)</p>
  <p>The U.S. Digital Service (USDS) exemplifies agile transformation in government, having successfully rescued the failing Healthcare.gov website in 2013. The team implemented iterative development cycles, continuous user feedback, and cross-functional collaboration to rebuild the platform within months rather than years, demonstrating how agile practices can rapidly address critical government technology failures.</p>
</details>
