# Agtech (Agricultural technology)

Question: What is the primary purpose of precision agriculture within agtech?

- [ ] Manipulating plant and animal DNA to produce pest-resistant crops
- [ ] Reducing labor costs by fully automating all farm operations
- [ ] Optimizing farming practices by gathering and analyzing data from sensors, drones, and satellites
- [ ] Replacing traditional soil with hydroponic growing systems

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>Optimizing farming practices by gathering and analyzing data from sensors, drones, and satellites</p>
  <p>Precision agriculture uses technology to collect data on weather, soil, and crop health through sensors, drones, and satellite imagery. This data is analyzed using machine learning algorithms to provide farmers with real-time insights and recommendations for crop management, ultimately improving yields and efficiency.</p>
</details>
