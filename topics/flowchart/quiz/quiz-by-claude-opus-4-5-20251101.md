# Flowchart

Question: In a flowchart, which symbol is used to represent a decision point or branching condition?

- [ ] Parallelogram
- [ ] Rectangle
- [ ] Diamond
- [ ] Oval

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>Diamond</p>
  <p>Diamond-shaped symbols represent decision points or branches in a flowchart. They typically contain a question or condition and have arrows pointing to different paths based on the outcome of the decision (e.g., Yes/No or True/False). This is one of the most fundamental flowchart concepts that technology professionals use when designing algorithms, business processes, and system logic.</p>
</details>
