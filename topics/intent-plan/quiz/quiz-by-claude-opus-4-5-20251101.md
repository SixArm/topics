# Intent plan

Question: What is the primary purpose of an intent plan in project management?

- [ ] To provide a rigid, unchangeable structure that must be followed exactly
- [ ] To assign blame when project milestones are missed
- [ ] To provide a clear outline of goals, objectives, and desired outcomes that guides decision-making and aligns stakeholders
- [ ] To document only the technical requirements of a software system

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>To provide a clear outline of goals, objectives, and desired outcomes that guides decision-making and aligns stakeholders</p>
  <p>An intent plan serves as a roadmap that describes intentions and goals for a project or initiative. Its core purpose is to ensure everyone involved understands what is expected and what they are working towards, while remaining flexible enough to allow adjustments as circumstances change.</p>
</details>
