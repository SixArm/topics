# Small business loan

Question: Which type of small business loan is backed by a government agency and typically offers easier qualification requirements, competitive interest rates, and longer repayment terms?

- [ ] Lines of credit
- [ ] SBA loans
- [ ] Unsecured term loans
- [ ] Secured term loans

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>SBA loans</p>
  <p>SBA (Small Business Administration) loans are backed by the Small Business Administration, a government agency that helps small businesses access funding. These loans are often easier to qualify for than traditional bank loans and offer competitive interest rates with longer repayment terms, making them particularly attractive for technology startups and small businesses that may not yet have extensive credit histories or substantial collateral.</p>
</details>
