# Sequoia Capital

Question: What is Sequoia Capital primarily known for in the venture capital industry?

- [ ] Focusing exclusively on late-stage companies preparing for IPO
- [ ] Specializing in leveraged buyouts of established corporations
- [ ] Identifying and investing in promising early-stage startups
- [ ] Providing only debt financing to technology companies

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>Identifying and investing in promising early-stage startups</p>
  <p>Sequoia Capital has a strong track record of focusing on early-stage startups, typically investing at the seed, early, and growth stages. Founded in 1972, the firm has successfully identified and helped grow companies like Apple, Google, Oracle, Airbnb, Zoom, and Instacart, establishing its reputation as one of the most successful venture capital firms in the industry.</p>
</details>
