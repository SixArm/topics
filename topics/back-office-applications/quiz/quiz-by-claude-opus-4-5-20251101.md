# Back-office applications

Question: What is the primary distinguishing characteristic of back-office applications compared to front-office applications?

- [ ] They generate revenue directly through customer transactions
- [ ] They are designed for external customer interactions
- [ ] They handle internal operations and administrative tasks
- [ ] They focus primarily on marketing and sales automation

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>They handle internal operations and administrative tasks</p>
  <p>Back-office applications are internal-facing software systems used by employees within an organization to manage administrative functions such as data processing, workflow automation, HR, supply chain management, and enterprise resource planning. In contrast, front-office applications focus on customer interactions and revenue generation.</p>
</details>
