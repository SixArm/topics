# 500 Startups

Question: What is the distinctive investment strategy that 500 Startups uses to build its portfolio?

- [ ] Investing exclusively in Series A rounds with amounts between $1 million and $5 million
- [ ] Making large investments of $500,000+ in a small number of carefully selected companies
- [ ] Investing small amounts ($50,000-$150,000) in a large number of companies to diversify the portfolio
- [ ] Focusing solely on U.S.-based companies in the consumer software sector

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>Investing small amounts ($50,000-$150,000) in a large number of companies to diversify the portfolio</p>
  <p>500 Startups has a unique investment strategy that involves investing in a large number of companies with small amounts of capital, typically between $50,000 and $150,000. This approach allows them to diversify their portfolio and support a large number of companies at different stages of development, having made over 2,500 investments across more than 75 countries.</p>
</details>
