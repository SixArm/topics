# Minimum Learnable Product (MLnP)

Question: What is the primary distinguishing focus of a Minimum Learnable Product (MLnP) compared to other minimum product approaches?

- [ ] Creating emotionally engaging features that users love
- [ ] Testing product ideas with the smallest feature set possible
- [ ] Maximizing revenue through premium pricing strategies
- [ ] Helping users develop skills and knowledge to use the product effectively

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>Helping users develop skills and knowledge to use the product effectively</p>
  <p>While MVP focuses on testing product ideas with minimal features and MLP emphasizes emotional engagement, MLnP specifically emphasizes education and skill development. It creates a product that not only satisfies users' immediate needs but also guides them through learning how to use the product effectively through tutorials, tips, and thoughtful design that supports the learning process.</p>
</details>
