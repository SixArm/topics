# AI + cleantech

Question: Which AI application in cleantech analyzes consumption patterns, weather data, and renewable sourcing to optimize grid stability and distribution?

- [ ] Waste Management
- [ ] Energy Management
- [ ] Sustainable Agriculture
- [ ] Environmental Monitoring

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>Energy Management</p>
  <p>Energy Management is the AI + cleantech application that analyzes energy consumption patterns, weather data, renewable sourcing, and other factors to optimize energy generation, grid stability, storage and distribution, and consumption. This is foundational to cleantech because efficient energy systems underpin most other sustainability efforts.</p>
</details>
