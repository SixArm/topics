# Chief Human Resources Officer (CHRO)

Question: What is the primary reporting relationship for a Chief Human Resources Officer (CHRO) in most organizations?

- [ ] Reports to the Chief Marketing Officer (CMO)
- [ ] Reports to the Board of Directors only
- [ ] Reports directly to the CEO or COO
- [ ] Reports to the Chief Financial Officer (CFO)

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>Reports directly to the CEO or COO</p>
  <p>The CHRO is a high-ranking executive who typically reports directly to the CEO or COO, reflecting the strategic importance of human resources in organizational leadership. This reporting structure ensures that HR strategy is aligned with overall business strategy and that the CHRO has a seat at the executive table for key organizational decisions.</p>
</details>
