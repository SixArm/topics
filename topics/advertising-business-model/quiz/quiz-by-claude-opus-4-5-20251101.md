# Advertising business model

Question: What is the primary way a company using an advertising business model generates revenue?

- [ ] Charging users a subscription fee for premium features
- [ ] Selling advertising space or time to other companies
- [ ] Taking a commission on transactions between buyers and sellers
- [ ] Licensing proprietary technology to third parties

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>Selling advertising space or time to other companies</p>
  <p>In the advertising business model, a company's primary revenue comes from selling advertising space or time to other companies. The company focuses on creating content or services that attract a large audience or user base, which can then be monetized through various ad formats such as banner ads, sponsored content, or video ads.</p>
</details>
