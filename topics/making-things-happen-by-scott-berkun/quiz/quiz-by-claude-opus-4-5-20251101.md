# Making Things Happen by Scott Berkun

Question: According to Scott Berkun's "Making Things Happen," what is emphasized as essential during the project initiation phase?

- [ ] Immediately assigning tasks to team members
- [ ] Focusing primarily on budget constraints
- [ ] Establishing clear project vision and objectives
- [ ] Documenting all potential risks

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>Establishing clear project vision and objectives</p>
  <p>Berkun emphasizes that successful project initiation requires defining clear project goals, scope, and stakeholders. Having a well-articulated project vision and objectives provides direction and alignment for the entire team, serving as a foundation for all subsequent planning and execution activities.</p>
</details>
