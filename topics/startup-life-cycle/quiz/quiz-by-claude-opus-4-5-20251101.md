# Startup life cycle

Question: Which stage of the startup life cycle focuses on achieving product-market fit through research, experimentation, and iteration while operating on a minimal budget?

- [ ] Efficiency
- [ ] Discovery
- [ ] Scale
- [ ] Harvest

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>Discovery</p>
  <p>The Discovery stage is the first phase of the startup life cycle where founders identify market opportunities, validate business concepts, and iterate on ideas to find product-market fit. This stage is characterized by a small team working on a shoestring budget while conducting research and experiments.</p>
</details>
