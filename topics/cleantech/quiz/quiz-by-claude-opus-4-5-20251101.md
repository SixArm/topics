# Cleantech

Question: What is the primary goal of cleantech (clean technology)?

- [ ] Maximizing shareholder returns through cost reduction
- [ ] Replacing all human workers with automated systems
- [ ] Reducing environmental impact while meeting demand for energy and resources
- [ ] Increasing production speed regardless of environmental effects

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>Reducing environmental impact while meeting demand for energy and resources</p>
  <p>Cleantech aims to reduce the impact of human activities on the environment while meeting the growing demand for energy and natural resources. This is achieved through innovative technologies, products, and services that contribute to environmental sustainability, energy efficiency, and waste reduction across industries including energy production, transportation, manufacturing, agriculture, and construction.</p>
</details>
