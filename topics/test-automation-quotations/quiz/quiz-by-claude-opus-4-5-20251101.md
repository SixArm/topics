# Test automation quotations

Question: According to best practices in test automation, what is the primary purpose of automation in software testing?

- [ ] To completely replace human testers and eliminate manual testing costs
- [ ] To automate every possible test case as quickly as possible
- [ ] To amplify human intelligence by freeing testers to focus on higher-value activities like exploratory testing
- [ ] To reduce the need for skilled testers on the development team

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>To amplify human intelligence by freeing testers to focus on higher-value activities like exploratory testing</p>
  <p>The quotation states that "the goal of automation is not to replace human intelligence, but to amplify it." Automation handles repetitive tasks while humans tackle complex scenarios requiring intuition and creativity.
