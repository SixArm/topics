# AI Agent

Question: What distinguishes an AI agent from a traditional software program?

- [ ] It can only process pre-programmed responses without learning
- [ ] It operates autonomously, perceives its environment, reasons about data, and works toward specific goals
- [ ] It must be connected to the internet at all times

  <p>AI agents are defined by their combination of autonomy, sensing capabilities, reasoning mechanisms, learning abilities, and goal-directed behavior.
