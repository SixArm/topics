# Strategic Balanced Scorecard

Question: What are the four perspectives that the Strategic Balanced Scorecard (SBS) framework uses to measure and manage organizational performance?

- [ ] Financial, Customer, Marketing, Human Resources
- [ ] Financial, Customer, Internal Business Processes, Learning and Growth
- [ ] Revenue, Operations, Technology, Employee Engagement
- [ ] Profitability, Market Share, Quality Control, Innovation

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>Financial, Customer, Internal Business Processes, Learning and Growth</p>
  <p>The Strategic Balanced Scorecard framework measures performance across four specific perspectives: Financial (revenue, ROI, cash flow), Customer (satisfaction, retention, loyalty), Internal Business Processes (cycle times, defect rates, efficiency), and Learning and Growth (employee satisfaction, innovation, training). These four perspectives work together to align strategy, objectives, and metrics across the organization.</p>
</details>
