# Pre-money valuation and post-money valuation

Question: If a startup has a pre-money valuation of $10 million and receives a $5 million investment, what percentage of the company will the investor own?

- [ ] 50%
- [ ] 25%
- [ ] Approximately 33%
- [ ] 10%

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>Approximately 33%</p>
  <p>The investor's ownership percentage is calculated by dividing their investment by the post-money valuation. The post-money valuation equals the pre-money valuation plus the investment ($10 million + $5 million = $15 million). Therefore, the investor owns $5 million รท $15 million = 1/3, which is approximately 33%.</p>
</details>
