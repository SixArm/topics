# See things in the present, even if they are in the future

Question: What is the core leadership principle conveyed by Larry Ellison's quote "See things in the present, even if they are in the future"?

- [ ] Leaders should have visionary foresight while maintaining clear understanding of present realities to proactively shape the future
- [ ] Leaders should avoid taking risks and maintain the status quo to ensure organizational stability
