# Control assessment

Question: What is the primary purpose of control assessment in an organization?

- [ ] To develop new software applications for the organization
- [ ] To evaluate the effectiveness of internal controls that mitigate risks, ensure compliance, safeguard assets, and achieve objectives
- [ ] To hire and train new employees for the compliance department
- [ ] To create marketing strategies for new products

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>To evaluate the effectiveness of internal controls that mitigate risks, ensure compliance, safeguard assets, and achieve objectives</p>
  <p>Control assessment is the process of evaluating and assessing the effectiveness of internal controls within an organization. These internal controls are policies, procedures, and mechanisms designed to mitigate risks, ensure compliance with regulations, safeguard assets, and achieve organizational objectives. This is the fundamental definition and purpose of control assessment.</p>
</details>
