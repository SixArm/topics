# Go-to-market strategy

Question: What is the primary purpose of a go-to-market (GTM) strategy?

- [ ] To create technical documentation for product features
- [ ] To define how a business will bring a product to market and achieve commercial success
- [ ] To manage internal team communication during development
- [ ] To establish employee onboarding procedures

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>To define how a business will bring a product to market and achieve commercial success</p>
  <p>A GTM strategy is a comprehensive plan that outlines the target market, customer segments, marketing channels, pricing, sales and distribution strategy, and competitive positioning. It creates a roadmap to help the company achieve business goals including revenue growth, customer acquisition, and market share.</p>
</details>
