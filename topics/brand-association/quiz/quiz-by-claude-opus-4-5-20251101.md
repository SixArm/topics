# Brand association

Question: What is the primary purpose of brand association in marketing strategy?

- [ ] To reduce marketing costs by eliminating the need for advertising campaigns
- [ ] To create mental connections that differentiate a brand and build customer loyalty
- [ ] To ensure all competitors use similar branding elements
- [ ] To standardize product pricing across different market segments

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>To create mental connections that differentiate a brand and build customer loyalty</p>
  <p>Brand association refers to the mental connections consumers form with a brand through its name, logo, messaging, and experiences. Positive associations—such as quality, innovation, or reliability—help differentiate a brand from competitors and foster customer loyalty. For technology professionals, understanding brand association is essential for product positioning, user experience design, and building trust with customers in competitive markets.</p>
</details>
