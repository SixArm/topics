# Values Statement Examples

Question: What is the primary purpose of a company values statement?

- [ ] To establish financial performance targets and revenue goals
- [ ] To outline the technical specifications of products and services
- [ ] To communicate the core beliefs and principles that guide a company's culture and decisions
- [ ] To define the organizational hierarchy and reporting structure

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>To communicate the core beliefs and principles that guide a company's culture and decisions</p>
  <p>A values statement articulates what a company stands for and what principles guide its behavior. As shown in the examples, Starbucks emphasizes inclusivity and belonging, Patagonia focuses on environmental responsibility, and Google prioritizes user-centricity. These statements shape company culture, inform decision-making, and help stakeholders understand what the organization considers most important beyond financial metrics.</p>
</details>
