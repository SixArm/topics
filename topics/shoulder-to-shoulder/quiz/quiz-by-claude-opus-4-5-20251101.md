# Shoulder to shoulder

Question: What does the idiom "shoulder to shoulder" primarily emphasize in a professional context?

- [ ] A hierarchical reporting structure where managers supervise employees directly
- [ ] The importance of collaboration, unity, and mutual assistance toward a common purpose
- [ ] Physical workspace arrangements requiring adjacent desk placement
- [ ] A competitive approach where team members work independently on parallel tasks

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>The importance of collaboration, unity, and mutual assistance toward a common purpose</p>
  <p>The idiom "shoulder to shoulder" conveys unity, collaboration, and mutual support. It symbolizes camaraderie and partnership where individuals work closely together, support each other, and face challenges as a united front. The phrase highlights the strength that comes from working in harmony toward shared objectives.</p>
</details>
