# Technical Assistance Agreement (TAA)

Question: What is the primary regulatory body that oversees Technical Assistance Agreements (TAAs) for defense-related technical data?

- [ ] U.S. Department of Commerce, Bureau of Industry and Security (BIS)
- [ ] U.S. Department of State, Directorate of Defense Trade Controls (DDTC)
- [ ] U.S. Department of Defense, Defense Contract Management Agency (DCMA)
- [ ] U.S. International Trade Commission (ITC)

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>U.S. Department of State, Directorate of Defense Trade Controls (DDTC)</p>
  <p>The TAA is regulated by the U.S. Department of State, Directorate of Defense Trade Controls (DDTC) under the International Traffic in Arms Regulations (ITAR). The DDTC oversees the export and temporary import of defense articles, defense services, and related technical data between U.S. companies and foreign entities.</p>
</details>
