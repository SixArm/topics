# How to find startup recruiters

Question: What is considered one of the most effective strategies for finding recruiters with proven startup experience?

- [ ] Posting exclusively on general job boards like Indeed or Monster
- [ ] Relying solely on cold outreach to recruiters with no prior connections
- [ ] Asking for referrals from current employees or professionals in your network
- [ ] Waiting for recruiters to contact you after launching your product

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>Asking for referrals from current employees or professionals in your network</p>
  <p>Referrals from trusted connections are an effective way to find recruiters who have verified experience working with startups. Your network can provide firsthand recommendations based on actual working relationships, helping you identify recruiters who understand the unique hiring challenges and culture of startup environments.</p>
</details>
