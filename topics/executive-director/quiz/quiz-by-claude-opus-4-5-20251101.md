# Executive Director

Question: To whom does an Executive Director (ED) report in an organization?

- [ ] The Chief Technology Officer
- [ ] The Human Resources department
- [ ] The board of directors
- [ ] The marketing team

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>The board of directors</p>
  <p>The Executive Director is a senior leadership position responsible for the overall strategic direction and operations of an organization, and they report directly to the board of directors. This reporting relationship is fundamental to understanding the ED's role, as the board provides governance oversight while the ED handles day-to-day leadership and strategy implementation.</p>
</details>
