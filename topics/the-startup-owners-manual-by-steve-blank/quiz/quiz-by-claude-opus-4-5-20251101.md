# The Startup Owner's Manual by Steve Blank

Question: What is the foundational methodology that "The Startup Owner's Manual" is based on, which emphasizes validating business ideas through direct customer engagement before building products?

- [ ] Six Sigma methodology
- [ ] Customer Development and Lean Startup methodology
- [ ] Waterfall project management
- [ ] Agile Scrum framework

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>Customer Development and Lean Startup methodology</p>
  <p>The book is based on Steve Blank's Lean Startup methodology and emphasizes customer development as its core principle. This approach teaches entrepreneurs to validate their business ideas by identifying target customers and understanding their needs before investing heavily in product development, rather than following traditional approaches that build products first and seek customers later.</p>
</details>
