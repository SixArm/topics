# Startup Business Guide Conclusion

Question: According to the Startup Business Guide, what is the most effective way to contribute feedback and suggestions to improve the guide?

- [ ] Send feedback through LinkedIn direct messages
- [ ] Post comments on the associated blog
- [ ] Open an issue on the GitHub repository
- [ ] Submit feedback through the official mobile app

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>Open an issue on the GitHub repository</p>
  <p>The guide explicitly states that you can open any issue on the repository at github.com/sixarm/startup-business-guide to ask questions, suggest improvements, point out errors, and provide other feedback. This open-source approach to collecting feedback allows for transparent, trackable, and collaborative improvement of the guide.</p>
</details>
