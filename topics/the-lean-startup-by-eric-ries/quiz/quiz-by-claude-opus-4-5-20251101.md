# The Lean Startup by Eric Ries

Question: What are the three primary components of The Lean Startup methodology?

- [ ] Market analysis, competitive positioning, and revenue optimization
- [ ] Product development, marketing strategy, and financial planning
- [ ] Validated learning, continuous innovation, and a process of testing and iteration
- [ ] Customer segmentation, brand building, and sales funnel management

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>Validated learning, continuous innovation, and a process of testing and iteration</p>
  <p>The Lean Startup methodology by Eric Ries consists of three primary components: validated learning (using data and feedback to make decisions), continuous innovation (focusing on customer needs and being willing to pivot), and a process of testing and iteration (creating feedback loops with minimum viable products to quickly test and validate new ideas).</p>
</details>
