# Agile and mindfulness

Question: What kind of thinking does Agile especially encourage through mindfulness practices?

- [ ] Hierarchical, directive, and process-heavy thinking
- [ ] Open, collaborative, and innovative thinking
- [ ] Risk-averse, predictable, and siloed thinking
- [ ] Individual, competitive, and efficiency-focused thinking

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>Open, collaborative, and innovative thinking</p>
  <p>Agile is a way of working that especially encourages mindfulness through ways of thinking that are open, collaborative, and innovative. This approach helps teams adapt to change, work together effectively, and generate creative solutions, even though the process can initially feel chaotic or confusing to those new to agile methodologies.</p>
</details>
