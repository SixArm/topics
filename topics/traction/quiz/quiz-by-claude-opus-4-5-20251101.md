# Traction

Question: Why is traction considered a critical metric for startup businesses?

- [ ] It determines the legal structure of the company
- [ ] It measures how fast the development team can write code
- [ ] It serves as a proxy for business viability and is key to attracting investors, media attention, and talent
- [ ] It calculates the total number of employees needed for operations

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>It serves as a proxy for business viability and is key to attracting investors, media attention, and talent</p>
  <p>Traction demonstrates the measurable progress a company has made toward its business goals, including customer interest, product-market fit, and revenue. Investors use traction as a key metric to determine whether or not to invest, and it also helps startups gain media attention and recruit top talent.</p>
</details>
