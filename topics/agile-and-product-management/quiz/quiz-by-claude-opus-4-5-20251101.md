# Agile and product management

Question: What is the primary role of a product manager in an agile environment?

- [ ] Creating detailed project timelines and Gantt charts for the entire development cycle
- [ ] Writing code and performing quality assurance testing alongside developers
- [ ] Serving as a bridge between stakeholders and development teams while maintaining and prioritizing the product backlog
- [ ] Managing employee performance reviews and team hiring decisions

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>Serving as a bridge between stakeholders and development teams while maintaining and prioritizing the product backlog</p>
  <p>In agile environments, product managers act as the crucial link between business stakeholders and technical teams. Their core responsibilities include maintaining the product backlog, prioritizing features based on business value, defining user stories with acceptance criteria, and ensuring alignment with business objectives. This differs from traditional project management roles that focus more on scheduling and resource allocation.</p>
</details>
