Question: What is the primary investment focus of Bethnal Green Ventures (BGV)?

- [ ] Enterprise software solutions for large corporations
- [ ] Cryptocurrency and blockchain technology startups
- [ ] Startups addressing societal and environmental challenges using technology
- [ ] Consumer entertainment and gaming applications

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>

  <p>BGV invests in startups that address societal and environmental challenges using technology-driven solutions. The fund has a broad impact focus, encompassing areas such as health, education, climate change, sustainability, civic engagement, and social justice. This impact-first approach distinguishes BGV from traditional venture capital funds.</p>
