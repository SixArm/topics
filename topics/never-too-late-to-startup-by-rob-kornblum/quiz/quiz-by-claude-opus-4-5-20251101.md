# Never Too Late to Startup by Rob Kornblum

Question: According to Rob Kornblum, what is the key mindset that aspiring entrepreneurs should adopt regardless of their age?

- [ ] Waiting for the perfect business opportunity before taking action
- [ ] Focusing exclusively on industries where they have prior experience
- [ ] Taking action and pursuing opportunities rather than waiting for perfect timing
- [ ] Starting only when they have secured complete funding

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>Taking action and pursuing opportunities rather than waiting for perfect timing</p>
  <p>Kornblum emphasizes that entrepreneurship is a mindset and that it is never too late to develop it. He stresses the importance of taking action and pursuing opportunities rather than waiting for the perfect moment or opportunity to present itself. He encourages readers to be open to new ideas and seek out opportunities for learning and growth.</p>
</details>
