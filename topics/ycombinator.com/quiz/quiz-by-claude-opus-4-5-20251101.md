# Y Combinator (YC)

Question: What is Y Combinator (YC) primarily known for in the startup ecosystem?

- [ ] A venture capital firm that only invests in late-stage companies
- [ ] A startup accelerator that provides seed funding, mentorship, and resources to early-stage companies
- [ ] A coworking space rental service for technology companies
- [ ] A recruitment platform connecting startups with software engineers

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>A startup accelerator that provides seed funding, mentorship, and resources to early-stage companies</p>
  <p>Y Combinator is a startup accelerator founded in 2005 that runs a three-month program providing early-stage companies with seed funding, office space, mentorship, and access to advisors and investors. The program culminates in a demo day where startups pitch to investors. YC has helped launch notable companies including Airbnb, Dropbox, Reddit, and Stripe.</p>
</details>
