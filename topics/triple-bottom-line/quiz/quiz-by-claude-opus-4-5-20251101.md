# Triple Bottom Line (TBL)

Question: What are the three aspects of performance that the Triple Bottom Line (TBL) framework measures?

- [ ] Revenue, expenses, and net income
- [ ] People, planet, and profit
- [ ] Quality, cost, and delivery
- [ ] Strategy, operations, and technology

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>People, planet, and profit</p>
  <p>The Triple Bottom Line framework expands traditional business metrics beyond just financial performance to include three dimensions: social impact (people), environmental impact (planet), and financial success (profit). This approach encourages businesses to create positive social and environmental outcomes while also achieving financial sustainability.</p>
</details>
