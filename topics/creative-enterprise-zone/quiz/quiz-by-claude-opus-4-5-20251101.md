# Creative Enterprise Zone

Question: What is the primary purpose of a Creative Enterprise Zone (CEZ)?

- [ ] To restrict creative businesses to specific urban areas for regulatory compliance
- [ ] To provide tax exemptions exclusively for technology startups
- [ ] To stimulate economic development, innovation, and collaboration within the creative industries
- [ ] To replace traditional business districts with artist-only residential communities

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>To stimulate economic development, innovation, and collaboration within the creative industries</p>
  <p>A Creative Enterprise Zone is a strategic initiative designed to support and foster growth in creative industries by bringing together creative businesses, artists, cultural organizations, and stakeholders. Its core purpose is stimulating economic development and innovation while creating a collaborative ecosystem that nurtures sectors like visual arts, design, digital media, film production, and performing arts.</p>
</details>
