# SPADE decision framework

Question: What do the letters in the SPADE decision framework stand for?

- [ ] Strategy, Plan, Action, Development, Evaluation
- [ ] Situation, Problem, Analysis, Decision, Execution
- [ ] Scope, Priority, Assessment, Deployment, Enhancement
- [ ] Survey, Prioritize, Analyze, Decide, Evaluate

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>Situation, Problem, Analysis, Decision, Execution</p>
  <p>The SPADE framework guides decision-makers through five sequential steps: defining the Situation (context), identifying the Problem, conducting Analysis of potential solutions, making the Decision by selecting the best option, and finally Execution by implementing and monitoring the chosen solution.</p>
</details>
