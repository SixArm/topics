# Eureka!

Question: What does the exclamation "Eureka!" signify in the context of innovation and problem-solving?

- [ ] A formal scientific methodology for testing hypotheses
- [ ] A moment of sudden discovery or breakthrough where a solution becomes clear
- [ ] A technique for collaborative brainstorming sessions
- [ ] The final step in a structured design thinking process

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>A moment of sudden discovery or breakthrough where a solution becomes clear</p>
  <p>"Eureka!" originates from the Greek word "heurÄ“ka" meaning "I have found it," famously attributed to Archimedes when he discovered the principle of buoyancy. In modern usage, it represents that "aha!" moment of inspiration where a previously elusive solution or understanding suddenly becomes apparent, bringing a sense of excitement and triumph in innovation, scientific discovery, and creative thinking.</p>
