# Partnership metrics

Question: Which category of partnership metrics would include Net Promoter Score (NPS) and referral rates?

- [ ] Financial metrics
- [ ] Customer metrics
- [ ] Innovation metrics
- [ ] Operational metrics

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>Customer metrics</p>
  <p>Customer metrics measure how the partnership impacts customers and includes indicators such as customer satisfaction levels, referral rates, and Net Promoter Score (NPS). These metrics help partners understand whether their collaboration is delivering value to end customers and driving loyalty.</p>
</details>
