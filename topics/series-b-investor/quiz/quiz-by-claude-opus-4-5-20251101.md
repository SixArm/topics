# Series B investor

Question: What is the primary purpose of Series B funding for a startup?

- [ ] To provide initial capital for developing a minimum viable product
- [ ] To scale the business, expand operations, and accelerate growth
- [ ] To fund the company's exit strategy through acquisition or IPO
- [ ] To compensate founders and early employees through stock buybacks

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>To scale the business, expand operations, and accelerate growth</p>
  <p>Series B funding occurs after a company has already proven its business model and gained market traction through seed and Series A rounds.
