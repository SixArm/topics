# Grants Manager

Question: What is the primary responsibility of a grants manager after a grant is awarded to an organization?

- [ ] Developing new grant proposal templates for future applications
- [ ] Recruiting additional program staff to expand grant capacity
- [ ] Ensuring monitoring, reporting, and compliance with all grant terms and conditions
- [ ] Negotiating higher funding amounts with the granting organization

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>Ensuring monitoring, reporting, and compliance with all grant terms and conditions</p>
  <p>Once grants are awarded, the grants manager's key responsibility shifts to grant administration, which includes monitoring progress, ensuring compliance with all grant terms and conditions, tracking outputs and outcomes, and handling required reporting. This ensures the organization fulfills its obligations to the funder and maintains eligibility for future funding.</p>
</details>
