# Business model

Question: What is the primary purpose of a business model?

- [ ] To create detailed financial projections for investors
- [ ] To outline how a company generates revenue by describing target customers, value proposition, and key activities
- [ ] To define the organizational hierarchy and reporting structure
- [ ] To document technical architecture and system requirements

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>To outline how a company generates revenue by describing target customers, value proposition, and key activities</p>
  <p>A business model is a framework that describes how a business creates, delivers, and captures value. It includes key components such as target customer segment, value proposition, revenue streams, cost structure, and the key activities, resources, and partnerships needed to make the business work. A well-defined business model helps a company understand its market position, identify revenue streams, and allocate resources effectively.</p>
</details>
