# Thinking Hats

Question: In the Thinking Hats technique, which hat should a team member wear when identifying potential risks, obstacles, and problems with a proposed solution?

- [ ] Green Hat
- [ ] Black Hat
- [ ] Yellow Hat
- [ ] White Hat

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>Black Hat</p>
  <p>The Black Hat represents critical thinking and is used to focus on risks, potential problems, and weaknesses associated with a decision or solution. This is essential for technology professionals who need to evaluate proposals for security vulnerabilities, scalability issues, or implementation challenges before committing resources.</p>
</details>
