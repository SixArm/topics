Question: According to research on startup failures, what is widely considered the most common reason startup businesses fail?

- [ ] Failure to comply with regulatory requirements
- [ ] Poor timing due to economic downturns
- [ ] Lack of market demand for the product or service
- [ ] Insufficient funding and capital

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>Lack of market demand for the product or service</p>
  <p>While all these factors contribute to startup failures, lack of market demand is consistently cited as the number one reason startups fail. Building a product that customers don't actually want or aren't willing to pay for means the business cannot generate sustainable revenue, regardless of how well-funded or well-managed it may be. This is why customer discovery and market validation are emphasized as critical early-stage activities.</p>
</details>
