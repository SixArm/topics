# Customer discovery

Question: What is the primary purpose of customer discovery in a startup context?

- [ ] To create marketing materials that appeal to the widest possible audience
- [ ] To validate and refine a business idea by understanding potential customers' needs, preferences, and pain points
- [ ] To determine the optimal pricing strategy for maximum profit margins
- [ ] To identify competitors and analyze their market positioning

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>To validate and refine a business idea by understanding potential customers' needs, preferences, and pain points</p>
  <p>Customer discovery is fundamentally about engaging with potential customers to understand their needs and gather feedback on a product or service. This process helps startups create products that people will actually use and pay for, which is essential for business success. The process involves identifying target segments, developing hypotheses about customer needs, engaging with customers to validate those hypotheses, and iterating based on feedback.</p>
</details>
