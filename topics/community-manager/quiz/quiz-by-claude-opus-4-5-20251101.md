# Community Manager

Question: What is the primary function of a community manager in a technology company?

- [ ] Writing source code for the company's software products
- [ ] Managing payroll and employee benefits administration
- [ ] Building and managing relationships between a company and its community or audience
- [ ] Conducting financial audits and regulatory compliance reviews

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>Building and managing relationships between a company and its community or audience</p>
  <p>A community manager is responsible for building and managing relationships with a specific community or audience. They serve as a bridge between the company and its community members, engaging in discussions, fostering connections, providing customer support, managing content, and analyzing community metrics to improve engagement and experiences.</p>
</details>
