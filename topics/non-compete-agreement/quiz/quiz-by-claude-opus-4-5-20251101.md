# Non-compete agreement

Question: What is the primary purpose of a non-compete agreement between an employer and employee?

- [ ] To guarantee the employee receives a severance package upon termination
- [ ] To protect the company's business interests by restricting the employee's ability to work for competitors or start competing businesses
- [ ] To establish the employee's salary and benefits package
- [ ] To grant the employee ownership rights to intellectual property created during employment

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>To protect the company's business interests by restricting the employee's ability to work for competitors or start competing businesses</p>
