# Big picture

Question: What is the primary benefit of considering the "big picture" when making decisions in a technology project?

- [ ] It helps identify the most detailed technical specifications required
- [ ] It allows decisions that align with long-term goals and broader context
- [ ] It speeds up the immediate implementation of features
- [ ] It reduces the need for stakeholder communication

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>It allows decisions that align with long-term goals and broader context</p>
  <p>Considering the big picture helps individuals make informed decisions by taking into account the larger context, understanding relationships between components, and ensuring choices have a positive long-term impact rather than only addressing immediate concerns.</p>
</details>
