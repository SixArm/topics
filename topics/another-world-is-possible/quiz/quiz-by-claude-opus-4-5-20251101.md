# Another World Is Possible

Question: According to Geoff Mulgan's book "Another World Is Possible," what is identified as the primary catalyst needed to create a more just and sustainable world?

- [ ] Strict government regulations and top-down policy enforcement
- [ ] Reigniting social and political imagination through creativity and collective action
- [ ] Technological advancement and digital transformation alone
- [ ] Economic growth through traditional market-based models

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>Reigniting social and political imagination through creativity and collective action</p>
  <p>Mulgan emphasizes that existing models and systems are inadequate to address complex contemporary issues. He argues that reigniting social and political imagination—through creativity, innovation, and collective action—serves as the essential catalyst for meaningful change. This approach encourages questioning prevailing assumptions, citizen participation, grassroots movements, and new models of governance and social innovation.</p>
</details>
