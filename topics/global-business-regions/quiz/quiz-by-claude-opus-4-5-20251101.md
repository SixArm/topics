# Global business regions

Question: What are the three primary global business regions commonly used to categorize international markets?

- [ ] NAFTA, EU, and ASEAN
- [ ] North, South, and Central
- [ ] AMERS, EMEA, and APAC
- [ ] Western, Eastern, and Pacific

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>AMERS, EMEA, and APAC</p>
  <p>The three widely recognized global business regions are AMERS (The Americas), EMEA (Europe, Middle East, Africa), and APAC (Asia-Pacific). These abbreviations are standard in international business for grouping countries with similar economic characteristics, business practices, and market dynamics, helping companies tailor strategies and understand regional market trends.</p>
</details>
