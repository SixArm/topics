# Agile + sectors

Question: Which of the following sectors is commonly cited as an example of applying agile methodologies beyond software development?

- [ ] Hospitality sector
- [ ] Real estate sector
- [ ] Healthcare sector
- [ ] Mining sector

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>Healthcare sector</p>
  <p>Healthcare is one of the key industry sectors where agile methodologies have been successfully applied. Along with education, financial, government, and manufacturing sectors, healthcare demonstrates how agile principles can improve processes, patient care, and organizational responsiveness in non-software contexts.</p>
</details>
