A theme chooser is a UI/UX component that enables users to switch between visual
themes—most commonly light and dark modes—to customize the appearance of an
application or website. It enhances user comfort, accessibility, and personal
preference by allowing users to select a theme that suits their lighting
environment or aesthetic taste. Light themes typically feature brighter
backgrounds with dark text for daytime use, while dark themes use darker
backgrounds with light text to reduce eye strain in low-light conditions. The
theme chooser is often implemented as a toggle switch or dropdown and
contributes to a more flexible and user-friendly interface.
