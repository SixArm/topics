# Legtech (Legal technology)

Question: What is the primary purpose of legtech in the legal industry?

- [ ] To replace lawyers with artificial intelligence systems
- [ ] To streamline, automate, and improve legal services
- [ ] To eliminate the need for legal research databases
- [ ] To increase billable hours for law firms

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>To streamline, automate, and improve legal services</p>
  <p>Legtech refers to the use of technology to streamline, automate, and improve legal services. It helps law firms and legal departments increase efficiency, reduce costs, and improve the quality of legal services through tools for case management, legal research, document automation, contract management, e-discovery, and predictive analytics.</p>
</details>
