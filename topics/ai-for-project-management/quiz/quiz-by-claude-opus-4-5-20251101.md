# AI for project management

Question: What is a key capability of AI in project management that helps managers make informed decisions by analyzing cost, schedule, resource availability, and risk assessments?

- [ ] Intelligent Document Management
- [ ] Natural Language Processing
- [ ] Decision Support
- [ ] Continuous Learning and Improvement

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>Decision Support</p>
  <p>AI provides data-driven decision support and forecasting by analyzing various project factors such as cost, schedule, resource availability, and risk assessments. This capability assists project managers in making informed decisions and optimizing project outcomes, which is central to effective project management.</p>
</details>
