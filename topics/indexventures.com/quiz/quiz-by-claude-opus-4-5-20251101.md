# Index Ventures

Question: What type of support does Index Ventures provide to its portfolio companies beyond financial investment?

- [ ] Only legal and regulatory compliance services
- [ ] Exclusively marketing and advertising campaigns
- [ ] Support in fundraising, marketing, product development, hiring, and access to a network of entrepreneurs, executives, and investors
- [ ] Limited to office space and administrative services

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>Support in fundraising, marketing, product development, hiring, and access to a network of entrepreneurs, executives, and investors</p>
  <p>Index Ventures provides comprehensive support beyond capital, including assistance with fundraising, marketing, product development, and hiring. Additionally, the firm has established a network of entrepreneurs, executives, and investors that portfolio companies can access for valuable connections, advice, and mentorship to help navigate the challenges of growing and scaling their businesses.</p>
</details>
