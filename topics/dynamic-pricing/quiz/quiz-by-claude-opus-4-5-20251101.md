# Dynamic pricing

Question: What is the primary goal of dynamic pricing as a business strategy?

- [ ] To maintain consistent prices across all customer segments
- [ ] To undercut competitors by always offering the lowest price
- [ ] To maximize revenue and profit by charging the optimal price for each unit sold
- [ ] To simplify pricing operations by using fixed seasonal rates

  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>To maximize revenue and profit by charging the optimal price for each unit sold</p>
