# Fractional consulting

Question: What is the primary advantage of fractional consulting for startups and small businesses?

- [ ] It guarantees permanent access to executive-level talent
- [ ] It provides access to high-level expertise without the full cost of a full-time executive
- [ ] It requires executives to work on-site at company offices
- [ ] It eliminates the need to evaluate consultant qualifications

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>It provides access to high-level expertise without the full cost of a full-time executive</p>
  <p>Fractional consulting allows companies to gain specialized knowledge and high-level expertise on a part-time or project basis, avoiding the overhead costs of hiring full-time executives. This is particularly valuable for startups and small businesses with limited resources.</p>
</details>
