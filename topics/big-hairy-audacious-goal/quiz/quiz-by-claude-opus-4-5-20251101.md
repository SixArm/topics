# Big Hairy Audacious Goal (BHAG)

Question: What is the typical time horizon for a Big Hairy Audacious Goal (BHAG)?

- [ ] 1 to 3 years
- [ ] 10 to 30 years
- [ ] 3 to 5 years
- [ ] 6 to 12 months

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>10 to 30 years</p>
  <p>A BHAG is designed to be a long-term goal that provides direction for a company over an extended period. The 10 to 30 year timeframe distinguishes it from typical strategic plans or quarterly objectives, allowing organizations to pursue truly transformational outcomes that require sustained effort and inspire stakeholders around a compelling vision for the future.</p>
</details>
