# Ways of working

Question: What are "ways of working" in a business context?

- [ ] A set of software tools used exclusively for remote team collaboration
- [ ] The physical office layout and workspace design of a company
- [ ] A set of principles, practices, and behaviors that guide how an organization achieves its goals
- [ ] The legal contracts and employment agreements between a company and its staff

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>A set of principles, practices, and behaviors that guide how an organization achieves its goals</p>
  <p>Ways of working encompass the methodology a business adopts to achieve its objectives, including governance, processes, communication, culture, technology, and continuous improvement. This structured approach helps organizations improve efficiency, effectiveness, and outcomes while remaining adaptable to changing business environments.</p>
</details>
