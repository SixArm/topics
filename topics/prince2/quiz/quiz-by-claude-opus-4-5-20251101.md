# PRINCE2 (Projects in Controlled Environments)

Question: What planning approach does PRINCE2 employ that distinguishes it from other project management methodologies?

- [ ] Resource-based planning focused on team allocation
- [ ] Product-based planning focused on defining and delivering project deliverables
- [ ] Time-based planning focused on schedule optimization
- [ ] Cost-based planning focused on budget management

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>Product-based planning focused on defining and delivering project deliverables</p>
  <p>PRINCE2 employs a product-based planning approach, which focuses on defining and delivering project deliverables or products. This distinguishes PRINCE2 from methodologies that center primarily on activities or tasks. By focusing on products (outputs and deliverables), teams gain clarity on what needs to be produced, quality criteria for acceptance, and dependencies between deliverablesâ€”making project scope more tangible and measurable.</p>
</details>
