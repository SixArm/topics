# Human resources (HR) department

Question: What is the primary responsibility of a company's Human Resources (HR) department?

- [ ] Managing the company's financial reporting and tax compliance
- [ ] Developing and maintaining the company's software systems
- [ ] Managing the workforce and ensuring company policies align with employee interests
- [ ] Overseeing product development and go-to-market strategies

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>Managing the workforce and ensuring company policies align with employee interests</p>
  <p>The HR department is responsible for managing a company's workforce across multiple functions including recruitment, onboarding, performance management, compensation and benefits, employee relations, compliance with labor laws, training and development, fostering workplace culture, and maintaining personnel records. All of these activities center on effectively managing people and aligning organizational policies with employee needs.</p>
</details>
