# Personas

Question: What is the primary purpose of creating user personas in product design and development?

- [ ] To create fictional characters for marketing campaigns and advertisements
- [ ] To provide a clear understanding of users and their needs to inform design decisions
- [ ] To document the technical specifications required for software architecture
- [ ] To establish legal compliance requirements for user data collection

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>To provide a clear understanding of users and their needs to inform design decisions</p>
  <p>User personas are created to represent typical users based on research and analysis of real users. They help designers and developers understand user goals, needs, motivations, and behaviors, which then informs decisions about product features, functionality, and user experience to create a product that meets the needs of its intended audience.</p>
</details>
