# Board of Directors

Question: What is the primary relationship between a board of directors and the CEO of a company?

- [ ] The CEO appoints board members and sets their compensation
- [ ] The board reports to the CEO on financial matters
- [ ] The board hires, evaluates, and oversees the CEO
- [ ] The CEO and board share equal authority over daily operations

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>The board hires, evaluates, and oversees the CEO</p>
  <p>One of the key responsibilities of the board of directors is to select and oversee the CEO, who is responsible for running the day-to-day operations of the company. This governance structure ensures accountability at the executive level and protects shareholder interests by maintaining proper oversight of company leadership.</p>
</details>
