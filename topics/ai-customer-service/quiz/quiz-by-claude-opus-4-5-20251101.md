# AI customer service

Question: What is the primary AI technology that enables chatbots and virtual assistants to understand and respond to customer inquiries in natural language?

- [ ] Predictive Analytics
- [ ] Natural Language Processing (NLP)
- [ ] Sentiment Analysis
- [ ] Machine Vision

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>Natural Language Processing (NLP)</p>
  <p>Natural Language Processing (NLP) is the core AI technology that enables chatbots and virtual assistants to understand, interpret, and respond to human language. NLP allows these systems to handle customer inquiries, answer phone calls, reply to emails, and participate on social media by processing and generating text in a way that mimics human communication.</p>
</details>
