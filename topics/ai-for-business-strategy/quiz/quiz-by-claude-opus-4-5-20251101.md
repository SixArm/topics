# AI for business strategy

Question: What are the key foundational elements that organizations must address when developing an AI strategy for business?

- [ ] AI requires only technical implementation without business alignment
- [ ] Data quality, talent development, and ethical controls are essential components
- [ ] AI strategies should focus exclusively on cost reduction
- [ ] External partnerships should be avoided to protect proprietary methods

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>Data quality, talent development, and ethical controls are essential components</p>
  <p>A comprehensive AI business strategy requires multiple foundational elements working together: harnessing quality data for training models, developing or acquiring talent in AI-related fields, establishing ethical guidelines and regulatory controls, creating a roadmap with prioritized use cases, and ensuring integration with existing business processes. This holistic approach aligns AI initiatives with business goals while managing risks and enabling continuous improvement.</p>
</details>
