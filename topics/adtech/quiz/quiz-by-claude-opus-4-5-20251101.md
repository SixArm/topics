# Adtech (Advertising technology)

Question: What is the primary function of a Demand-Side Platform (DSP) in the adtech ecosystem?

- [ ] To serve and manage ads, delivering them to users' devices
- [ ] To allow advertisers to buy ad inventory across multiple exchanges in real-time
- [ ] To help publishers sell their ad inventory at maximum revenue
- [ ] To collect and analyze data related to advertising campaigns

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>To allow advertisers to buy ad inventory across multiple exchanges in real-time</p>
  <p>A Demand-Side Platform (DSP) enables advertisers to purchase ad inventory across multiple ad exchanges and supply-side platforms in real-time. DSPs use sophisticated algorithms to evaluate available inventory and target specific audiences based on demographics, location, behavior, and device type, making programmatic ad buying efficient and scalable.</p>
</details>
