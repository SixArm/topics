# General and Administrative (G&A) department

Question: What is the primary responsibility of a company's General and Administrative (G&A) department?

- [ ] Developing new products and conducting market research for competitive advantage
- [ ] Managing direct sales activities and customer acquisition strategies
- [ ] Overseeing manufacturing processes and supply chain optimization
- [ ] Handling administrative functions to ensure smooth company operations

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>Handling administrative functions to ensure smooth company operations</p>
  <p>The G&A department is responsible for the administrative functions of a company, including financial management, legal compliance, human resources, facilities management, information technology, and administrative services. These functions support the organization's core business activities rather than directly generating revenue.</p>
</details>
