# Minimum Viable Product (MVP)

Question: What is the primary purpose of creating a Minimum Viable Product (MVP)?

- [ ] To create a fully-featured product that can compete with established market leaders
- [ ] To test the market and gather customer feedback with minimal investment before committing significant resources
- [ ] To delay product launch until all possible features have been developed
- [ ] To reduce the quality of the product in order to save development costs

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>To test the market and gather customer feedback with minimal investment before committing significant resources</p>
  <p>The MVP approach allows companies to release a basic version of a product with just enough features to satisfy early customers and gather feedback.
