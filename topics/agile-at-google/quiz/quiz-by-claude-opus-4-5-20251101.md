# Agile at Google

Question: What practice do Google's Site Reliability Engineering (SRE) teams use during retrospectives that aligns with agile's emphasis on team collaboration and continuous improvement?

- [ ] Individual performance reviews with management oversight
- [ ] Mandatory blame assignment for system failures
- [ ] "Blameless" retrospectives focusing on learning and system improvements
- [ ] Quarterly audits conducted by external consultants

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>"Blameless" retrospectives focusing on learning and system improvements</p>
  <p>Google's SRE teams practice "blameless" retrospectives, which focus on learning and system improvements rather than individual accountability. This approach aligns with agile's core emphasis on team collaboration and continuous improvement, enabling teams to openly discuss failures and bottlenecks without fear of punishment, leading to more effective problem-solving and system reliability enhancements.</p>
</details>
