# Kleiner Perkins

Question: What is Kleiner Perkins primarily known for in the technology industry?

- [ ] A software development company specializing in enterprise solutions
- [ ] A venture capital firm that has invested in companies like Amazon, Google, and Slack
- [ ] A technology consulting firm based in New York City
- [ ] A hardware manufacturing company founded in the 1990s

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>A venture capital firm that has invested in companies like Amazon, Google, and Slack</p>
  <p>Kleiner Perkins is one of the most well-known and successful venture capital firms in Silicon Valley, founded in 1972 in Menlo Park, California. The firm has invested in some of the most successful tech companies of all time, including Amazon, Google, Genentech, Netscape, Twitter, Snapchat, and Slack, with over 850 investments since its inception.</p>
</details>
