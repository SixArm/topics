# Product-market fit

Question: What is the primary indicator that a startup has achieved product-market fit?

- [ ] Securing Series A funding from venture capitalists
- [ ] Launching the product in multiple geographic markets
- [ ] High customer retention and loyal users who continue using the product over time
- [ ] Having a large engineering team to build new features

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>High customer retention and loyal users who continue using the product over time</p>
  <p>Customer retention is identified as one of the key indicators of product-market fit. When a product satisfies customers and meets their genuine needs, users become loyal and continue using the product over time.
