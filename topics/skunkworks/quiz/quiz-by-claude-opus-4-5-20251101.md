# Skunkworks

Question: What is the defining characteristic of a skunkworks team within an organization?

- [ ] A team focused on maintaining existing products with minimal budget
- [ ] A group given high autonomy and resources to work on breakthrough innovations
- [ ] An external consulting firm hired to audit organizational processes
- [ ] A committee that enforces compliance with traditional development procedures

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>A group given high autonomy and resources to work on breakthrough innovations</p>
  <p>Skunkworks describes a team within an organization that operates with a high degree of autonomy, often outside normal organizational structures and processes. These small, highly skilled teams are given freedom to explore new ideas, take risks, and experiment with approaches that might not be feasible in a more structured environmentâ€”with the goal of developing breakthrough technology or solving complex problems.</p>
</details>
