# Chief Security Officer (CSO)

Question: What is the primary responsibility of a Chief Security Officer (CSO)?

- [ ] Managing the organization's marketing campaigns and brand reputation
- [ ] Overseeing financial audits and budget allocations across departments
- [ ] Developing and implementing strategies to protect the organization's physical and digital assets
- [ ] Leading product development and engineering teams

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>Developing and implementing strategies to protect the organization's physical and digital assets</p>
  <p>A CSO is a high-level executive responsible for protecting the organization's physical and digital assets, including personnel, facilities, and data. This includes creating security policies and procedures, identifying potential risks and vulnerabilities, developing mitigation strategies, and establishing incident response procedures.</p>
</details>
