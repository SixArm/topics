# Edtech (educational technology)

Question: What is a primary goal of edtech that distinguishes it from traditional educational approaches?

- [ ] Replacing teachers with artificial intelligence systems
- [ ] Standardizing curriculum across all educational institutions
- [ ] Increasing access to education while making it more affordable and flexible
- [ ] Eliminating the need for physical classroom spaces

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>Increasing access to education while making it more affordable and flexible</p>
  <p>One of the main goals of edtech is to increase access to education and make it more affordable and flexible. By leveraging technology, students can learn anytime, anywhere, and at their own pace. This distinguishes edtech from traditional approaches by removing barriers related to location, cost, and scheduling constraints.</p>
</details>
