# Account Executive

Question: What is the primary responsibility of an Account Executive (AE) in a technology company?

- [ ] Managing the company's financial accounting and budgets
- [ ] Writing and deploying production code
- [ ] Driving growth by developing client relationships
- [ ] Overseeing human resources and hiring processes

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>Driving growth by developing client relationships</p>
  <p>An Account Executive (AE) is a sales and business development role whose primary responsibility is to drive growth by developing client relationships. This includes generating new business opportunities, understanding client needs through consultative selling, managing the sales pipeline, and negotiating deals to close new business.</p>
</details>
