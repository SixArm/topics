# AI + regtech

Question: What is the primary benefit of using AI for Anti-Money Laundering (AML) efforts in regulatory technology?

- [ ] Generating marketing materials for financial products
- [ ] Analyzing transactional data and customer behavior patterns to detect suspicious activities
- [ ] Replacing human compliance officers entirely
- [ ] Automating customer service chatbots

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>Analyzing transactional data and customer behavior patterns to detect suspicious activities</p>
  <p>AI assists in AML efforts by analyzing transactional data, customer behavior patterns, suspicious activities, fraud cases, and other relevant information. This enables financial institutions to more effectively identify potential money laundering activities and comply with regulatory requirements.</p>
</details>
