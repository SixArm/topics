# Phishing

Question: What is the primary technique used in phishing attacks to steal sensitive information from victims?

- [ ] Exploiting software vulnerabilities to gain unauthorized system access
- [ ] Using brute force algorithms to crack user passwords
- [ ] Impersonating a trustworthy entity to deceive users into revealing their information
- [ ] Installing keyloggers through drive-by downloads

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>Impersonating a trustworthy entity to deceive users into revealing their information</p>
  <p>Phishing is a social engineering attack where attackers pose as legitimate sources—such as banks, retailers, or colleagues—to trick users into providing sensitive information like login credentials or credit card numbers. Unlike technical exploits, phishing relies on human deception rather than software vulnerabilities.</p>
</details>
