# Swoop and poop

Question: What does the term "swoop and poop" describe in business and project management?

- [ ] A rapid prototyping method where teams quickly build and discard ideas
- [ ] When someone enters a project late and makes critical comments that undermine completed work
- [ ] A collaborative brainstorming technique involving quick feedback cycles
- [ ] A project handoff process between development and operations teams

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>When someone enters a project late and makes critical comments that undermine completed work</p>
  <p>"Swoop and poop" describes a situation where an individual or team comes into a project at the last minute and makes critical comments or decisions that undermine work that has already been done. The "swoop" refers to the sudden entrance, while the "poop" refers to the negative impact. To avoid this scenario, team members should communicate regularly and involve all stakeholders from the outset.</p>
</details>
