# Spacelabs Eclipse Pro

The Spacelabs Eclipse Pro is a compact, lightweight extended Holter recorder built for ambulatory cardiac monitoring. It supports two primary recording modes: up to 14 days of continuous 3-channel ECG recording for extended arrhythmia screening, or 72 hours of full 12-lead ECG capture with pacer detection for more detailed diagnostic evaluations.

The device prioritizes patient compliance through its small form factor and flexible attachment options. Clinicians can choose between a reusable adhesive patch configuration or traditional snap-in lead wire sets depending on the clinical scenario and patient preference. A full-color display on the device allows patients to monitor recording status without requiring additional equipment.

Bluetooth connectivity to a companion smartphone application enables patients to document symptoms in real time, correlating subjective events with the ECG data stream. Patients can also log events using a physical button on the recorder itself. This dual-input approach improves the clinical value of recorded data by linking rhythm abnormalities to reported symptoms.

The Eclipse Pro integrates with several Spacelabs cardiology information management platforms, including Pathfinder SL, LifeScreen Pro, and Sentinel. This compatibility streamlines the workflow from recording through analysis and reporting. The device supports simultaneous recharging and data downloading, which reduces turnaround time between patients and keeps devices in active rotation.

The recorder is designed specifically for arrhythmia screening and diagnostics in outpatient settings. One operational consideration is that the device is not waterproof, which must be communicated to patients during hookup. Overall, the Eclipse Pro balances extended recording duration, multi-lead flexibility, and system integration in a single portable unit aimed at improving both diagnostic yield and patient experience.
