# Spacelabs Eclipse Pro

The Spacelabs Eclipse Pro is a versatile, lightweight extended Holter recorder designed for up to 14 days of 3-channel ECG recording or 72 hours of 12-lead ECG with pacer detection. It improves patient compliance via comfortable, re-usable patch or lead wire options, and features Bluetooth connectivity to a smartphone app for symptom documentation. 

Key Features of the Eclipse Pro:
- Versatile Recording: Supports 3-channel recording for up to 14 days or 12-lead ECG for 72 hours.
- Patient Compliance: Compact and lightweight design, with options for patch application or traditional leads.
- Easy Connectivity: Uses snap-in lead sets, and patient symptoms can be recorded via a button or directly integrated through a smartphone app.
- Compatibility: Works seamlessly with Pathfinder SL, LifeScreen Pro, and Sentinel cardiology information management systems.
- Fast Turnaround: Allows for simultaneous recharging and data downloading, reducing patient waiting times.
- Patient Interface: Includes a full-color display for monitoring device status. 

The device is designed for arrhythmia screening and diagnostics, allowing for improved patient comfort and recording quality. It is important to note that, according to specific patient instructions, the device itself is not waterproof.
