# Big Four accounting firms

Question: Which of the following is NOT one of the Big Four accounting firms?

- [ ] Deloitte
- [ ] Arthur Andersen
- [ ] KPMG
- [ ] PwC

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>Arthur Andersen</p>
  <p>Arthur Andersen was part of the former Big Eight accounting firms but collapsed in 2002 following the Enron scandal. The current Big Four are Deloitte, Ernst & Young (EY), KPMG, and PwC. These four firms are the largest professional services networks in the world as measured by revenue, and they provide audit, assurance, taxation, management consulting, and other professional services.</p>
</details>
