# Vision statement

Question: What is the primary purpose of a vision statement for a technology company?

- [ ] To document the company's current financial performance and quarterly revenue targets
- [ ] To describe the desired future state and long-term goals while inspiring stakeholders
- [ ] To outline the technical specifications and architecture of the company's products
- [ ] To define the day-to-day operational procedures and employee responsibilities

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>To describe the desired future state and long-term goals while inspiring stakeholders</p>
  <p>A vision statement describes what a company wants to achieve and how it sees itself in the future. It is intended to inspire and motivate employees, customers, and other stakeholders while providing direction and focus for the company's strategy and operations. Unlike mission statements that focus on present activities, vision statements are future-oriented and aspirational.</p>
</details>
