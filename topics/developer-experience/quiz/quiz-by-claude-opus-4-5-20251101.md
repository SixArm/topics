# Developer Experience (DX)

Question: What is the primary focus of Developer Experience (DX)?

- [ ] Maximizing the number of features shipped per sprint
- [ ] The overall experience and satisfaction of developers when working with tools, frameworks, libraries, and APIs
- [ ] Reducing the cost of software development infrastructure
- [ ] Ensuring compliance with industry security standards

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>The overall experience and satisfaction of developers when working with tools, frameworks, libraries, and APIs</p>
  <p>Developer Experience (DX) refers to the overall experience and satisfaction of software developers when working with tools, frameworks, libraries, and APIs to create applications or software solutions. It encompasses usability, efficiency, effectiveness, and enjoyment that developers derive from their development process and the tools they use. Key aspects include documentation, ease of use, robustness, debugging and testing capabilities, integration and compatibility, and community support.</p>
</details>
