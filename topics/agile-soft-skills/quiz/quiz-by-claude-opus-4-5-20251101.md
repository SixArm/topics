# Agile soft skills

Question: Which soft skill in Agile involves the capacity to understand and manage one's own emotions while also empathizing with the emotions of others?

- [ ] Time management
- [ ] Emotional intelligence
- [ ] Adaptability
- [ ] Creativity

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>Emotional intelligence</p>
  <p>Emotional intelligence is the capacity to understand and manage one's own emotions, as well as empathize with the emotions of others. This skill is essential in Agile environments because it helps team members navigate interpersonal dynamics, handle conflicts constructively, and maintain productive working relationships during the collaborative and iterative nature of Agile work.</p>
</details>
