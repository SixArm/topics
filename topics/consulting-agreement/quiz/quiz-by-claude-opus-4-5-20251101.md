# Consulting agreement

Question: What is the primary purpose of a consulting agreement?

- [ ] To establish a permanent employment relationship between consultant and client
- [ ] To transfer ownership of the client's existing intellectual property to the consultant
- [ ] To create a legal contract that defines the scope, compensation, and terms when a client needs specialized expertise without hiring a full-time employee
- [ ] To guarantee unlimited liability protection for both parties involved

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>To create a legal contract that defines the scope, compensation, and terms when a client needs specialized expertise without hiring a full-time employee</p>
  <p>A consulting agreement is a legal contract between a consultant (or consulting firm) and a client, used when specialized expertise is needed without full-time employment. It defines key components including scope of services, schedule, compensation, confidentiality, intellectual property ownership, termination conditions, and governing law.</p>
</details>
