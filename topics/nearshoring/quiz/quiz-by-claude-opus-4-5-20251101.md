# Nearshoring

Question: What is the primary advantage of nearshoring compared to offshoring to distant locations?

- [ ] Significantly lower labor costs than any other outsourcing option
- [ ] Geographic proximity enabling easier communication, collaboration, and similar time zones
- [ ] Access to more specialized technical skills unavailable domestically
- [ ] Complete elimination of all cultural and language barriers

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>Geographic proximity enabling easier communication, collaboration, and similar time zones</p>
  <p>Nearshoring's main benefit is the geographic closeness of the outsourcing partner, which facilitates communication and collaboration.
