# Grit by Angela Duckworth

Question: According to Angela Duckworth's research, what is grit defined as?

- [ ] Raw talent combined with high intelligence
- [ ] The ability to quickly learn new skills and adapt
- [ ] A combination of passion and perseverance towards long-term goals
- [ ] Natural aptitude plus favorable circumstances

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>A combination of passion and perseverance towards long-term goals</p>
  <p>Duckworth's central thesis is that grit—the combination of sustained passion and perseverance toward long-term goals—is a crucial factor in achieving success. Her research across diverse groups including West Point cadets, spelling bee contestants, and salespeople demonstrates that the ability to persist through obstacles and setbacks is what distinguishes successful people, more so than talent or intelligence alone.</p>
</details>
