# Global business

Question: What is a primary driver that has facilitated companies conducting operations across national borders and in multiple countries?

- [ ] Increased trade barriers and protectionist policies
- [ ] Advances in transportation, communication, and information technologies
- [ ] Reduction in available global resources
- [ ] Higher fixed costs due to market fragmentation

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>Advances in transportation, communication, and information technologies</p>
  <p>Technology is one of the key drivers of global business. Advances in transportation, communication, and information technologies have greatly facilitated global business operations by making it easier and faster for companies to coordinate activities, move goods, and communicate across national borders.</p>
</details>
