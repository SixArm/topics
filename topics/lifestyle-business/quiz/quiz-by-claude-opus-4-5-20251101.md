# Lifestyle business

Question: What is the primary distinguishing characteristic of a lifestyle business compared to a traditional business?

- [ ] It requires significant venture capital investment to start
- [ ] It is designed to provide a comfortable way of life for the owner rather than maximize profits and growth
- [ ] It focuses on rapid scaling and market domination
- [ ] It must be incorporated as a C-corporation for tax purposes

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>It is designed to provide a comfortable way of life for the owner rather than maximize profits and growth</p>
  <p>A lifestyle business is built around the owner's interests and lifestyle preferences, prioritizing work-life balance and personal fulfillment over aggressive profit maximization and expansion. This contrasts with traditional businesses that focus primarily on growth and returns for investors.</p>
</details>
