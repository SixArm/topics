# Facility Use/Service Agreement

Question: What is the primary purpose of a Facility Use/Service Agreement (FUSA)?

- [ ] To transfer ownership of a facility from one party to another
- [ ] To specify the terms and conditions of using a facility or receiving services between a provider and customer
- [ ] To establish a merger between two service providers
- [ ] To provide insurance coverage for facility maintenance

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>To specify the terms and conditions of using a facility or receiving services between a provider and customer</p>
  <p>A FUSA is a legal agreement that defines the relationship between a facility owner or service provider and a customer or tenant. It establishes clear expectations by specifying provisions such as scope, compensation, duration, responsibilities, liability, intellectual property, and dispute resolution methods.</p>
</details>
