# Storyboard

Question: What is the primary purpose of a storyboard in visual production?

- [ ] To calculate the final budget and allocate financial resources
- [ ] To replace the need for a written script entirely
- [ ] To visually outline and plan the sequence of events in a narrative
- [ ] To serve as the final deliverable shown to audiences

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>To visually outline and plan the sequence of events in a narrative</p>
  <p>A storyboard is a visual tool consisting of illustrated panels arranged in sequence to represent key scenes, actions, and shots. It helps translate a story into a visual format, enables planning and organization of composition, camera angles, and visual details, and facilitates communication and collaboration among team members during pre-production.</p>
</details>
