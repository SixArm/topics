# Inferential statistics

Question: What is the primary purpose of inferential statistics?

- [ ] To organize raw data into tables and charts for presentation
- [ ] To calculate the mean, median, and mode of a dataset
- [ ] To draw conclusions about a population based on a sample of data
- [ ] To collect and store large volumes of data efficiently

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>To draw conclusions about a population based on a sample of data</p>
  <p>Inferential statistics enables researchers to make predictions, test hypotheses, and estimate population parameters when it is not feasible to collect data from an entire population. This is essential in scientific research, medical studies, and market research where sampling is necessary.</p>
</details>
