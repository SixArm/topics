# Developer Experience (DX) - benefits

Question: What is the primary reason that a positive Developer Experience (DX) boosts developer productivity?

- [ ] It allows developers to work longer hours without fatigue
- [ ] It reduces the time and effort required to accomplish tasks by minimizing obstacles and inefficiencies
- [ ] It automates all coding tasks so developers don't need technical skills
- [ ] It eliminates the need for documentation and testing

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>It reduces the time and effort required to accomplish tasks by minimizing obstacles and inefficiencies</p>
  <p>A positive DX boosts developer productivity by providing well-designed tools, efficient workflows, and clear documentation. This enables developers to focus on coding and problem-solving rather than dealing with unnecessary obstacles or inefficiencies, directly improving their ability to deliver quality work.</p>
</details>
