# Organizational values frameworks

Question: What is a critical success factor when implementing organizational values frameworks?

- [ ] Limiting framework development to senior executives only
- [ ] Keeping values frameworks confidential to prevent criticism
- [ ] Involving employees and stakeholders in defining and refining the frameworks
- [ ] Maintaining the same framework indefinitely to ensure consistency

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>Involving employees and stakeholders in defining and refining the frameworks</p>
  <p>Effective implementation of organizational values frameworks requires involving employees and stakeholders in the process of defining and refining them. This participatory approach ensures buy-in, relevance, and alignment with actual organizational culture. Additionally, frameworks should be aligned with mission and vision, communicated clearly and consistently, and regularly evaluated and updated to remain effective.</p>
</details>
