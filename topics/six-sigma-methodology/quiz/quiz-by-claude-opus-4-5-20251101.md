# Six Sigma methodology

Question: What does the DMAIC approach in Six Sigma stand for?

- [ ] Design, Monitor, Assess, Implement, Certify
- [ ] Define, Measure, Analyze, Improve, Control
- [ ] Develop, Manage, Audit, Integrate, Complete
- [ ] Document, Map, Align, Inspect, Close

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>Define, Measure, Analyze, Improve, Control</p>
  <p>DMAIC is the structured approach at the heart of Six Sigma methodology. It provides a systematic framework for identifying, measuring, and eliminating the causes of defects in a process: Define the problem, Measure current performance, Analyze root causes, Improve by implementing solutions, and Control to sustain improvements over time.</p>
</details>
