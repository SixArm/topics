# Agile certifications

Question: Which agile certification covers the broadest range of methodologies including Scrum, eXtreme Programming (XP), Lean, Kanban, and Test Driven Development (TDD)?

- [ ] Certified Scrum Master (CSM)
- [ ] International Consortium for Agile (ICAgile)
- [ ] Project Management Institute - Agile Certified Practitioner (PMI-ACP)
- [ ] Professional Scrum Master I (PSM I)

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>Project Management Institute - Agile Certified Practitioner (PMI-ACP)</p>
  <p>The PMI-ACP is described as a "far-reaching Agile certification" that covers multiple methodologies including Scrum, eXtreme Programming (XP), Lean, Kanban, and Test Driven Development (TDD). In contrast, certifications like CSM and PSM I focus specifically on Scrum, while ICAgile teaches broad principles and concepts but does not go deep into specific methodologies like Kanban and Scrum.</p>
</details>
