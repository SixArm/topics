# AI sales

Question: What is the primary benefit of AI-powered sales process automation for sales representatives?

- [ ] It eliminates the need for human sales representatives entirely
- [ ] It allows them to spend more time on high-value activities like building relationships
- [ ] It guarantees a higher conversion rate on all leads
- [ ] It replaces the need for sales forecasting

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>It allows them to spend more time on high-value activities like building relationships</p>
  <p>AI sales process automation handles routine tasks such as lead scoring, data entry, and task management. By automating these repetitive activities, sales representatives can focus their time and energy on higher-value work that requires human skills, such as building customer relationships, negotiating deals, and providing personalized service.</p>
</details>
