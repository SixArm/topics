# Quick wins

Question: What is the primary purpose of pursuing "quick wins" in a technology project or business initiative?

- [ ] To replace the need for comprehensive long-term strategic planning
- [ ] To build momentum, show progress, and gain stakeholder buy-in
- [ ] To maximize resource expenditure on high-impact initiatives
- [ ] To complete the most complex technical challenges first

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>To build momentum, show progress, and gain stakeholder buy-in</p>
  <p>Quick wins are initiatives that require minimal resources, time, and effort but yield significant, tangible results. Their primary purpose is to build momentum and demonstrate progress, which helps gain buy-in from stakeholders and builds enthusiasm among team members. They complement—rather than replace—comprehensive strategies and long-term planning efforts.</p>
</details>
