# Chief Marketing Officer (CMO)

Question: What is the primary role of a Chief Marketing Officer (CMO) in a company?

- [ ] Managing the company's financial statements and investor relations
- [ ] Overseeing software development and IT infrastructure
- [ ] Developing, implementing, and managing marketing strategies that align with company goals
- [ ] Handling legal compliance and corporate governance

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>Developing, implementing, and managing marketing strategies that align with company goals</p>
  <p>The CMO is a senior executive responsible for driving marketing strategy, managing marketing budgets and teams, conducting market research, overseeing brand management, and measuring the effectiveness of marketing efforts to achieve the company's business objectives.</p>
</details>
