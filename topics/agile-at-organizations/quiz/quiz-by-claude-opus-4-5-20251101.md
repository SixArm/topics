# Agile at organizations

Question: What is the primary benefit of studying agile implementations at companies like Amazon, GitHub, Google, and Netflix?

- [ ] To copy their exact processes without modification
- [ ] To understand how agile principles adapt to different real-world contexts
- [ ] To prove that agile only works at large technology companies
- [ ] To learn proprietary software tools these companies developed

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>To understand how agile principles adapt to different real-world contexts</p>
  <p>Studying agile at various organizations helps technology professionals see how agile principles are applied in practice across different company cultures and scales. Rather than copying processes exactly, the goal is to gain insight into how agile methods can be adapted and tailored to fit specific organizational needs and contexts.</p>
</details>
