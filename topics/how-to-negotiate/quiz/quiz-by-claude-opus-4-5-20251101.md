# How to negotiate

Question: When negotiating, what is the most effective approach to reaching a successful outcome?

- [ ] Focus on your own position and stand firm on all demands
- [ ] Make as few concessions as possible to maximize your gains
- [ ] Seek win-win outcomes by focusing on interests, not positions
- [ ] Avoid setting an agenda to maintain flexibility and surprise

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>Seek win-win outcomes by focusing on interests, not positions</p>
  <p>Effective negotiation focuses on understanding underlying interests rather than fixed positions. This approach enables creative problem-solving and trade-offs that create value for both parties, leading to mutually beneficial agreements and stronger long-term relationships.</p>
</details>
