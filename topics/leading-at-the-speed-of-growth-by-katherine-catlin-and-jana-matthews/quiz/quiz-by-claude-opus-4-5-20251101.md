# Leading at the Speed of Growth by Katherine Catlin and Jana Matthews

Question: According to the book's framework, which leadership capability becomes most critical during the scale-up stage of company growth?

- [ ] Being adaptable and working quickly and decisively
- [ ] Innovating and adapting to changing market conditions
- [ ] Building and managing teams while establishing processes
- [ ] Delegating effectively and creating a scalable business model

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>Delegating effectively and creating a scalable business model</p>
  <p>The scale-up stage focuses on maintaining growth while managing increasing complexity. At this stage, leaders can no longer do everything themselves and must delegate effectively, establish a strong culture, and create business models that can scale. This differs from the start-up stage (adaptability and quick decisions), ramp-up stage (team building and processes), and maturity stage (innovation and adapting to market changes).</p>
</details>
