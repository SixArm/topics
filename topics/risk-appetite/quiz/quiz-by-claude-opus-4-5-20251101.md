# Risk appetite

Question: What is the primary purpose of a risk appetite statement in an organization?

- [ ] To eliminate all potential risks from business operations
- [ ] To outline the organization's approach to risk and guide decision-makers on acceptable levels of risk exposure
- [ ] To transfer all organizational risks to third-party insurers
- [ ] To document past incidents and security breaches

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>To outline the organization's approach to risk and guide decision-makers on acceptable levels of risk exposure</p>
  <p>A risk appetite statement articulates the organization's approach to risk and provides guidance to decision-makers on the acceptable level of risk exposure in various areas of operation. It helps set boundaries and guides decision-making regarding risk-taking, enabling informed decisions about resource allocation and strategic planning.</p>
</details>
