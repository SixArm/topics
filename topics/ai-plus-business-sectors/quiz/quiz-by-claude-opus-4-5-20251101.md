# AI + business sectors

Question: Which capability is AI primarily used for in the cybersecurity sector?

- [ ] Personalized marketing and customer preference analysis
- [ ] Autonomous vehicle navigation and traffic management
- [ ] Real-time threat detection and response by analyzing network traffic patterns
- [ ] Predictive maintenance and production line optimization

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>Real-time threat detection and response by analyzing network traffic patterns</p>
  <p>In cybersecurity, AI is primarily used to detect and respond to threats in real-time by analyzing network traffic, identifying patterns of suspicious behavior, and proactively defending against cyberattacks, fraud, and malware. The other options describe AI applications in retail (personalized marketing), transportation (autonomous vehicles), and manufacturing (predictive maintenance), respectively.</p>
</details>
