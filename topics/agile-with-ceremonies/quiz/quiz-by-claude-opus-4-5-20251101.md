# Agile with ceremonies

Question: What is the primary purpose of Agile ceremonies in Scrum frameworks?

- [ ] To create formal documentation of project progress for executive stakeholders
- [ ] To replace traditional project management status reports
- [ ] To foster collaboration, transparency, and continuous improvement through time-boxed events
- [ ] To assign individual accountability for task completion

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>To foster collaboration, transparency, and continuous improvement through time-boxed events</p>
  <p>Agile ceremonies are structured, time-boxed meetings designed to bring teams together to collaborate openly, maintain transparency about work progress and challenges, and identify opportunities for improvement.
