# Five Forces Analysis

Question: Which of Porter's Five Forces analyzes how easily new competitors can enter a market and disrupt existing businesses?

- [ ] Intensity of Rivalry
- [ ] Bargaining Power of Buyers
- [ ] Threat of New Entrants
- [ ] Threat of Substitutes

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>Threat of New Entrants</p>
  <p>The Threat of New Entrants examines barriers to entry in an industry, such as capital requirements, regulations, and brand loyalty. For technology professionals, this is critical because tech markets often have low initial barriers but can develop strong network effects and switching costs that protect incumbents. Understanding this force helps in evaluating market opportunities and competitive positioning for new products or services.</p>
</details>
