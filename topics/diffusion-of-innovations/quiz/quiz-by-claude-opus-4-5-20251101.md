# Diffusion of innovations

Question: According to the Diffusion of Innovations theory, which group of adopters serves as opinion leaders and signals to others about the potential of a new technology?

- [ ] Late Majority
- [ ] Early Adopters
- [ ] Innovators
- [ ] Early Majority

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>Early Adopters</p>
  <p>Early Adopters are opinion leaders and influencers who tend to have higher social status and are eager to try new ideas. Their adoption serves as a signal to others about the potential of an innovation, making them critical for technology professionals to identify and engage when launching new products or technologies.</p>
</details>
