Question: What distinguishes NEA's investment approach from many other venture capital firms?

- [ ] NEA only invests in companies that have reached profitability
- [ ] NEA focuses exclusively on seed-stage startups in Silicon Valley
- [ ] NEA is committed to a long-term investment approach, building lasting relationships with portfolio companies over many years
- [ ] NEA limits investments to blockchain and cryptocurrency companies

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>NEA is committed to a long-term investment approach, building lasting relationships with portfolio companies over many years</p>
  <p>NEA is known for its long-term investment approach, committing to work with portfolio companies for many years. This enables the firm to build lasting relationships with entrepreneurs and help them achieve their long-term goals, rather than seeking quick exits. Combined with their support services including recruiting talent, go-to-market strategy, and access to a global network of partners, this long-term commitment differentiates NEA's approach to venture capital.</p>
</details>
