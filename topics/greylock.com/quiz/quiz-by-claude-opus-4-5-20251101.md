# Greylock Partners

Question: What is the primary investment focus of Greylock Partners?

- [ ] Late-stage companies preparing for IPO
- [ ] Real estate and infrastructure projects
- [ ] Early-stage companies in enterprise and consumer technology
- [ ] Government and defense contractors

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>Early-stage companies in enterprise and consumer technology</p>
  <p>Greylock Partners, founded in 1965 and headquartered in Menlo Park, California, focuses on investing in early-stage companies in the enterprise and consumer technology sectors. The firm is known for building long-term partnerships with entrepreneurs and has backed notable companies including Facebook, LinkedIn, Airbnb, Dropbox, and Workday.</p>
</details>
