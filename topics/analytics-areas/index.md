# Analytics areas

Analytics is a very large topic. Analytics practitioners sometimes describe these major areas of interest below, and these are described in more depth in the next section of the guide.

- **Business Analytics:** Using data to make informed decisions and improve business outcomes.

- **Data Analytics:** The process of examining data sets to draw conclusions about the information they contain.

- **Descriptive Analytics:** Summarizing past data to understand what has happened.

- **Predictive Analytics:** Using past data to forecast future outcomes.

- **Diagnostic Analytics:** Investigating past data to determine why something happened.

- **Prescriptive Analytics:** Recommending actions to take based on data analysis.

- **Cognitive Analytics:** Using AI to analyze complex data, simulate human thinking, and improve decision-making.
