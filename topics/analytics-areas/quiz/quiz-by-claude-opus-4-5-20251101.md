# Analytics areas

Question: Which type of analytics focuses on recommending specific actions to take based on data analysis results?

- [ ] Predictive Analytics
- [ ] Diagnostic Analytics
- [ ] Descriptive Analytics
- [ ] Prescriptive Analytics

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>Prescriptive Analytics</p>
  <p>Prescriptive Analytics goes beyond describing what happened (descriptive), why it happened (diagnostic), or what will happen (predictive). It recommends specific actions to take based on data analysis, helping organizations make optimal decisions by suggesting the best course of action to achieve desired outcomes.</p>
</details>
