# Agile entrepreneur quotations

Question: Which quotation best captures the agile principle of releasing early and iterating based on feedback?

- [ ] "The value of an idea lies in the using of it." - Thomas Edison
- [ ] "Chase the vision, not the money; the money will end up following you." - Tony Hsieh
- [ ] "If you're not embarrassed by the first version of your product, you've launched too late." - Reid Hoffman
- [ ] "The best way to predict the future is to create it." - Peter Drucker

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>"If you're not embarrassed by the first version of your product, you've launched too late." - Reid Hoffman</p>
  <p>This quotation from LinkedIn co-founder Reid Hoffman directly addresses the agile concept of launching a minimum viable product (MVP) quickly rather than waiting for perfection. It emphasizes that getting a product to market early—even if imperfect—allows teams to gather real user feedback, learn, and iterate. This is a core agile principle: delivering working software frequently and adapting based on what you learn from actual users.</p>
</details>
