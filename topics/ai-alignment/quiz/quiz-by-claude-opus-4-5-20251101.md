# AI alignment

Question: What is the primary goal of AI alignment?

- [ ] To make AI systems run faster and more efficiently
- [ ] To ensure AI behavior and decision-making are consistent with human values, goals, and intentions
- [ ] To reduce the cost of developing AI systems
- [ ] To increase the autonomy of AI systems without human oversight

<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  <p>To ensure AI behavior and decision-making are consistent with human values, goals, and intentions</p>
  <p>AI alignment (also called value alignment or goal alignment) focuses on ensuring that AI systems behave in ways that are consistent with human values, goals, and intentions.
